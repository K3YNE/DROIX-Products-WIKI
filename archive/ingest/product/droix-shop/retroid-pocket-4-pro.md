### **Detailed Features**

### **Performance**

Retroid Pocket 4: Equipped with a [Mediatek Dimensity 900](https://www.mediatek.com/products/tablets/mediatek-dimensity-900) Octa-core CPU and G68 MC4 GPU, it delivers smooth and efficient gaming. The 2xA78 cores at 2.4Ghz and 6x A55 cores at 2.0GHz ensure a balance between power and energy efficiency on this portable gaming handheld.

Retroid Pocket 4 PRO: Steps up with the [Mediatek Dimensity 1100](https://nanoreview.net/en/soc/mediatek-dimensity-1100) Octa Core, featuring 4xA78 cores at 2.6Ghz for even more robust performance. The G77 MC9 GPU complements this, handling more demanding games with ease.

### **Memory and Storage**

Both mobile gaming handhelds come with 128GB of [UFS 3.1](https://semiconductor.samsung.com/estorage/ufs/ufs-3-1/) internal storage, providing swift game load times and ample space for a vast library of retro games. The Retroid Pocket 4 has 4GB [LPDDR4x](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LPDDR) RAM, suitable for most retro gaming needs. In contrast, the PRO version doubles this to 8GB, allowing for more intensive gaming sessions and better multitasking. Both feature a Micro SD card slot to expand the storage.
