At the heart of the **Beelink GTi14 Ultra** lies a choice of two powerful processors:

* **Intel® Core™ Ultra 9 185H** – A 16-core/22-thread powerhouse capable of reaching frequencies up to 5.1GHz for peak performance.
* **Intel® Core™ Ultra 7 155H** – A highly capable 16-core/22-thread processor with excellent efficiency and performance for demanding applications.

Both configurations come equipped with **Intel Arc graphics** running at **2.35GHz**, ensuring smooth performance for both work and entertainment. With compact dimensions of **158mm x 158mm x 55.8mm**, the **GTi14 Ultra** is an ideal choice for users with limited desk space or those seeking a portable yet powerful computing solution.

The **Beelink GTi14 Ultra** supports **dual-channel DDR5 5600MHz RAM**, expandable up to **96GB**, for smooth multitasking. Storage options include **dual M.2 2280 PCIe 4.0 x4 slots**, each supporting up to **8TB**, providing ample and high-speed storage solutions. Running on **Windows 11**, this mini PC is equipped to handle a wide range of applications, making it a versatile choice for any computing need.
