### RockTek GX1: Revolutionize Your Entertainment with 4K Streaming and Smart Features

Step up your home entertainment with the RockTek GX1, a powerful UHD Google TV set-top box (STB) that combines cutting-edge 4K streaming, Dolby Vision HDR, and Google TV 12 for an unparalleled entertainment experience. Designed to elevate your streaming, gaming, and multimedia consumption, the RockTek GX1 is compact yet feature-rich, making it the perfect addition to any modern entertainment setup.

### **Product Overview**

At the core of the RockTek GX1 is the Realtek RTD1325TPV processor, an ARM Quad-Core 64-bit Cortex-A55 CPU, delivering top-tier performance with a 20K DMIPS capacity. Combined with the ARM Mali-G57 GPU, this device ensures smooth visuals and lightning-fast response times. Supporting Google TV 12 (with Google TV 14 planned for future updates), the GX1 brings an intuitive and seamless user interface for all your entertainment needs.

Equipped with 4GB of DDR4 RAM and 32GB of eMMC storage, the GX1 provides ample space for apps, media, and games while ensuring smooth multitasking. Its ultra-compact design and impressive hardware make it an essential upgrade for a clutter-free entertainment center.

### **Performance**

The RockTek GX1 excels in delivering a premium entertainment experience. The Realtek RTD1325TPV chipset ensures fast and efficient performance, while its advanced codec support—including AV1, VP9, H.264, and HEVC (H.265)—offers seamless playback of high-resolution content up to 4K at 60fps. Widevine Level 1 and HDCP 2.3 certifications ensure secure access to premium streaming services.

### **Video and Audio Quality**

For a truly immersive experience, the RockTek GX1 supports Dolby Vision HDR, HDR10, and HDR10+, providing vibrant colors and crisp details. The inclusion of Dolby Atmos and Dolby Audio ensures superior sound quality, enveloping you in a rich and lifelike audio experience that makes movies, games, and shows come alive.

### **Smart Features**

Powered by Google TV, the GX1 provides a personalized entertainment experience by curating recommendations across your favorite streaming platforms. The built-in Chromecast functionality allows you to effortlessly cast content from your phone, tablet, or laptop. The Bluetooth voice-enabled G20 remote makes navigating and controlling your entertainment easier with hands-free Google Assistant integration.

### **Build and Design**

With dimensions of just 70 x 70 x 15 mm, the RockTek GX1 is designed to fit seamlessly into any setup. Its minimalist design ensures it stays unobtrusive while delivering cutting-edge performance. The device also includes two USB ports, HDMI 2.1 with eARC and dynamic HDR support, RJ45 Ethernet, and SPDIF for versatile connectivity options.

### **Conclusion**

The RockTek GX1 redefines what a UHD Google TV set-top box can do, offering a complete package of outstanding 4K visuals, immersive audio, and smart features in a compact design. With future-proof technology, advanced codec support, and seamless integration with Google services, the GX1 is the ultimate entertainment solution for any home.

Don’t wait—order the RockTek GX1 today and transform your home entertainment setup into a state-of-the-art multimedia hub.
