The **Beelink SEi 12 features the 12th gen Intel** processor which has eight [efficient cores](https://www.makeuseof.com/intel-cpus-explained-what-are-e-cores-and-p-cores/) and two performance cores for the best combination of mini PC performance.

The Beelink SEi 12 has the Intel 12th generation [i5-1235U](https://www.intel.co.uk/content/www/uk/en/products/sku/226266/intel-core-i51235u-processor-12m-cache-up-to-4-40-ghz-with-ipu/specifications.html) processor with the **8+2 combined Cores and 12 Threads** for high end performance at **4.4Ghz up to 55W TDP**. It is the perfect mini PC for your daily workload in the home or office.

The Beelink SEi 12 comes with **up to 64GB** [DDR4](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DDR4_SDRAM) RAM and **2TB m.2** [NVMe SSD](https://www.kingston.com/unitedkingdom/en/ssd/what-is-nvme-ssd-technology) . The RAM and storage provide plenty of space for **Windows 11** and your software on the **mini PC.**

The **Beelink SEi 12** mini PC has support for [USB Type-C](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USB-C) on the front, with USB 2.0 on the back for peripherals. It also has **two [HDMI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HDMI) ports** for out to dual monitors at **4k 60Hz,** great for working with multiple software and windows open simultaneously.

The mini PC supports **[WiFi 6](https://www.intel.co.uk/content/www/uk/en/gaming/resources/wifi-6.html) and Bluetooth 5.2** for fast and reliable wireless connectivity. For wired data on the Beelink SEi 12 there is **gigabit ethernet.**

Measuring only **4.9 x 4.4 x 1.6 inches** (12.6 x 11.3 x 4.2 cm), the Beelink SEi 12 mini PC takes minimal space on your desktop or TV stand

The Beelink SEi 12 is ideal for those that desire a mini PC that is best suited to software that requires high performance where the efficient and performance cores can handle your daily workload at the home or office environment.
