Introducing the **GMKTec G1 mini PC**, the perfect office mini PC for professionals and businesses alike. **Powerful Performance with Intel Alder Lake N95 CPU:**   
The **GMKTec G1** mini PC is powered by an **Intel 12th Alder Lake [N95 CPU](https://www.intel.co.uk/content/www/uk/en/products/sku/231800/intel-processor-n95-6m-cache-up-to-3-40-ghz/specifications.html)**, with 4 cores and 4 threads, 7nm fabrication, and a maximum clock speed of 3.4Ghz. With a 6MB cache and TDP of just 15W, the GMKTec G1 office mini PC delivers powerful performance while remaining energy efficient. The Alder Lake N95 CPU is ideal for multi-tasking and running resource-intensive applications, making this mini PC perfect for office use.   
   
**Stunning Graphics with Intel UHD Graphics:**   
The **GMKTec G1 mini PC** comes equipped with [Intel UHD Graphics](https://ark.intel.com/content/www/us/en/ark/products/graphics/226067/intel-uhd-graphics-for-12th-gen-intel-processors.html) with up to 1.2Ghz clock speed and 16 execution units. This GPU delivers stunning graphics, making it perfect for presentations, video conferencing, and more. The Intel UHD Graphics also supports 4K video output, allowing you to connect multiple monitors and increase your productivity.   
   
**Fast and Efficient Storage and Memory:**   
The GMKTec G1 Intel mini PC ships with 8 or 16GB of [DDR4 RAM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DDR4_SDRAM) at 3200MT/s, with a maximum capacity of 32GB. This high-speed RAM ensures that you can run multiple applications and programs simultaneously without experiencing any lag. The mini PC also comes with 512GB or 1TB M.2 [NVMe PCIe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NVM_Express) 3.0 storage, with a **maximum capacity of 2TB.** This fast and efficient storage allows for quick boot-up times and fast application load times, ensuring that you can work efficiently and effectively.   
   
**High-Speed Network Connectivity:**   
The GMKTec G1 mini PC comes with Bluetooth 5.2 and [WiFi 6](https://www.theverge.com/2019/2/21/18232026/wi-fi-6-speed-explained-router-wifi-how-does-work) network connectivity, ensuring that you have fast and reliable internet connectivity. Whether you’re working from home or in the office, you can rely on the GMKTec G1 work mini PC to keep you connected and productive.   
   
**Multiple Ports for Increased Connectivity:**   
The GMKTec G1 mini PC comes with multiple ports, including 2 x HDMI 2.0 for dual monitor display, 1 x 3.5mm audio jack, 1 x [2.5 Gigabit Ethernet](https://www.vsolcn.com/blog/25-gigabit-ethernet-25gbe-overview.html) port, and 4 x USB-A 3.2. These ports allow for increased connectivity, making it easy to connect to multiple devices and peripherals.   
   
**Compact Design:**   
Measuring just 4.48 x 4.17 x 1.6 in / 11.4 x 10.6 x 4.2cm, and weighing just 360g, the **GMKTec G1 office mini PC** is compact and easy to transport. Its compact design allows it to fit into any workspace, whether you’re working from home or in the office.   
   
**Windows 11 Pro Pre-Installed:**   
The GMKTec G1 mini PC with Windows 11 Pro pre-installed, makes it easy to get started right out of the box. Windows 11 Pro offers a range of features and tools designed specifically for professionals and businesses, making it the perfect operating system for the GMKTec G1 mini PC.   
   
**Supports Linux:**   
In addition to Windows 11 Pro, the GMKTec G1 mini PC also supports Linux, allowing you to choose your preferred operating system and customize your Intel mini PC to your liking. Linux is known for its stability and security, making it a popular choice for businesses and professionals who prioritize data protection and privacy.
