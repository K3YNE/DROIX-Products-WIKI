### **Performance**

At the heart of the AYN Odin 2 Pro & MAX lies the Qualcomm Kyro [Snapdragon 8](https://www.qualcomm.com/products/mobile/snapdragon/smartphones/snapdragon-8-series-mobile-platforms/snapdragon-8-gen-2-mobile-platform) Gen 2 CPU, a powerhouse that drives the device’s exceptional performance. With its multi-core configuration, it ensures smooth, responsive gameplay and efficient multitasking. The [Adreno 740](https://www.notebookcheck.net/Qualcomm-Adreno-740-GPU-Benchmarks-and-Specs.669947.0.html) GPU complements this, delivering stunning graphics and a vivid display on the 6″ IPS LCD touchscreen.

### ****Unmatched Gaming Power****

In the 3DMark Wildlife Extreme, a test of graphical prowess, the Odin 2 scores an impressive 3704, dwarfing the AYA NEO Pocket AIR’s 1331 and Retroid Pocket 4 PRO’s 1268.  
This trend continues in the Antutu benchmark, where the Odin 2 achieves a staggering score of 1,569,886, showcasing its raw processing power, compared to the AYA NEO’s 824,367 and the Retroid’s 740,216.

Furthermore, in the Geekbench tests, which measure CPU performance, the Odin 2 Pro & MAX leads with a single-core score of 1485 and a multi-core score of 5047, again outstripping its competitors by a significant margin.

### ****User Experience****

The AYN Odin 2 Pro & MAX runs on Android 13, offering a familiar and versatile OS. This allows users to enjoy a wide range of Android games, apps, and emulators. Connectivity features like [Wi-Fi 7](https://www.qualcomm.com/products/technology/wi-fi/wi-fi-7) and Bluetooth 5.3 ensure fast and stable connections, essential for online gaming and data transfer. The 8000mAh battery with 65W Quick Charge 5.0 means longer play times and shorter charging breaks.

These specs not only reduce game load times but also enhance overall performance, ensuring a seamless gaming experience.
