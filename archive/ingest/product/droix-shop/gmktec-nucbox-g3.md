### **Product Overview**

At the heart of the NucBox G3 is either an Intel N100 or N150 processor with speeds up to 3.40GHz & 3.60GHz, coupled with an efficient 6W TDP that ensures smooth performance while conserving energy. Intel UHD Graphics, capable of reaching 750 MHz, support dual 4K outputs at 60Hz via two HDMI ports, providing clear and vibrant visuals. Compact yet mighty, the NucBox G3 offers significant connectivity options—including four USB Type-A 3.2 ports and advanced wireless features like Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.2—packed into a minimalist footprint that can fit into any work environment without taking up valuable space.

### **Detailed Features**

* **Performance:** Equipped with 8GB DDR4 RAM at 3200 MT/s and 256GB NVMe SSD or 16GB DDR4 RAM at 3200 MT/s and a 512GB NVMe SSD, the NucBox G3 delivers fast data access and storage capabilities. It includes an empty M.2 22*42 SATA SSD slot and a used PCI-e 22*80 NVMe port for optional storage expansion.
* **Connectivity:** Features a single RJ45 Ethernet port supporting up to 2500Mbps, ensuring fast and reliable wired internet connections, complemented by state-of-the-art Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.2 for versatile wireless connectivity. The dual 4K HDMI outputs allow for extensive display configurations and peripheral setups.
* **Graphics and Display:** Utilize Intel UHD Graphics for professional software applications, media creation, and playback, supporting seamless multitasking across dual 4K displays which enhance productivity and visual engagement.
