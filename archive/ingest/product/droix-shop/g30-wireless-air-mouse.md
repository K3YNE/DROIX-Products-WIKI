The **G30 wireless air mouse** uses 2.4GHz RF technology up to 10 meters away from your Android, NVidia Shield, PC or Raspberry Pi device. Simply connect the USB dongle to your device and it is ready to use.

The 34 keys offers full control of your device including direction navigation buttons, play and pause, volume, page scrolling and home and back buttons.

The G30 **air mouse for Android** voice command button with [Google Assistant](https://assistant.google.com/) compatible devices allows you to use the built in microphone with support for apps including YouTube, Google Play and Google Voice Search.

The **Air Mouse** is controlled by a Six Axis Gyroscope chip consisting of three [Gyroscopes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gyroscope)and three Gsensors to provide accurate on screen cursor movement. Simply point and move the remote to move the cursor.

Infrared learning is supported for controlling your TV with the remote. Both the power button and the “TV” button can be configured.

The **G30 air mouse** measures 55x165x25 mm and weighs 70g. It is powered by a single AAA battery (not included).
