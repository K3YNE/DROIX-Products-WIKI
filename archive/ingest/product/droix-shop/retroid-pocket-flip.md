The **Retroid Pocket Flip** has 4GB of fast [LPDDR4x](https://www.techcenturion.com/ddr4-vs-lpddr4x) RAM @ 1866Mhz and 128GB of high-speed [eMMC](https://www.windowscentral.com/emmc-vs-ssd) storage, which can be further increased via a micro SD card slot if required. Its classic D-Pad is comfortable and responsive for pixel-perfect movement, and its buttons are double-injected moulded, improving the lifespan of the ABXY logos that will not chip or fade after use. The retro handheld emulator also has a **high-quality 4.7” touchscreen display** with a resolution of **750×1334.** It has an excellent **450 nits** backlight for gaming wherever you are.

The **Retroid Pocket Flip has high-speed** **[WiFi 5](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wi-Fi)** **2.4G / 5G 802.11 b/g/n/ac** for updating the **Android 11** apps and playing multiplayer games. Its **[Bluetooth 5.0](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bluetooth)** allows connectivity with high-speed peripherals, and its **USB Type-C** port provides fast charging of the handheld gaming console’s **5000mAh battery**. Moreover, it has a **mini-HDMI** output to connect and play on your TV or monitor at **720P resolution**.
