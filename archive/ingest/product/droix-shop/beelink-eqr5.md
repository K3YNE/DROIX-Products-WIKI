## **Beelink EQR5: Compact Mini PC with Ryzen 5 5650U Power**

The **Beelink EQR5** is a **compact and efficient mini PC** designed for **productivity, entertainment, and everyday computing**. Powered by the **AMD Ryzen 5 5650U**, this **space-saving powerhouse** delivers smooth performance for office work, web browsing, media playback, and multitasking. Whether you’re setting up a **home office** or need a **powerful yet portable computing solution**, the **EQ R5** is built to deliver **speed and reliability**.

### **Product Overview**

At the core of the **Beelink EQR5** is the **AMD Ryzen 5 5650U** processor, a **6-core, 12-thread** CPU with **boost speeds up to 4.2GHz**. Combined with **AMD Radeon Graphics**, this mini PC provides **smooth visuals** and efficient processing power for a variety of tasks.

Despite its **compact size (124mm × 113mm × 41mm)**, the **EQ R5** supports **expandable memory and storage**, allowing you to upgrade **up to 64GB of DDR4 RAM** and add additional storage via its **M.2 2280 PCIe 3.0 SSD slot** and **2.5-inch SATA drive bay**.

Running on **Windows 11**, this mini PC offers a **modern and responsive user experience**, making it an excellent choice for **professionals, students, and casual users** alike.
