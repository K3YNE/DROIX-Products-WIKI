### **Introduction:**

Discover the practical and versatile GuliKit 7In1 Steam Deck Docking Station, a multi-device accessory perfect for gaming enthusiasts. This dock, inspired by the classic [American Super Nintendo (SNES)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Nintendo_Entertainment_System), not only serves as a functional tool but also pays homage to a pivotal era in gaming history. 

#### **Product Overview:**

The GuliKit Dock, influenced by the iconic [SNES](https://www.nintendo.com/about/history/), stands out with its distinct gray shell and purple accents. It’s a multifunctional hub that offers seamless connectivity for a range of devices including the [Steam Deck](https://www.steamdeck.com/en/), [Nintendo Switch](https://www.nintendo.com/switch/), and [ASUS ROG Ally](https://rog.asus.com/), making it an ideal accessory for diverse gaming setups. The dock also supports the [Lenovo Legion GO](https://www.lenovo.com/us/en/legion/), adding to its versatility. 

#### **Compatibility with Other Handheld Devices:**

In addition to the devices mentioned above, the GuliKit Dock is also compatible with: 

* [AYA NEO](https://droix.net/product-attribute/brands/ayaneo/) handhelds
* [GPD Win 4](https://droix.net/product-attribute/brands/gpd/) (with keyboard slid up)
* [GPD Win 3](https://droix.net/product-attribute/brands/gpd/) (with keyboard slid up)
* [Onexplayer](https://droix.net/product-attribute/brands/onexplayer/)
* [AOKZOE](https://droix.net/product-attribute/brands/aokzoe/)

#### **Comparison with Steam Deck Docks:**

In comparison to Valve’s [Steam Deck Docking Station](https://store.steampowered.com/steamdeck), the GuliKit Dock offers a stylish, SNES-themed alternative. It features a unique port layout including USB 3.0 ports, HDMI output, and a Gigabit Ethernet port, catering to modern connectivity needs with a nod to classic gaming designs. 

#### **Detailed Features:**

* Three USB 3.0 ports
* Gigabit Ethernet

* 4K 60Hz HDMI output
* 100W USB-C power delivery
* Compatible with Steam Deck, ROG Ally, Ayaneo systems, Nintendo Switch, and Lenovo Legion GO
* Hidden storage for four microSD cards
* SNES-inspired sliding door doubling as a USB-C connection dust cover
