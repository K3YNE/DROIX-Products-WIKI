The Beelink EQi12 is equipped with the [Intel® Core™ i7-12650H](https://droix.net/product-attribute/processor-cpu-model/core-i7-12650h/), a high-performance processor designed to manage demanding workloads with ease. With its sleek dimensions, the EQi12 is a perfect choice for users seeking a compact, space-saving solution without compromising on performance.

Supporting 24GB of Micron [LPDDR5](https://droix.net/product-attribute/memory-ram-technology/lpddr5/) RAM at 5200MHz, this mini PC ensures smooth operation for multitasking and intensive software. Running on Windows 11, the EQi12 is ready to take on modern computing tasks efficiently.
