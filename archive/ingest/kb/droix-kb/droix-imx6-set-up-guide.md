Take your DroiX® unit out of its packaging.Connect the supplied HDMI cable to the iMX6 and your TV/monitor/projector, and then connect the iMX6’s power supply.

Wait for the DroiX® unit to complete booting up – you’ll then be directed to a set up screen.

Complete the set up screens according to your TV and home network’s configuration.

You will then get a screen that asks you to select 3D Launcher or Android launcher.

Select 3D launcher then click Always.

You will then get the launcher’s home screen.

Next step :

If you skipped the set up procedures for network configuration, then please click on settings

You will then get a screen that looks like this

You can now enable & configure the wifi and ethernet connections. Click on the name of your WiFi network and enter the password.

Your wifi network should now be connected

Now enable your ethernet connection if you will be using a network cable instead of WiFi (see below). Navigate down to etherent and toggle the etherent connection to on. In most cases this is all you need to do. Click on Ethernet configuration if you intend to use a static IP address or need to enter other details.

Next you will need to setting the correct time zone

*\*\*\*Note: it is important that you properly configure the time zone or some of your addons will not update properly \*\**

In the same setting menu navigate down to date and time, then select your time zone.

Then hit return on your remote to exit this menu

When on this screen navigate to the XBMC icon

You will see XBMC informing you thatit needs to configure a few things during its first run.

The XBMC homescreen will then be displayed, you’re ready to install your preferred add-ons now!
