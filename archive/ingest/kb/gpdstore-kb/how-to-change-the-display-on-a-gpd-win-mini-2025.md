This video provides a step-by-step guide on how to replace the display on a [GPD WIN Mini 2025](https://gpdstore.net/gpd-handheld-gaming-pcs/gpd-win-mini-2025/). You’ll need a few tools, including a **spudger** and a **hot air gun** or **hairdryer** to soften the adhesive holding the display. A hot air gun is better, but a hairdryer can also work, though it will take longer.

Remember that while we do allow self-repairs, any damage you cause is not covered by our warranty. Please only attempt this repair if you are confident in your abilities. We recommend watching the entire video first to decide if you’re comfortable with the process.
