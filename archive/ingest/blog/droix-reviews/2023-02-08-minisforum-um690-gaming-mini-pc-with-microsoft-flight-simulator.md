Here’s some quick info on other games that work on the UM690. On Forza Horizon 5 you can run at Low graphics 720P and get 125 frames per second. You can of course increase the settings for higher quality graphics.

On [Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2](https://www.callofduty.com/modernwarfare2) you can run at 720P on the Minimal graphics settings for around 100 frames per second. Again you can increase the graphics for higher quality visuals.

With the [UM690](https://droix.net/product/minisforum-elitemini-um690/) you can play a variety of games. Newer ones will run just fine and it really does depend on the game. Flight Simulator for example is very demanding on the graphics but we do still get very playable frame rates but at lower graphics. Other games do generally run at higher frames per second and as such you can increase the graphics or run at higher resolutions.

The UM690 is also very good for emulation. You can run all the classic 8 and 16 bit consoles as well as Dreamcast PS1 and PS2 with no issues at all. For PlayStation 3 providing the game is compatible you can generally run them at great speeds with some minor shader caching now and again. You can even play compatible games on the Ryujinx and Yuzu emulators, not all games run at full speed though, but there are a decent number that do.
