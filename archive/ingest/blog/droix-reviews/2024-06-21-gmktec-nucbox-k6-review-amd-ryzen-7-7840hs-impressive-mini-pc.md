## GMKTec NucBox K6’s Technical Specifications

The [GMKTec NucBox K6](https://droix.net/product/gmktec-nucbox-k6/) mini PC with AMD processor features the powerful AMD [Ryzen 7 7840HS](https://droix.net/product-attribute/processor-cpu-model/ryzen-7-7840hs/) processor, boasting 8 cores and 16 threads built on 4nm architecture, with a base clock speed of 3.8GHz and a turbo boost up to 5.1GHz. Complementing the CPU is the Radeon Graphics with 12 cores clocked at 2700 MHz, ensuring exceptional graphics performance. It supports up to 64GB of high-speed [DDR5](https://droix.net/product-attribute/memory-ram-technology/ddr5-so-dimm/) RAM at 5600MHz across two SODIMM slots, providing ample memory capacity for intensive tasks.

## Build and Design

Despite its small footprint, the NucBox K6 is slightly larger than its predecessor, the K4, to accommodate a more advanced cooling system. The device measures 72 x 72 x 44.5mm and weighs 168 grams, making it highly portable. The improved cooling system includes a single fan setup designed to keep the NVMe SSDs and RAM cool, ensuring stable performance under load.

Included with the NucBox K6 is a 120W power supply, an HDMI cable, and a VESA mounting bracket, allowing for flexible installation options. The device runs Windows 11 Pro out of the box, providing a modern and secure operating system for all your needs.

## Benchmark Results and Performance

In benchmark tests, the [NucBox K6](https://droix.net/product/gmktec-nucbox-k6/) with the Ryzen 7 7840HS delivers excellent performance. In Geekbench 6, it scored 2,465 in single-core and 12,774 in multi-core tests, aligning with top-tier mini PCs. The GPU benchmarks in 3DMark Fire Strike showed a score of 7,972, and Time Spy reached 3,347, slightly outperforming similar systems with the same chip.

Gaming performance is also impressive. “Mortal Kombat 1” ran smoothly at 900p with balanced FSR, maintaining a steady 60 FPS. “Shadow of the Tomb Raider” at 1080p low settings averaged 77 FPS, while “Forza Horizon 5” at 1080p medium settings achieved an average of 116 FPS. These results indicate that the NucBox K6 is well-suited for both modern and older games at modest settings.
