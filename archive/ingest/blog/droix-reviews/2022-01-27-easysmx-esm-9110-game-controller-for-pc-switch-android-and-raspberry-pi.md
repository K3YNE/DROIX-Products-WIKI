## Features of the EasySMX Game Controller

Pressing the Home button will switch on the EasySMX ESM-9110 controller and it will connect to the wireless dongle near instantly.

The gamepad can be used on Windows PC, PlayStation 3, Switch / Switch Lite, Android devices and [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.org/). You can switch between controller modes for these devices by holding the Mode button for six seconds and the LED status will change. You can refer to the manual for each mode and which is best for your device.

The LED brightness can be changed via the button, it has five brightness strengths including off. The rumble button controls the strength of vibration on supported games, it also has five strengths including Off.

LED Brightness

The turbo button can be set up for turbo functions mapped to one button at a time. Simply hold the button to wish to use,  and then press the Turbo button. The LED will turn red to indicate that it has been set and you can now use the button in turbo mode. If you wish to cancel the turbo, simply press the button and turbo again.

Turbo Button

The macro buttons on the back of the pad can be easily configured for multiple button presses. Simply hold one of the Macro buttons, then press BACK and the LED will turn purple to indicate it is ready for the button presses. Press and hold any buttons and the LED will turn blue, and then press the Macro button again.

## Final Thoughts

The [EasySMX ESM-9110 gaming controller](https://go.droix.co.uk/EASYSMX-ESM-9110) feels very comfortable in the hand. While testing the controller during a six hour gaming session, I did not feel any hand cramp or fatigue. The analogues also feel very responsive and accurate to the movement used.

The controls and buttons also feel very responsive and I did not feel like there was any lag due to wireless signal which works up to 10 meters clear sight away.

Battery life, on paper, is around 8 hours. I found that with my own testing, from a full charge I got around 15 hours. This was likely due to playing sitting at the PC desk with the wireless dongle being an arms length away. Your usage may vary, but up to 8 hours is the given length.

I can highly recommend the EasySMX ESM-9110 game controller! Over the period of time I used it, I had no issues at all. It has now replaced my old pad which was nowhere near as good as this.
