The 3 in 1 [Rii MX6](https://droix.net/product-category/accessories/) Remote features traditional remote buttons, a keyboard, air mouse functionality and a high-quality microphone to voice-control your device.

On one side of the [Rii MX6](https://droix.net/product-category/accessories/) remote are the traditional control buttons that enable you to navigate menus and common controls. On the reverse side of the remote is a full QWERTY keyboard and additional navigation buttons. The air mouse feature allows you to wave your remote at the television to move the on-screen navigation cursor. It provides a convenient, fast and intuitive experience.

The remote is compatible with the Android devices such as the T8/T8-S/T8-S Plus/T8-SE, iMXQpro Line, Android TV, PC, PlayStation and XBOX.
