## System Benchmarks

### PassMark

We start the system benchmarks with PassMark which runs a series of artificial tests to push the CPU, GPU, RAM and storage to their maximum performance.

PassMark Benchmark Result

The P2 MAX 2022 scores 1,958 which is a respectable score for this specification of ultrabook.

### PCMark

Next in our benchmarks is PCMark. This runs a series of benchmark tests across your typical day to day tasks. This includes web browsing, video conferencing, working with office documents and much more.

PCMark Benchmark Result

The GPD P2 MAX scores 2,646. Again a good score for the specification with fast boot times as well.

### 3DMark

While the [P2 MAX](https://droix.net/product-category/laptops/mini-laptops/) is not a gaming Ultrabook, it is capable of handling less demanding games on lower graphics settings.

## The P2 Max 2022 Is a Refresh of a Popular Classic Ultrabook – P2 Max 2022 FAQ

*Originally posted by [benjamin](https://droix.net/blogs/author/benjamin/) on: 29/12/2021*

Welcome to our P2 Max 2022 FAQ. Where we’ll be cataloguing all the info relating to the recently announced P2 Max 2022 laptop as it comes to light. Check back regularly for new updates!

### What is the P2 Max (2022)?

The P2 Max 2022 was announced on December the 27th by GPD via their [official Twitter feed](https://twitter.com/softwincn/status/1475404536974278669). Stating that the device would “soon be listed on the Aliexpress store”.

GPDs P2 Max line is a collection

The P2 Max 2022 is intended as a refresh of an existing GPD product: the P2 Max.

The aim of the P2 Max is to provide a middle-ground between GPD’s micro-laptop, the [GPD Pocket 2](https://go.droix.co.uk/GPD-PCOKET-2), and more conventional larger laptops.

The P2 Max is noticably larger than the GPD Pocket 2, as it features a much larger display. As well as a more comfortable chiclet-style keyboard (similar to the one on the [GPD WIN MAX 2021](https://go.droix.co.uk/GPD-WIN-MAX-2021)) and a touchpad that is much more user-friendly.

### P2 Max 2022 Preliminary Specifications

The P2 Max 2022 is a refresh of the GPD P2 Max. A small laptop released in 2019 by [GPD](https://go.droix.co.uk/GPD).

It came in two configurations – one being a low-end model with an [Intel Celeron 3965Y](https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/products/sku/122698/intel-celeron-processor-3965y-2m-cache-1-50-ghz/specifications.html) and an 8/256 RAM/SSD configuration, and a higher-end model with an [Intel Core M3-8100Y](https://www.intel.co.uk/content/www/uk/en/products/sku/185282/intel-core-m38100y-processor-4m-cache-up-to-3-40-ghz/specifications.html) and twice the amount of RAM and storage.

The P2 Max 2022 has been stated to feature the following upgrades:

* A new processor – The [Intel Pentium N6000](https://www.intel.co.uk/content/www/uk/en/products/sku/212330/intel-pentium-silver-n6000-processor-4m-cache-up-to-3-30-ghz/specifications.html), released in early 2021.
* Wi-Fi 6 functionality
* 16GB of LPDDR4 RAM @ 2933MHz (as opposed to the 8/16GB of LPDDR3 on the previous model)
* A 1TB M.2 NVMe 22\*80 SSD – Which should be upgradable, much like the [previous model](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IJQuNtw9pBw).

Beyond that, the specifications of the device should be similar to the [original P2 Max](https://go.droix.co.uk/GPD-P2-MAX). Including the 8.9-inch display, and the awkwardly placed camera in the centre of the unit.

The the new processor should provide a slight performance improvement over the old M3-8100Y. While not an increase in terms of raw speed, the higher core count, faster RAM, and enhanced integrated graphics should provide an even smoother experience overall.
