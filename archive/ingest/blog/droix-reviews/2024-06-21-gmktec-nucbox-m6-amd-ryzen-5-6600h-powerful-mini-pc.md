## GMKTec NucBox M6’s Technical Specifications

The [GMKTec NucBox M6](https://droix.net/product/gmktec-nucbox-m6/) mini PC with AMD processor is equipped with the AMD [Ryzen 5 6600H](https://droix.net/product-attribute/processor-cpu-model/ryzen-5-6600h/) processor, featuring 6 cores and 12 threads, with a base clock speed of 3.3GHz and a max boost clock of 4.5GHz. Built on a 6nm process, this CPU offers superior energy efficiency and performance. The integrated [Radeon Graphics](https://droix.net/product-attribute/graphics-gpu-model/radeon-graphics/) with 6 cores running at 1900 MHz ensures smooth visuals and reliable performance for both work and play. The system comes with 32GB of [DDR5](https://droix.net/product-attribute/memory-ram-technology/ddr5-so-dimm/) RAM, clocked at 4800MHz, upgradeable to 64GB. Storage is provided by a 1TB PCIe 3.0 M.2 2280 SSD, offering fast data transfer rates and ample space for your files.

GMKTec NucBox M6 Processor

For connectivity, the [GMKTec NucBox M6](https://droix.net/product/gmktec-nucbox-m6/) includes [Bluetooth 5.2](https://droix.net/product-attribute/bluetooth/5-2/) and RZ616 WiFi 6E, ensuring fast and stable wireless connections. Video output options are extensive, supporting up to three displays via HDMI 2.0, USB Type-C, and DisplayPort, all capable of 4K resolution at 60Hz. The mini AMD desktop also features multiple I/O ports, including USB 3.2 Gen2, USB 2.0, a 3.5 mm headphone jack, and dual 2.5G Ethernet ports, providing versatile connectivity options for peripherals and networks.
