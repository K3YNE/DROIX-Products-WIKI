Mini PC Technical Specifications

The AMD Mini PC features a choice of either the Ryzen 5 4500U CPU with 6 cores and 6 threads running up to 4Ghz. It has the AMD Radeon Graphics with 6 cores running up to 1,500Mhz.

The Ryzen 7 [Mini PC from ASUS](https://go.droix.co.uk/ASUS) has the 4700U CPU, with 8 cores and 8 threads running up to 4.1Ghz. It also features the AMD Radeon Graphics but with 7 cores and running up to 1,600 Mhz.

There are options for memory of either 16GB or 32GB DDR4 3200Mhz RAM, and storage options from 256GB, 512GB up to 1TB NVMe. With space inside the Mini PC to install a 2.5″ [HDD or SSD](https://smallbusiness.chron.com/difference-between-25inch-internal-solid-state-drive-ssd-25inch-internal-hard-drive-71628.html).

## Mini PC Overview

Other features on the Ryzen Mini PC include a HDMI and DisplayPort for connecting to multiple TV or Monitors. There are two USB Type-C ports, one of which can also be used for display output. There is a high speed Gigabit Ethernet port, WiFi 6 and Bluetooth 5 for fast data transfer.
