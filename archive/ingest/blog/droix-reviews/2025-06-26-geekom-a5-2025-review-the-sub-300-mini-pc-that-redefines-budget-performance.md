## GEEKOM A5 2025 Overview

We begin our GEEKOM A5 2025 review with an overview of it. The Geekom A5 2025 measures around 4.6 x 4.4 x 1.93 inches (11.7 x 11.2 x 4.92 cm) and weighs 652g (1.43 lbs). The silver casing is made from ABS plastic and definitely looks the part on your workspace.

GEEKOM A5 2025 Front View

GEEKOM A5 2025 Back View

## GEEKOM A5 2025 Technical Specifications

Next in our GEEKOM A5 2025 review we take a look at the technical specification as well as our own power usage, fan noise and temperature tests.

The GEEKOM A5 2025 comes with the AMD Ryzen 5 7430U process with AMD Radeon RX Vega 7 graphics. We will be putting it through its paces with some benchmarks shortly.

GEEKOM A5 2025 Internal View

As standard it comes with 16GB DDR4-3200 LPDDR4 RAM which can be expanded up to 64GB. The storage is handled with the default 512GB M.2 2280 NVMe PCIe Gen 3.0 x 4 SSD which can be expanded up to 2TB. The SATA drive allows for up to 1TB drives.

For connectivity we have high speed Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.2 as well as the 2.5Gbps ethernet for wired data.

GEEKOM A5 2025 thermals

In our power usage tests we saw around 3.9W whilst idle on the desktop. During average usage we got a power draw of around 20W. And at full load we got around 39W. In our fan noise tests we got a low of 38dB (average office noise) and high of around 46dB. In our temperature tests we saw a very cool maximum of around 55 °C.
