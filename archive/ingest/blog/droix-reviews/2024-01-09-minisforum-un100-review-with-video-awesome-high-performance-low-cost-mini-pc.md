## System Benchmarks

Next in our Minisforum UN100 review we are performing some benchmark tests. We are comparing the results with the [ACEPC PicoBox Mini](https://droix.net/product/acepc-picobox-mini/) and [ACEPC Picobox Pro](https://droix.net/product/acepc-picobox-pro/) which also feature the Intel N100 processor.

### PassMark

PassMark Benchmark Results Comparison

PassMark runs a series of tests for the CPU, GPU, RAM and Storage. While there is not a massive difference in the scores, around 1.5%, the [Minisforum UN100](https://droix.net/product/minisforum-un100/) takes the lead with 1,273.

### PCMARK

The PCMark benchmarks runs a series of tests on your more day to day tasks such as web browsing, working with office documents, light image editing and more.

PCMark Benchmark Results Comparison

Again we see very similar scores with the [Minisforum UN100](https://droix.net/product/minisforum-un100/) taking second place with 3,001, around a 2.8 % difference between the [ACEPC Picobox Mini](https://droix.net/product/acepc-picobox-mini/).

### Cinebench

Next in our benchmarks is Cinebench which tests the single and multi-core performance of the processor.

Cinebench Benchmark Results Comparison

On single core the UN100 comes 3rd place with a 5% difference in performance. However on multi-core performance we see the places switch to 1st with 2,939, a 3% increase in performance.

### 3DMark

3DMark Benchmark Results Comparison

And on 3DMark which tests the CPU and GPU working together we see the [Minisforum UN100](https://droix.net/product/minisforum-un100/) mini PC for business take 1st place with 372 points. That’s around 3% difference in performance over the ACEPC Picobox Mini.

We sum up our Minisforum UN100 review with our final thoughts.
