## Device Overview

The [GPD WIN MAX 2 2024](https://gpdstore.net/gpd-win-max-2-2024/) measures 8.9 x 6.2 x 0.9 inches (22.7 x 16 x 2.3 cm) and weighs 1005g. It boasts a 10.1-inch touchscreen with a native resolution of 1200P, which can be adjusted up to 1600P. This display size is ideal for both productivity and gaming on this handheld gaming PC.

Device Dimensions

### Controls and Connectivity

The lower part of the device features a backlit keyboard that is very comfortable to type on. Above the keyboard are removable covers that reveal standard gaming controls, including hall sensor analogues, a D-Pad, gaming buttons, a Microsoft Precision TouchPad, and a built-in camera for video calls.

WIN Max 2 2024 Controls

On the left side, you’ll find micro and full-sized SD card slots. The right side houses two USB 3.2 Gen 1 ports. The front has a power button with an integrated fingerprint sensor for secure login.

The back of the device includes triggers and shoulder buttons, along with USB 4 and USB 3.2 Gen 2 ports, a full-size HDMI port, an Oculink port for eGPU docking stations like the GPD G1, and a 3.5mm headphone port.

On the bottom, there are two additional macro buttons that can be configured through the provided software. There are also removable covers for M.2 2230 SSD expansion and an optional 4G LTE module.

## Final Thoughts

The [GPD WIN MAX 2 2024](https://gpdstore.net/gpd-win-max-2-2024/) shows significant improvements over the 2023 model, especially with the upgraded 8840U processor. While it might not be worth upgrading from the 2023 model, it is an excellent choice for new buyers, offering a great balance of work and gaming capabilities.

The 10.1-inch screen is ideal for both productivity and gaming, making it a strong contender against the GPD WIN 4 2024 and GPD WIN Mini 2024 models. For those who prioritize portability in their gaming device, the [GPD WIN Mini 2024](https://gpdstore.net/gpd-handheld-gaming-pcs/gpd-win-mini-2024/) might be a better option.

Overall, the GPD WIN MAX 2 2024 is a robust and versatile portable gaming computer that offers substantial improvements and excellent performance for both work and play.
