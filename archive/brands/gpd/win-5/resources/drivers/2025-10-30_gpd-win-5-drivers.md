# GPD WIN 5 Drivers (2025-10-30)

This is the driver package for the GPD WIN 5, containing the latest drivers.

After extracting the files, double-click the `AutoInstallDrivers.bat` batch file to perform automatic installation.

## Notes on ongoing driver maintenance (from GPD page)

The official driver package may not be updated in real time. After a period of device usage, the driver version may become outdated and introduce bugs. Users may need to manually upgrade. Primary methods:

- AMD Graphics Card Driver: use AMD's official driver download at https://www.amd.com/en/support/download/drivers.html.
