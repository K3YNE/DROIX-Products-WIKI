## Inspect the GPD WIN 5

Upon unboxing your GPD WIN 5, we highly recommend conducting a thorough inspection to confirm that every component is in perfect working order.

- **Inspect the device casing:** Carefully examine the outer shell for any cracks, scratches, or other forms of damage that might have occurred during shipping.
- **Check all buttons and joysticks:** Use the gamepad tester at [https://hardwaretester.com/gamepad](https://hardwaretester.com/gamepad) to ensure all buttons and joysticks are responsive and registering input properly.
- **Assess touchscreen functionality:** Test the entire surface of the touchscreen to ensure it responds accurately to touch. Check that you can interact with all corners and edges of the screen without issue.
- **Confirm USB port functionality:** Connect common USB devices like a flash drive, mouse, or external keyboard to verify that all USB ports are functioning as expected.
- **Check the audio output:** Test both the built-in speakers and the headphone jack to ensure you are receiving clear and consistent audio.
- **Check the battery connector:** Check that the battery connector on the back of the GPD WIN 5 is visually OK before connecting the battery.

By performing this comprehensive inspection of your GPD WIN 5, you can be confident that all its components are functioning correctly and address any potential issues immediately. Should you encounter any problems, our team at GPD Store is ready to provide full support. Simply visit our [Contact Us](https://gpdstore.net/contact-us/ "Contact Us") page to get in touch.

## Update GPD WIN 5 Windows & Drivers

### Updating Windows 11

To ensure your GPD WIN 5 operates with peak performance and the latest security features, it’s crucial to keep it updated. Follow these steps to check for and install Windows 11 updates:

1. Navigate to the **Start menu** by clicking the Windows icon or by pressing the Windows Key.
2. Choose the **Settings** icon (shaped like a gear) or type “Settings” into the search bar and press Enter.

## How to Update Drivers on the GPD WIN 5

### Windows Update

For most drivers, using Windows Update is the easiest and most reliable approach:

1. Access **Settings** via the Start menu.
2. Choose **Windows Update** from the side panel.
3. Click on **Check for updates**.
4. Windows will then find, download, and install any necessary driver updates alongside regular system updates.

### Device Manager

If you need to update a specific driver:

1. Right-click on the Start button and choose **Device Manager**.
2. Locate and expand the hardware category for the device you wish to update.
3. Right-click the specific device and select the **Update driver** option.
4. Select **Search automatically for drivers** to let Windows find the best available driver.

### AMD Drivers Updates

The GPD WIN 5 is powered by an AMD processor. To ensure the GPU and chipset drivers are fully optimized, use the AMD Auto-Detect tool.

1. First, download and install the **AMD Auto-Detect and Install Driver Updates** tool from the official AMD website [here](https://www.amd.com/en/support).
2. Launch the application and follow the on-screen instructions to select and install the recommended updates.

You can read our detailed [How to uninstall and install AMD graphics drivers guide here](https://gpdstore.net/kb/software-guides/kb-article/how-to-uninstall-and-install-amd-graphics-drivers/ "How to uninstall and install AMD graphics drivers guide here").

**Note:** To maintain system stability and security, always download drivers directly from official sources. While Windows Update handles many essential drivers, the dedicated AMD tool is crucial for keeping the specific components of your GPD WIN 5 optimized. Consistent driver updates will ensure your handheld PC maintains peak performance and full compatibility with the newest software and games.
