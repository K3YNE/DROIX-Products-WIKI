### Experience Retro Gaming Like Never Before with the AYANEO Pocket ACE​

The AYANEO Pocket ACE is a revolutionary handheld gaming device that seamlessly blends the charm of retro gaming with cutting-edge modern technology. Drawing inspiration from classic horizontal handheld consoles, this device offers a nostalgic aesthetic coupled with powerful performance, making it an excellent choice for both retro gaming enthusiasts and modern gamers.​

---

### Performance

At the core of the Pocket ACE lies the Snapdragon G3x Gen 2 gaming platform, delivering up to 15W of sustained performance. This powerful processor ensures smooth emulation of retro titles and efficient handling of modern Android games. The device offers multiple performance modes—Saving, Balance, Game, and Max—allowing users to optimize performance based on their gaming needs.

### Controls and Inputs

The Pocket ACE is equipped with a versatile control layout designed to accommodate various gaming genres. Key features include:​

* Medium Hall-effect dual analog joysticks for precise input
* Linear Hall-effect triggers for responsive control
* A retro-style D-pad positioned at the top for classic gaming feel
* ABXY face buttons with customizable layouts
* Additional buttons including Home, Turbo, and emulator shortcuts​

### Storage and Connectivity

The AYANEO Pocket ACE offers flexible storage options to cater to different user needs:​

* 8GB RAM + 128GB storage
* 12GB RAM + 256GB storage
* 16GB RAM + 512GB storage
* 16GB RAM + 1TB storage​

A microSD card slot is also available for easy expansion of storage capacity. In terms of connectivity, the device includes high-speed Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.3, and a USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-C port that supports 10Gbps data transfer and DP 1.4 video output.

---

### Design and Display

The AYANEO Pocket ACE features a sleek and compact design, reminiscent of traditional horizontal handheld consoles. Its 4.5-inch IPS display boasts a resolution of 1620×1080 pixels with a 3:2 aspect ratio, delivering vibrant colors and sharp visuals that bring both retro and modern games to life. The device is available in three distinct color options:​

* Shadow Dance Black
* Light Blade White
* Retro Power (limited edition)​

The quad-curved edges and advanced UV coating provide a comfortable grip and a premium feel, ensuring extended gaming sessions remain enjoyable.

### Battery and Additional Features

The Pocket ACE is powered by a 6,000 mAh battery, supporting 40W PD fast charging to keep you gaming longer. Other noteworthy features include:​

* 0916B Surge Linear Motor for immersive haptic feedback
* Six-axis gyroscope for motion controls
* Advanced cooling system with ultra-thin fan, heat dissipation fins, and copper plate
* Fingerprint recognition integrated into the power button​

These features ensure optimal performance and an enhanced gaming experience

### User Experience and Conclusion

The AYANEO Pocket ACE stands out as a top-tier handheld gaming device that captures the essence of classic retro gaming while delivering the power and versatility of modern technology. Its compact design, powerful performance, and customizable controls make it an exceptional choice for gamers seeking a portable and immersive gaming experience.​
