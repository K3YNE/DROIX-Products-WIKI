（New）Please note: If you have not replaced the SSD, please do not upgrade the BIOS firmware
The prerequisite for upgrading this BIOS is that you have replaced the SSD, and the SSD is the WD series SN550 or SN750. It fixes problem that the SSD cannot be found after restarting (not turning it off and then on again). This problem is because the WD series SSDtakes a long time to power up, which causes the BIOS to fail to find the hard disk device after restarting, and then the boot jumps to the EFI or BIOS interface.

（New）GPD WIN Max 2021 (1195G7) BIOS Firmware V1.18
1. Optimize display output
2. Optimized the refresh rate
3. Fixed the black screen problem in some cases after upgrading the latest graphic driver.
Please download the firmware to the desktop as soon as possible, instead of downloading it to the USB flash drive. If the BIOS upgrade is enabled in the USB flash drive, the upgrade may fail. Once the upgrade fails, please do not turn off the computer, and re-upgrade after ending the DOS process, otherwise the machine will not be able to boot.
