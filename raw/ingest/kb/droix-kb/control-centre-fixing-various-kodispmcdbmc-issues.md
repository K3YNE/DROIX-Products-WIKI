If you have encountered issues with Kodi, DBMC or SPMC, this video will guide you through how you can fix them using our DroiX® Control Centre app.

*This app was designed with DroiX® devices in mind. Using it on another Android device is never going to be an officially supported avenue. It shouldn’t do anything bad, but if your dog drives off in your new car and the cat eats your budgerigar, DroiX® won’t be held responsible.*

You can look at the application’s Play Store entry here – <https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=uk.co.droidbox.control_center&hl=en_GB> 

If you’ve not used the Play Store before, please have a read of <https://droix.net/first-run-of-googles-play-store/> . Once you have signed in, you will be able to initiate installations of applications from your main computer’s browser.
