This video will show you step by step on how to replace the [GPD WIN 4](https://gpdstore.net/gpd-handheld-gaming-pcs/gpd-win-4-2025/) display. You will need a few tools including a spudger, hot air gun or a hairdryer to soften the glue holding the display in place. A hot air gun is preferable, but an hairdryer can be used though it may take longer to soften the glue.

As always, while we do allow self repairs, any damage caused by yourself is not covered by our warranty (see [Terms & Conditions here](https://droix.net/terms-and-conditions/)). Please only do so if you feel you are confident that you can do it. Watch the video first, and judge for yourself.
