This video will show you step by step on how to replace the [GPD WIN MAX 2 2025](https://droix.net/product/gpd-win-max-2-2025/) (and 2024) display. You will need a few tools including a spudger, hot air gun or a hairdryer to soften the glue holding the display in place. A hot air gun is preferable, but an hairdryer can be used though it may take longer to soften the glue.
