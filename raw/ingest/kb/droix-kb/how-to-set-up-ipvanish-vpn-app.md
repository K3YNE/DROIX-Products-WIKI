If you have a DroiX® T8, T8-S or T8-S Plus you may already have the IPVanish app installed if you are on the latest firmware (April 2016 or later). If you have not updated you can follow our guides for your DroiX® [here](https://droix.net/?s=OTA).

If you have a different DroiX®, you can download the IPVanish app from the Google Play Store [here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ixolit.ipvanish).
