This video guide shows how to replace the [GPD WIN Mini](https://droix.net/product/gpd-win-mini-2025/) 2025 right joystick module. It is a fairly straightforward process with only having to remove a few parts to reach it. We made a small mistake in the video just before putting the back of the case back on, we forgot to reattach the cable. So dont forget to reattach it! 🙂
