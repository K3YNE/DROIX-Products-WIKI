## UM780 XTX Setup

Setting up your Minisforum PC is as easy as pie, connect:

1. The power adapter up to the mini PC and a wall socket.
2. The UM780 XTX to your display(s) using any HDMI/DisplayPort/USB 4.0 cable.
3. Any peripherals (mouse, keyboard, etc.) to your UM780.

You can also make use of the provided stand and/or VESA mount to improve your overall aesthetic, space management and versatility, but is not necessary for the necessary set-up process.

And… that’s it! You’re all ready to go! Power on the mini PC and it will boot to the [Windows 11 Pro](https://www.microsoft.com/en-gb/windows/business/windows-11-pro-onward-itdm) setup screen (see *Image* below). Follow the onscreen prompts, which consist of a series of steps to help configure and tailor the UM780 XTX to meet your specific needs. This includes selecting your region, preferred language, keyboard layout, accessibility settings and more. You will also be able to connect to the internet and start downloading the latest updates during the process.

Windows Setup Screen

The mini PC may reboot once or twice during the setup process – this is completely normal. Once complete, you will be able to log into Windows.

## **Updating Windows, Firmware & Drivers**

Downloading and updating your device frequently isn’t something many people look forward to… however, it’s extremely important and necessary to do so in order not to only improve performance and compatibility but also to ensure your device is protected and less vulnerable to security breaches. We’ll go through and cover everything you should update on your UM780 XTX below.

### Update Windows

Windows is the preinstalled Operating System on your Minisforum UM780 XTX, so updating it regularly will be core to ensure you have access to the latest Windows and software features, as well as to resolve any vulnerabilities such as prior or existing bugs, potential security breaches and even performance or stability issues. These updates are incredibly important and you should be updating these as soon as they come out – in case you’re not sure how to do this, we already have a step-by-step guide detailing exactly what you need to do below.

### Update AMD Graphics Drivers

Updating your UM780 XTX graphics drivers doesn’t only boost the in-game frame rate but it can also improve general device stability and performances for any software. Old drivers can’t always communicate with the latest software and updates, and outdated drivers can cause poor performance, screen flickering, and even program and system crashes. We recommend typically updating your graphics drivers as soon as a new update is available, or whenever you encounter graphical problems, so be sure to check often.

We’ve got a short and straightforward how-to guide below covering where to find, and how to install these drivers.

Restarting your device will finalise any Firmware or Software updates, which are often required to ensure these updates correctly complete the installation process. ## (Optional) Troubleshooting OcuLink Cable

You may run into an issue when trying to get OcuLink working, where you are prompted that BitLocker is outdated and is requesting to be updated. Following the instructions will update BitLocker and work as intended. However, connecting/disconnecting the OcuLink device will result in BitLocker prompting the same message. A Windows security issue causes this, and there is not much we can do to fix this. However, we can circumvent this by either of the following:

1. Updating each time you connect/disconnect a device.
2. Disabling BitLocker in Windows (at your discretion).

### Disable BitLocker

BitLocker is a full encryption feature incorporated within Windows OS that is designed to protect your data. You can turn off this feature as a hotfix for the issues occurring when connecting a device via OcuLink. If you decide to disable Bitlocker on your Minisforum UM780 XTX, do the following:

1. Select **Start Menu** (Windows logo) from the taskbar.
2. Search & select **Manage BitLocker**.
3. Select **Turn off BitLocker**.
4. Confirm **Turn off BitLocker**.

This process may take a few hours to decrypt your drive and you may also be required to input your password or PIN (if you have one). You can continue to use your device during this time.
