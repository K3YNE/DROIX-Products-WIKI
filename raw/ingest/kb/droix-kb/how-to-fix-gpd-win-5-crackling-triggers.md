This video guide shows how to fix the ‘crackling’ noise when you press the [GPD WIN 5](https://droix.net/product/gpd-win-5/) triggers. It is caused by a little bit of glue residue in the path of the trigger when it is pressed. You can first try clearing the residue with some IPA and a cotton bud without opening by pressing the trigger down and carefully inserting the bud. If the issue persists, you can open the back of the case with a Phillips 000 screwdriver and have easier access to clear it.
