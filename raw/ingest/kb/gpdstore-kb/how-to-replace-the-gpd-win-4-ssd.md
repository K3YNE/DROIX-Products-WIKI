This video provides a step-by-step guide on how to replace the display on a [GPD WIN 4](https://gpdstore.net/gpd-handheld-gaming-pcs/gpd-win-4-2025/). To do this, you’ll need a spudger and a hot air gun or a hairdryer to soften the adhesive holding the screen in place. A hot air gun is the better option, but a hairdryer can also be used, though it will take longer.
