This instructional video demonstrates the procedure for how to replace the the [GPD WIN 4](https://gpdstore.net/product/gpd-win-4-2025/ "GPD WIN 4") triggers and controller boards. The required tools are a small Phillips-head screwdriver and a plastic spudger. The use of a hot air gun or a hairdryer is also recommended to help loosen the adhesive that holds the strip covering the screws on the bottom of the device.

While we do permit self-repairs, any damage you cause is not covered by our warranty (see our [Terms & Conditions here](https://gpdstore.net/terms-of-service/)). Please only attempt this repair if you are confident in your abilities. We recommend watching the full video first to decide if you are comfortable with the process.
