## Frequently Asked Questions

### GPD MicroPC 2

* Windows Update is not working, asking to Retry with error code 0x8007005

  If you are attempting to update windows and are constantly being prompted to Retry, with error 0x8007005. Please see our guide on [how to enable Windows Updates on GPD MicroPC 2 here](https://gpdstore.net/kb/gpd-micropc-2-support-hub/kb-article/how-to-enable-windows-updates-on-gpd-micropc-2/) to re-enable updates.
* How can I change the TDP on the GPD MicroPC 2?

  Follow our step-by-step guide [here](https://gpdstore.net/kb/gpd-micropc-2-support-hub/kb-article/how-to-change-the-tdp-on-the-gpd-micropc-2/) for how to change the GPD MicroPC 2 TDP.
* How can I update the GPD MicroPC 2 BIOS?

  Follow our step-by-step guide [here](https://gpdstore.net/kb/gpd-micropc-2-support-hub/kb-article/how-to-update-the-gpd-micropc-2-bios/) for how to update the BIOS.
* What is the battery life like on the GPD MicroPC 2?

  While stress testing the GPD MicroPC 2 on full load at full brightness at 10W TDP we got a battery life of around 1 hour 42 minutes. Average usage will of course be higher, in the range of 4 to 6 hours. You can also lower the TDP to improve battery life.
* Does the GPD MicroPC 2 have a touch screen?

  The GPD MicroPC 2 has a touch screen. It can also be used with a capacitive stylus (not an active stylus).
* Does the GPD MicroPC 2 support external displays?

  The GPD MicroPC 2 has a dedicated HDMI 2.1 port for output direct to a TV or monitor. You can also use the USB Type-C port to output to up to three monitors with an appropriate hub. We have tried a triple monitor setup via USB-C and one additional monitor via HDMI for a quad monitor setup.
* What is the screen size and resolution of the GPD MicroPC 2?

  The GPD MicroPC 2 has a 7″ LTPS 1080P (1920×1080) display, 60Hz refresh rate
* Can I connect an eGPU to the GPD MicroPC 2?

  The GPD MicroPC 2 has a USB 3.2 Type-C port which supports up to 10Gbps data transfer speed. While it is possible to connect an eGPU such as the GPD G1, you would not get the full benefit of USB 4 (40Gbps) which will reduce the performance. You would however see a vast improvement on GPU related tasks with an eGPU.
* Can I use the GPD MicroPC 2 for gaming as well as work?

  The GPD MicroPC 2 is not a gaming device but it is possible to player lower demanding or older games and emulators. You can connect any USB or Bluetooth joystick.
* Can I upgrade the storage on the GPD MicroPC 2

  You can increase the storage on the GPD MicroPC 2 up to 4TB M.2 2280 SSD. As a note, we do offer upgrades up to 4TB when purchasing from us.
* When will the GPD MicroPC 2 launch

  The GPD MicroPC 2 is expected to launch towards the end of August 2025
* What are the main features of the GPD MicroPC 2?

  The GPD MicroPC 2’s main feature is its versatile 2-in-1 design, incorporating a 7-inch 1080p touchscreen that rotates 360 degrees to convert from a mini-laptop into a tablet. It stands out with exceptional connectivity for its compact size, boasting a rare built-in 2.5Gbps Ethernet port alongside four USB ports (two Type-A, two Type-C) and a full-size HDMI output. Powered by a modern Intel N-series CPU (N250 or N300) and 16GB of RAM, it also includes a backlit keyboard, dedicated mouse buttons, and a fingerprint sensor integrated into the power button for security.
* What are the differences between the GPD MicroPC 2 N250 & N300 models

  The main difference are the CPUs, with the N300 being the higher performer:

  + Intel Processor N250, 4 Cores / 4 Threads, 3.8 GHz, 6W – 15W
  + Intel Processor N300 8 Cores / 8 Threads, 3.8 GHz, 7W
* What is the GPD MicroPC 2 size and weight? Is it portable?

  The GPD MicroPC 2 measures around 6.7 x 4.3 x 0.92 inches (17.1 x 11.0 x 2.3 cm) and weighs around 500g (1.10lbs). It is designed to be highly portable.
* How much faster is the GPD MicroPC 2 than the original MicroPC

  The GPD MicroPC 2 is considerably faster. Depending on the benchmark software ranging from 100% to 800% increase in performance. See our [GPD MicroPC 2 review here](https://gpdstore.net/blog/gpd-micropc-2-review/) for benchmark comparisons.
* Does the GPD Micro PC 2 support a stylus

  It supports a capacitive stylus only, an active stylus is not compatible.
* Is the GPD MicroPC 2 keyboard backlit?

  Yes, it has three levels; Off and two brightness levels
* What is the battery capacity of the GPD MicroPC 2?

  The GPD MicroPC 2 is equipped with a 27.5Wh rechargeable battery.
