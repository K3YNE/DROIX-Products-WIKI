## AYN Thor: The Dual-Screen AMOLED Gaming Revolution

The golden era of dual-screen gaming is reborn, brighter and more powerful than ever before. The AYN Thor is a masterpiece of engineering, fusing the beloved clamshell form factor with two stunning AMOLED displays and the raw power of a flagship Snapdragon processor. This revolutionary combination delivers the definitive handheld for both faithful retro emulation and demanding modern Android gaming. The Thor isn’t just a gaming device; it’s an immersive, tactile experience that single-screen handhelds can only dream of.

## Choose Your Power: Four Thor Models

The AYN Thor is available in four distinct models, allowing you to select the perfect balance of performance and value for your gaming needs.

### Thor Lite

The Thor Lite is the perfect gateway into the world of dual-screen Android gaming. Powered by the proven and highly capable **Qualcomm Snapdragon 865** processor, it delivers outstanding performance for a massive library of Android titles and flawless emulation up to and including demanding retro systems. It’s the ideal choice for gamers who prioritise a versatile and feature-rich experience.

* **Processor:** Qualcomm Snapdragon 865
* **Memory:** 8GB LPDDR4x
* **Storage:** 128GB UFS 3.1
* **Connectivity:** Wi-Fi 6 + BT 5.1

### Thor Base, Pro & Max

For the ultimate enthusiast who demands uncompromising power, the Base, Pro, and Max models step up to the flagship **Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 Gaming Platform**. This cutting-edge chipset provides elite-level performance to conquer the latest Android games at their highest settings and push the boundaries of high-end emulation. With faster RAM, next-generation storage, and the latest connectivity standards, these are the definitive models for power users.

* **Processor:** Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 Gaming Platform
* **Memory:** 8GB, 12GB, or 16GB LPDDR5x
* **Storage:** 128GB, 256GB, or 1TB UFS 3.1
* **Connectivity:** Wi-Fi 7 + BT 5.3

### Breathtaking Dual AMOLED Displays

Shared across all models, the Thor’s visual centerpiece is its dual AMOLED screens. The primary 6-inch display boasts a crisp 1080×1920 resolution and a buttery-smooth 120Hz refresh rate, bringing every game to life with perfect blacks and explosively vibrant colors. The secondary 3.92-inch touch display opens up a new world of possibilities, from displaying maps and inventory to acting as a virtual controller or multitasking hub, creating an unparalleled interactive experience.

### Uncompromised Gaming Performance

Whether you choose the capable Snapdragon 865 or the flagship Snapdragon 8 Gen 2, every Thor is built for sustained performance. To maintain peak speeds during marathon sessions, the Thor integrates a sophisticated active cooling system within its sleek frame, guaranteeing a cool, stable, and throttle-free experience that lets you push your games to the limit.

### A Refined User Experience

The AYN Thor runs a highly refined version of Android 13, complete with AYN’s custom AOSP launcher built to harness the power of two screens. The hardware experience is equally premium, featuring precision Hall effect joysticks and triggers for zero drift and maximum durability. For big-screen gaming, the USB-C port supports DisplayPort output up to 4K, transforming the Thor into a versatile home console.
