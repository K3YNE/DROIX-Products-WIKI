Beelink return with what is effectively their most powerful mini PC yet: The Beelink GTi 11!

This high-performance mini PC features an Intel Core i5-1135G7 processor, leveraging four cores and a frequency of (up to) 4.20GHz to provide the smoothest performance in any tasks you can throw at it. This is also backed up by cutting-edge Intel Iris Xe Graphics for stable, high quality visuals in games, movies and more.

By default, the Beelink GTi 11 comes with 16GB of high-speed dual-channel DDR4 RAM. If you so wish, this can be increased to a MASSIVE 64GB of RAM. Making this an excellent mini PC for photo editing, streaming and more.

This small-form-factor PC is also great for productivity. With support for up to three displays (1x HDMI, 1x DisplayPort, and 1x USB-C), you can expand your workspace as you see fit.

The Beelink GTi 11 is extremely small and portable (sizing up at a tiny 168mm\*120mm\*39mm), but does not lack in interfaces. With six USB-A ports, a USB-C port, a dedicated headphone jack, and even a fingerprint sensor, this Intel NUC will comfortably fit into almost every setup.

To round off this amazingly portable PC, it features Wi-Fi 6 functionality for lightning-fast wireless connectivity. But for those in need of a wired connection, it features TWO 2.5 Gigabit ethernet ports for the ultimate in wired internet speeds.
