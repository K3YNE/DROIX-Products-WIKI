The X9 Pro Game Stick combines classic gaming nostalgia with modern technology, turning your TV into a retro gaming powerhouse. Designed for gamers who want easy, direct access to thousands of retro games, this compact console provides hours of fun for single or multiplayer experiences. With 4K resolution support, the X9 Pro Game Stick is perfect for reliving the classics with a new visual twist.

### **Product Overview**

At the heart of the X9 Pro Game Stick is the Amlogic S905X3 64-bit quad-core ARM Cortex A55 processor. Paired with 2GB of DDR3 RAM, this game stick is built to deliver a reliable performance for retro titles, though it has some limitations with higher-end emulation. Available in 64GB, 128GB, and 256GB versions, the X9 Pro offers an extensive game library straight out of the box. It operates on EmuELEC 4.3, a user-friendly OS designed to optimize retro gaming performance and compatibility with multiple classic emulators.

### **Detailed Features**

1. **Performance** – The X9 Pro’s Amlogic S905X3 processor and 2GB RAM offer smooth gameplay for retro systems. While it handles PlayStation 1, PSP, and other retro consoles well, more intensive systems like N64 and Dreamcast may see occasional performance drops.
2. **Memory and Storage** – the X9 Pro allows users to enjoy between 30,000 and 50,000 preloaded games. This extensive library spans various classic consoles, providing endless gaming options.
3. **Display** – With up to 4K resolution support at 60fps, the X9 Pro Game Stick enables retro titles to look sharp on modern displays. An HDMI output ensures compatibility with most televisions, making it a simple plug-and-play experience.
4. **User Experience** – The game stick includes a 2.4GHz wireless connection for two controllers, allowing seamless multiplayer gameplay without cable clutter. An HDMI cable and power cable are also included, making setup fast and straightforward. The device is designed to be compact and portable, so users can easily bring it to gatherings or enjoy solo play at home.
