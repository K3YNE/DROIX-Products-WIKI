## **Innovative Modular Design**

The AYANEO 3 pioneers a groundbreaking modular controller system, allowing users to freely combine and customize controller modules to suit their gaming preferences. With detachable and interchangeable ABXY buttons, players can configure up to 56 unique layout combinations, ensuring optimal comfort and control for any game genre. ## **Stunning Visuals**

* **OLED Display**: Features a 144Hz refresh rate, 800 nits brightness, and 110% DCI-P3 color gamut coverage, delivering vibrant and crisp visuals.
* **LCD Display:** Features a smooth 120Hz refresh rate, 500 nits brightness, 100% sRGB coverage, and VRR with AMD FreeSync Premium — delivering a clear, vibrant, and tear-free gaming experience.

Both LCD and OLED screens provides an immersive visual experience, allowing gamers to enjoy stunning graphics with exceptional clarity.

## **High-Powered Performance**

Under the hood, the AYANEO 3 offers two powerful processor options:

**AMD Ryzen™ AI 9 HX 370**: Equipped with 12 cores and 24 threads, this processor delivers top-tier performance for demanding AAA titles and multitasking.

**AMD Ryzen™ 7 8840U**: A balanced processor offering reliable performance for both high-demand and casual gaming sessions.

Paired with up to 64GB of LPDDR5X RAM and up to 4TB of PCIe 4.0 SSD storage, the AYANEO 3 ensures fast load times and ample space for your gaming library.

### **Enhanced Controls**

Experience superior control with the AYANEO 3’s advanced features:

### **Dual-Stage Hall-Effect Triggers**

Switch between a 7.5mm long travel for precise control and a 3mm short travel for rapid inputs, catering to different gaming scenarios.

### **Magnetic Suspension Motor**

Developed in collaboration with GuliKit Technology, this feature supports both linear and rotor motor vibration modes, providing realistic and immersive haptic feedback.

### **Keyboard and Mouse Mode**

Seamlessly switch between controller and keyboard/mouse inputs, enhancing versatility across various gaming and productivity applications.

### **Immersive Audio**

The AYANEO 3 delivers an exhilarating audio experience with its HyperSound 2.0 symmetrical dual speakers, providing clear and powerful sound that enhances gameplay immersion.

### **User Experience**

Running on Windows 11, the AYANEO 3 supports various applications, from demanding AAA titles to essential productivity tools, making it a versatile companion for both work and play. The device features an ergonomic design with customizable controls, ensuring comfort during extended gaming sessions. Robust connectivity options, including dual USB4 ports and an OCuLink interface for high-speed connections, facilitate seamless integration into any environment.
