## **Connectivity and Modular Versatility**

The [GPD Pocket 4](https://gpdstore.net/gpd-mini-laptop/gpd-pocket-4/) excels in connectivity, offering an [HDMI 2.1](https://gpdstore.net/product-attribute/i-o-video/1x-hdmi-2-1/) port and a USB-A 3.2 Gen 2 port on the left side, along with another USB-A port and a 3.5mm audio jack on the right. The back houses a 2.5Gbps Ethernet port, USB-C charging port, a USB4 port for high-speed peripherals, and the modular port system, a defining feature of GPD’s ultra-portable notebooks.
