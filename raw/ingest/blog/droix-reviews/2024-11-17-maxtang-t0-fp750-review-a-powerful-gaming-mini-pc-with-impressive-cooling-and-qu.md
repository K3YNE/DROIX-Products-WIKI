## Maxtang T0-FP750 Overview

Measuring approximately 5.31 x 5.00 x 2.16 inches (13.5 x 12.7 x 5.5 cm) and weighing 561g (1.2 lbs), the Maxtang T0-FP750 has a compact and efficient design ideal as a mini PC for home or office use.

Maxtang T0 FP750 Front

The front panel features two USB 3.2 ports, a USB-C 3.2 port, a 3.5mm audio jack, and a power button.

Maxtang T0 FP750 Back

On the back, you’ll find a 2.5Gbps Ethernet port, a USB-C 4 port, a USB-A 2.0 port, a DisplayPort, an HDMI port, and the power port.

Maxtang T0 FP750 Bottom

The abundance of USB ports, including two USB-C ports, provides excellent connectivity options, making it a versatile [mini PC](https://droix.net/product-category/mini-pcs/) for office tasks and gaming alike. With the USB-C, DisplayPort and HDMI you can connect up to three monitors at 4K or a single monitor at 8K.

## Maxtang T0-FP750 Technical Specifications

Maxtang T0 FP750 CPU GPU Specs

Powered by the AMD [Ryzen 7 8845HS](https://droix.net/product-attribute/processor-cpu-model/ryzen-8845hs/) processor and AMD Radeon 780M GPU, the Maxtang T0-FP750 is a robust gaming mini PC. With 16GB or 32GB dual-channel [DDR5](https://droix.net/product-attribute/memory-ram-technology/ddr5-so-dimm/) RAM options, upgradeable to 64GB, it can handle a variety of tasks from everyday work to intensive gaming. Storage choices include either a 512GB or 1TB M.2 2280 NVMe SSD, with an additional slot supporting up to 2TB of extra storage, making it well-suited as a mini PC for home or office users with extensive data needs.

For connectivity, it includes WiFi 6, [Bluetooth 5.2](https://droix.net/product-attribute/bluetooth/5-2/), and 2.5G Ethernet for fast wired connections. During our power consumption tests, the device averaged 9.3W while idle, 43W under software load, and peaked at 69.5W during full load. Despite intensive testing, the fan noise reached a maximum of just 56dB, while the highest temperature recorded was 53°C, which speaks to its excellent cooling system.

## Real World Gaming Performance

When testing for our Maxtang T0-FP750 review on a variety of titles for real-world gaming performance, we found it to be highly capable, delivering smooth gameplay across different genres and graphics settings.

Maxtang T0 FP750 Horizon Forbidden West

In **Horizon Forbidden West**, we opted for 720p resolution with medium graphics settings and used AMD FSR 2.2 Balanced mode to help maintain a stable frame rate. This demonstrates the T0-FP750’s aptitude as a gaming mini PC, allowing it to run AAA titles smoothly at lower resolutions.

Maxtang T0 FP750 Gaming Performance

In **Oceanhorn 2**, we aimed for a higher frame rate, selecting 720p with high graphics settings. The T0-FP750 maintained over 60 FPS, delivering a fluid experience with no noticeable lag or stutter. This flexibility in graphics options highlights the mini PC’s versatility, as it allows users to adjust settings based on performance needs. Those looking for a mini PC for home gaming will find the T0-FP750’s ability to manage 60 FPS at 720p advantageous, as it ensures smooth gameplay for most titles at comfortable settings.

With **Spider Man Remastered**, we mixed low and medium graphics settings at 720p to target a stable 60 FPS. The T0-FP750 handled the demanding city environment well, maintaining a consistent frame rate with minimal dips. Exploring the open-world environment felt smooth, and the mini PC’s cooling system managed heat effectively during these sessions. This game serves as a strong example of the T0-FP750’s gaming power and cooling efficiency, proving that it can handle resource-intensive titles over extended periods, making it a suitable mini PC for gaming.

The T0-FP750’s performance on this game proves it can manage games that rely heavily on CPU power and physics rendering, making it a capable mini PC for home or casual gaming setups.

Maxtang T0 FP750 Hades II

For users interested in indie or lower-demand titles, this mini PC can deliver high-resolution gaming experiences smoothly, making it a highly effective mini PC for home entertainment.

Overall, the Maxtang T0-FP750 demonstrated outstanding gaming performance across various titles.
