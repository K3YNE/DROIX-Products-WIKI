## AYA NEO Official Case Review

The top of the case features the [Aya](https://go.droix.co.uk/AYANEO) logo which looks cool and gives the case a premium feel than a generic case. The material feels high quality and is toughened to help prevent any knocks or scrapes.

AYA NEO official case

The [AYA NEO case](https://go.droix.co.uk/AYANEO-2021-CASE) has got a reinforced rubber handle which provides plenty of grip and comfortability when carrying the case.

AYA NEO rubber handle

The case flips open to reveal the inside. There are three compartments on the bottom where you can store cables and other items. There is also space on the lid for some small items.

AYA NEO case storage compartments

The flap in the middle thoughtfully acts as a protection for both the back and screen of the [AYA NEO](https://go.droix.co.uk/AYANEO-2021) to help prevent any scratches. It is secured with a Velcro tab.

AYA NEO securely inside the case

There’s some additional storage space on the flap for very small items such as [micro SD cards](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SD_card) or USB dongles. The lid can then be closed and zipped up, keeping everything secure.

AYA NEO Case

The carry case feels very high quality and hard wearing. Clearly, [Aya](https://go.droix.co.uk/AYANEO) have put some thought into the design with plenty of storage space for accessories. If you have an AYA NEO and want to keep it secure while travelling, then we can’t recommend the official case enough!
