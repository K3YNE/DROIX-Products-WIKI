## GMKTec NucBox M3’s Technical Specifications

Under the hood, the [GMKTec NucBox M3](https://droix.net/product/gmktec-nucbox-m3/) is powered by the Intel [Core i5 12450H](https://droix.net/product-attribute/processor-cpu-model/core-i5-12450h/), which boasts 8 cores and 12 threads, with a maximum clock speed of 4.40GHz. This mini PC with Intel processor, paired with the integrated Intel UHD Graphics, ensures smooth performance for a wide range of applications. The system supports up to 32GB of [DDR4](https://droix.net/product-attribute/memory-ram-technology/ddr4-so-dimm/) 3200MHz RAM, providing ample memory for multitasking and demanding software. Storage options include 512GB and 1TB PCIe 3.0 SSDs, ensuring fast read and write speeds for quick data access and application loading.

GMKTec NucBox M3 Spec

In terms of connectivity, the NucBox M3 is well-equipped. It features a variety of ports including one USB-C, three USB 3.2, one USB 3.0, two HDMI, a 3.5mm audio jack, and a LAN port. This selection allows for seamless integration with multiple peripherals and displays, supporting up to three screens simultaneously via HDMI and DisplayPort. For network connectivity, the mini PC includes [2.5Gbps Ethernet](https://droix.net/product-attribute/ethernet/1x-rj45-port-2500mbps/), WiFi 6, and [Bluetooth 5.2](https://droix.net/product-attribute/bluetooth/5-2/), ensuring fast and stable connections whether you’re wired or wireless. The device runs on Windows 11 Pro, offering the latest features and security enhancements from Microsoft.

## Build and Design

The [GMKTec NucBox M3](https://droix.net/product/gmktec-nucbox-m3/) sports a sleek and compact design, measuring just 6.4 x 6.1 x 4.1 inches (16.4 x 15.6 x 10.5 cm). Its small footprint makes it an excellent choice for environments with limited space, such as small home offices or compact workspaces. The mini PC comes with a VESA mount, allowing it to be attached to the back of a monitor or under a desk, further saving space.

The front panel includes two USB 3.2 ports and a power button, while the side panels feature air intake vents to ensure proper cooling. The rear panel houses the majority of the I/O ports, along with exhaust vents to dissipate heat effectively. This makes it an ideal mini PC for home office setups.

## Benchmark Results and Performance

Benchmarking the [GMKTec NucBox M3](https://droix.net/product/gmktec-nucbox-m3/) reveals its competitive performance in various scenarios. In CPU benchmarks like Cinebench R23, the NucBox M3 shows strong single-core performance, making it suitable for tasks that require high processing power. In multi-core tests, it achieves approximately 80% of the performance of the higher-end Intel i7 12650H, highlighting its efficiency in parallel processing tasks.

Gaming benchmarks show that while the integrated GPU handles 2D games and less demanding 3D titles like Rocket League at lower settings, more intensive games like Fortnite and Counter-Strike 2 require significant compromises in graphics quality and resolution for smooth gameplay. System benchmarks further underline its capability in handling day-to-day tasks and media consumption, with smooth 4K video playback and efficient multitasking abilities.
