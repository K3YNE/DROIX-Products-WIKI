Both models come with over 3000 games built in, with the possibility to add more via the external SD card slot. With the included A/V cables you can connect them to your TV. With the RS-07 Arcade you also get two game pads so you can play with a friend.

The [RS-97 Pro](https://droix.net/product-category/retro-game-console/) supports custom firmware (RS-07 Arcade supported soon!) so you can add emulators for systems including Amstrad, Spectrum, Amiga and many more.
