## Unboxing the Beelink SER 4

Inside we have a user manual which is in multiple languages including English, German, Spanish and Italian. Underneath is the [Beelink SER 4](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser4-ryzen-7-mini-pc/) mini PC. We will take a closer look at it shortly.

Beelink SER 4 mini PC

Inside the box is a power supply. We will include the correct adapter for your country. There are two HDMI cables. One is shorter than the other and is useful if mounting the mini PC behind your display.

Beelink SER 4 Unboxing

## Overview of the Beelink SER 4

The [SER 4](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser4-ryzen-7-mini-pc/) measures 4.9 x 4.4 x 1.6 inches (12.6 x 11.3 x 4.2 cm) and weighs 455 grams.

Beelink SER 4 Dimensions

Along the front are two USB 3 ports and a [USB Type-C](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USB-C) port. There’s a 3.5mm headphone jack and power button.

Beelink SER 4 Front

On the back is a gigabit ethernet port, two more USB 3.0 ports and two HDMI for up to triple monitor support.

Beelink SER 4 Back

You can remove the 4 screws on the bottom of the [Beelink SER 4](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser4-ryzen-7-mini-pc/) to install a 2.5” hard drive or SSD.

## System Benchmark

As part of our Beelink SER 4 review we will perform some system and gaming benchmarks. Then afterwards compare the results with other mini PC’s in the same mid-range price category.

### PassMark

PassMark pushes the CPU, GPU, RAM and storage to their maximum in a series of tests.

Passmark Benchmark

The [Beelink SER4](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser4-ryzen-7-mini-pc/) office mini PC scores 4484. We start with a very good score, amongst the highest in this price range!

### PCMark

PCMark is a series of more natural tests covering day to day tasks from web browsing to image processing.

PCMark Benchmark

The SER4 scores 5224 which is very impressive, it’s actually the highest score in the mid-range price category!

### Cinebench

Cinebench tests the CPU to see its performance with either single or multi-core tests.

Cinebench Benchmark

We ran the multi-core benchmark and got a score of 6653. It is a good score, with the Beelink SER 4 mini computer beating some of the high end mini PC’s!

### 3DMark

3DMark tests the CPU and GPU to see how well they work together in video processing tasks.

3DMark Benchmark

We got a score of 1209 on the [Beelink SER 4](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser4-ryzen-7-mini-pc/) which is a decent score. We don’t normally do graphics tests on mid-range PCs but we are getting some great results which are promising for some light gaming.

### CrystalDiskMark

CrystalDiskMark runs a series of tests on the storage to see its performance across different read/write patterns.

CrystalDiskMark Benchmark

We got a highest read speed of 2559 MB/s, and highest write speed of 1835 MB/s which are both great speeds for this price range.

## Games Benchmarks

### Forza Horizon 5

We start the game benchmarks with Forza Horizon 5 which is running at 720P on the Very Low graphics settings.

Beelink SER 4 Forza Horizon 5 Benchmark

At the end of the benchmark we got an average of 69 frames per second. As mentioned we do not usually benchmark games on mid-range PC’s but the [Beelink SER 4](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser4-ryzen-7-mini-pc/) actually performs very well.

### Final Fantasy XIV

Next we take a look at the Final Fantasy XIV benchmark. We are also running this at 720P but on the maximum graphics settings.

Beelink SER 4 Final Fantasy XIV Benchmark

We got a score of 5113 which is a standard rating for playing the game. Again it is very impressive, you can play the game fine at 720P on maximum graphics on the Beelink SER 4 home mini PC.

### Shadow of the Tomb Raider

We are running Shadow of the Tomb Raider at 720P on the Lowest graphics settings.

At the end of the benchmark we got an average of 55 frames per second which is very good for this quite highly demanding game.

### Cyberpunk 2077

Our final benchmark is Cyberpunk 2077 which, like Tomb Raider, is very demanding. We are running at 720P on the Low graphics settings.

Beelink SER 4 Cyberpunk 2077

We got an average of 30.66 frames per second at the end of the benchmark. It is a little too low to play this game as you will see frame drops in busier scenes. But still a very good score for this specification.
