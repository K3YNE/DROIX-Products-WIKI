## System Benchmarks

We continue our Beelink GK35 Pro review with some benchmarks which we can compare the performance with other models in this price range.

### PassMark

PassMark pushes the CPU, GPU, RAM and storage to their maximum in a series of tests.

PassMark Benchmark Result

The [Beelink GK35 Pro](https://droix.net/product/beelink-gk35/) scores 852. We will be comparing the scores with other similar priced models at the end of the benchmarks.

### PCMark

PCMark is a series of more natural tests covering day to day tasks from web browsing to image processing.

PCMark Benchmark Result

The [GK35 Pro](https://droix.net/product/beelink-gk35/) scores 1738. There’s good scores for boot times, software loading speed and working with office documents.

### 3DMark

[3DMark](https://benchmarks.ul.com/3dmark) tests the CPU and GPU to see how well they work together in video processing tasks. Graphics are not just for gaming, but also used for video decoding for example.

3DMark Benchmark Result

The Beelink scores 142. It is a low score but it is enough for tasks such as high quality 4K video decoding as an example.

### CrystalDiskMark

CrystalDiskMark runs a series of tests on the storage to see its performance across different read/write patterns.

CrystalDiskMark Benchmark Results

We get a highest read speed of 501 MB/s, and highest write speed of 505 MB/s.
