## DroiX NT8 Clear USB Hub Unboxing & Overview

[DroiX NT8 Clear USB Hub](https://droix.net/product/droix-nt8-clear-usb-hub/) measures around 4 x 1.6 x 0.5 in (10.4 x 4.3 x 1.3 cm) and weighs 131g. The case is made partially with a zinc alloy frame for heat dissipation, and clear plastic top so you can see the components inside.

DroiX NT8 clear USB hub unboxed

The USB Type-C cable measures around 7.2 inches (18.5 cm) and connects to your device’s [USB Type-C port](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USB-C).

DroiX NT8 clear USB hub front view

On one side from left to right are a [HDMI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HDMI) port which you can connect to a TV or monitor supporting up to 4K 60Hz. Beside that is a SD and micro SD card reader.

DroiX NT8 clear USB hub side view

On the next side we have a gigabit ethernet port for high speed and secure data transfer.

DroiX NT8 clear USB hub back view

And on the final side we have three USB 3.0 ports and a USB Type-C port which supports up to 100W for charging the connected device.

DroiX NT8 clear USB hub bottom view

The bottom has two non-slip pads to help keep it secure on your desk.

Or with a PC or mini PC, you can use the built-in HDMI together with the HDMI on the hub for dual monitor support.
