## GPD WIN MAX 2 2025 OVERVIEW

We start our [GPD WIN MAX 2 2025](https://droix.net/product/gpd-win-max-2-2025/) review with an overview of the device itself. The [GPD WIN MAX 2 2025](https://droix.net/product/gpd-win-max-2-2025/) measures around 8.9 x 6.2 x 0.9 inches (22.7 x 16 x 2.3 cm) and weighs 1098g (2.42 lbs).

GPD WIN MAX 2 2025 Dimensions

The lid flips open to reveal a great looking 10.1” display. By default its 1200P resolution but does support up to 1600P. It is a touchscreen and also active stylus compatible, supporting pens with up to 4096 levels of pressure.

GPD WIN MAX 2 2025 Camera



A gloomy day in London

On the hinge we have a 2MP camera with up to 1612×1212 super wide angle resolution and high refresh rate. It’s suitable for video calls but we would  have liked to have seen an upgraded camera like the amazing one on the [GPD Duo](https://droix.net/product/gpd-duo/).

On to the base we have the gaming controls hidden with the removable covers which can be stored in the back of the device. There are dual analogue sticks, D-Pad and the usual gaming buttons. In the middle we have a nicely sized touchpad which is pressable for left and right mouse clicks. It’s a little strange having the pad at the top but you get used to it pretty fast.

GPD WIN MAX 2 2025 Keyboard Close Up

The keyboard takes up most of the space with laptop sized keys that feel great to type with. I wrote this review using the [GPD WIN MAX 2 2025](https://droix.net/product/gpd-win-max-2-2025/) and had no issues at all. The keys are well spaced and require minimal effort to press. Very nice to use!

GPD WIN MAX 2 2025 Front Side



GPD WIN MAX 2 2025 Left Side



GPD WIN MAX 2 2025 Right Side

On the front side we have the power button with an integrated fingerprint scanner for fast and secure logging in. The left side has both a full size and micro sd card reader which is always useful to have. The right side has two USB 3.2 ports for peripherals.

GPD WIN MAX 2 2025 Back Side

The back side features trigger and shoulder buttons on either side. And along the back we have two USB 4 for high speed devices, a HDMI 2.1 port, and [OCuLink](https://droix.net/blogs/oculink-transforming-graphics-card-connectivity/) port for connecting up to an eGPU for more graphics performance. And we finish up with a 3.5mm audio jack.
