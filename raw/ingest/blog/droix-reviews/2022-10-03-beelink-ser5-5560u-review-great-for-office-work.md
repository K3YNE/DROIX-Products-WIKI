## Beelink SER5 5560U Overview

The [Beelink SER5 5560U](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser-pro-amd-ryzen-5-mini-pc/) measures around 4.9 x 4.4 x 1.6 inches (12.6 x 11.3 x 4.2 cm) and weighs around 430g. On the front there are two [USB 3.0](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USB_3.0) and one [USB Type-C](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USB-C) port for easy connecting peripherals to. Beside those are a 3.5mm headphone jack and the power button.

Beelink SER5 5560U front

On the back are a gigabit ethernet port for fast data transfer. There are two USB 2.0 ports and two [HDMI](https://www.hdmi.org/spec/index) ports for dual monitor display.

Beelink SER5 5560U back

The bottom can be opened by unscrewing the four screws, you can upgrade the RAM and storage as well as installing the 2.5” drive.

## System Benchmarks

### PassMark

PassMark Result

Continuing our Beelink SER 5 review with, as always, the system benchmarks. PassMark pushes the CPU, GPU, RAM and storage to their maximum in a series of tests. The [Beelink SER5](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser-pro-amd-ryzen-5-mini-pc/) got a final score of 4441. We will be comparing the benchmark results with other models further on.

### PCMark

Beelink SER5 5560U PCMark Benchmark Result

[PCMark](https://benchmarks.ul.com/pcmark10) is a series of more natural tests covering day to day tasks from web browsing to image processing. We got a final score of 5467. You can read a more in depth breakdown of the test scores on our blog post linked in the description.

### Cinebench

Cinebench Result

Cinebench tests the CPU to see its performance with either single or multi-core tests. We got a multi core test score of 7660. We usually see very good scores for AMD processors on Cinebench and the [Beelink SER5 56560U](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser-pro-amd-ryzen-5-mini-pc/) mini PC is no exception.

### 3DMark

3DMark Result

3DMark tests the CPU and GPU to see how well they work together in video processing tasks. We got a final score of 1080 which was a little lower than we expected.

### CrystalDiskMark

Beelink SER5 5560U Cinebench Result

CrystalDiskMark runs a series of tests on the storage to see its performance across different read/write patterns. We get a highest read speed of 2552, and highest write speed of 1905 MB/s.

## Gaming Benchmarks

We continue our Beelink SER5 review with some gaming benchmarks. While this is not a gaming mini PC, the AMD processors are generally quite good for some light gaming.

### Forza Horizon 5

Forza Horizon 5 Result

We start the gaming benchmarks with Forza Horizon 5 running at 720P on the Very Low graphics settings. We get an average frame per second of 66 which is very good.

### Street Fighter V

Beelink SER5 5560U Street Fighter V Result

Next in our benchmarks we are running Street Fighter V at 720P on the Maximum graphics settings. At the end of the first match we get an average frame per second of 46. AMD processors are usually slower than Intels for this game so it’s a fair score.

### Final Fantasy XIV

Final Fantasy XIV Result

For the Final Fantasy XIV benchmark we are again running at 1280×720 on the Maximum graphics settings. We get a final score of 4702 which puts it into the standard category, you could play the game just fine at maximum graphics settings

### Shadow of the Tomb Raider

Street Fighter V Result

Next in our benchmarks is Shadow of the Tomb Raider. We have it running on the low settings at 720P. We get an average frame per second of 40 which is good. You could play the game fine with some minor slowdowns in very busy scenes.

### Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 Result

For our final benchmark we are pushing the [Beelink SER5](https://droix.net/product/beelink-ser-pro-amd-ryzen-5-mini-pc/) with Cyberpunk which is a high demand game. We are running at 720P on the Low graphics settings and get a final average frame per second of 28.93. You probably would not want to play the game on this mini computer. but for the specification it’s a good score.
