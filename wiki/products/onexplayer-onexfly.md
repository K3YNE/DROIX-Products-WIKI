## Summary

The ONEXFLY is a mid-size Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer]], released in late 2023, available in Ryzen 7 7840U and Ryzen 7 8840U configurations. DROIX reviewed it as "a solid medium sized handheld gaming PC," sitting between the larger ONEXPLAYER 2 and the more compact Mini in terms of form factor. It runs full Windows 11 and is powered by AMD's Phoenix-generation APUs with RDNA 3 integrated graphics.

## Design & Build

The ONEXFLY adopts a more ergonomic, mid-size handheld form factor compared to the larger ONEXPLAYER 2. It retains the full Windows handheld gamepad layout with thumbsticks, triggers, face buttons, and bumpers.

### In the Box

- 1x ONEXPLAYER ONEXFLY
- 1x Power Adapter
- 1x USB Type-C Cable
- 1x User Manual

## Emulation Notes

Per the DROIX review, emulation performance on the ONEXFLY is excellent:

- All emulators up to PS1 and Dreamcast era work at lower TDPs, with rendering resolution scaling available.
- PlayStation 3 (RPCS3) runs very well for many games.
- Yuzu and Ryujinx (Switch) run very well on most games, with occasional dips that don't significantly impact the experience.
- The DROIX review specifically notes: "Yuzu and Ryujinx emulator on Payday 3 runs great on the ONEXPLAYER ONEXFLY."
