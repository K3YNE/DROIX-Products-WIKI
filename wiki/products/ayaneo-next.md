# AYANEO Next

Legacy Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]], based on the AMD Zen 3 architecture. Powered by the AMD Ryzen 7 5825U (8 cores / 16 threads, 2.0–4.5 GHz, 15–28W) with Radeon Graphics (8 CUs, 2000 MHz). Features a 7-inch H-IPS 1280x800 16:9 215 PPI touchscreen, 16GB LPDDR4X, and up to 2TB PCIe 3.0 storage. This is a prior-generation device — current AYANEO Windows handhelds use Ryzen 7000/8000 series or Ryzen AI processors. Currently out of stock at DROIX; available in New and Refurbished (Grade B) conditions.

## Legacy Note

The AYANEO Next uses the AMD Zen 3 Ryzen 7 5825U — two generations behind current AYANEO Windows handhelds. It lacks USB4, OCuLink, and Wi-Fi 6E found on newer models like the [[ayaneo-3]] and [[ayaneo-flip]]. Its 1280x800 display and PCIe 3.0 storage are also below current standards.
