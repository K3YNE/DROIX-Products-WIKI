# ACEPC PicoBox Mini

[[acepc|ACEPC]] PicoBox Mini is an ultra-compact budget mini PC powered by the Intel N100 (4c/4t, up to 3.4 GHz, 6W TDP). Ships with 16 GB LPDDR4 RAM and 512 GB or 1 TB M.2 2242 SATA SSD. Features triple HDMI 4K@60Hz output, dual 1 Gbps Ethernet, and Wi-Fi 5. Measures just 3.52 x 3.52 x 1.71 inches (203.7 g). Runs Windows 11 Pro. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
