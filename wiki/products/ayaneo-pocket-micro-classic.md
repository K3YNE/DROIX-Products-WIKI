## Summary

The AYANEO Pocket Micro Classic is an Android gaming handheld from [[ayaneo]], reviewed by DROIX in April 2025. It is a retro-styled compact handheld in the Pocket Micro family, described as a "sleek retro upgrade with key design changes" over its predecessor. The device targets retro gaming and emulation enthusiasts seeking a premium compact form factor with Android under the hood. DROIX reviewed and stocks the Pocket Micro Classic.

## Design & Build

The Pocket Micro Classic introduces design changes compared to earlier Pocket Micro models, with a focus on a retro aesthetic. As a compact Android handheld it is smaller than AYANEO's larger Pocket Air devices. Specific dimension and weight data are not available in current sources.
