## Summary

The AYANEO AIR 1S is a Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo]], the successor to the [[ayaneo-air]] in AYANEO's slim handheld line. It is powered by AMD's Ryzen 7 8840U (with a separate driver package confirming 8840U support alongside an earlier model variant). The AIR 1S prioritises a slim form factor and improved APU performance over the original AIR while maintaining AYANEO's premium handheld build quality. Windows 11 with AYA SPACE companion software is standard. DROIX stocks the AYANEO AIR 1S.
