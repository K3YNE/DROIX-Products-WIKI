# ONEXPLAYER ONEXFLY F1 Pro

Award-winning 7" OLED 144Hz Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]]. Named PC Gamer's "Best Gaming Handheld PC in 2024". Available with [[amd-ryzen-ai-9-hx-370|AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370]] (Strix Point), Ryzen AI 9 HX 365, or Ryzen 7 8840U processors, all paired with AMD Radeon RDNA 3/3.5 integrated graphics. Features Harman speakers, active cooling, and up to 4TB storage. In stock at DROIX.

## Design & Build

- 7" OLED display (16:9), 144Hz refresh rate
- 800 nits brightness, 315 PPI, 10,000:1 contrast ratio
- Touchscreen
- Fingerprint sensor (Windows Hello)
- Windows 11 Home
- Active cooling: liquid bearing fan up to 4700 RPM, 3 pure copper heat pipes, pure aluminium radiator fins (14,952 mm2 heat dissipation area)
- Harman AudioEFX dual speakers
- OneXConsole Game Center software
