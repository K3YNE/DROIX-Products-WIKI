# GPD WIN Mini

The GPD WIN Mini is [[gpd|GPD]]'s compact clamshell-style Windows handheld, refreshed annually from 2023 to 2025. It features a ~7" display, built-in keyboard, and OCuLink support for use with the [[gpd-g1]] eGPU. Sold by [[droix|DROIX]] with a 2-year warranty.

## Yearly Models

| Year | CPU | GPU | RAM | Storage | Wi-Fi | Bluetooth |
|------|-----|-----|-----|---------|-------|-----------|
| 2023 (original) | AMD Ryzen 5 7640U / Ryzen 7 7840U | Radeon 760M / 780M | Up to 64 GB LPDDR5 @ 6400 MT/s | Up to 2 TB PCIe Gen 4 NVMe | Wi-Fi 6 | 5.2 |
| 2024 | AMD Ryzen 5 7640U / Ryzen 7 8840U | Radeon 760M / 780M (12 CUs) | Up to 32 GB LPDDR5 @ 6400 MT/s | Up to 2 TB PCIe 4.0 NVMe | Wi-Fi 6E | 5.3 |
| 2025 | AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 / Ryzen 7 8840U | Radeon 890M / 780M | Up to 64 GB LPDDR5X @ 7500 MT/s | Up to 4 TB PCIe 4.0 NVMe | Wi-Fi 6E | 5.3 |

## Design & Build

- Clamshell form factor with integrated keyboard and gamepad controls.
- Compact size — the smallest WIN-series handheld.
- OCuLink connectivity for [[gpd-g1]] eGPU docking.
- Original 2023 model included carry strap and USB-C to USB-A converter in-box.
