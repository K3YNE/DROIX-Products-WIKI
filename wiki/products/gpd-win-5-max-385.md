# GPD WIN 5 (Ryzen AI Max 385)

Lower-cost variant of the [[gpd-win-5]]. Keeps the Strix Halo platform but with the 8-core / 32-CU Ryzen AI Max 385 instead of the flagship [[gpd-win-5-max-395|Max+ 395]]. Shares design, display, cooling, and resources with the 395 variant — only the APU and (typically) RAM ceiling differ.

## Specs

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| APU | AMD Ryzen AI Max 385 |
| CPU cores / threads | 8 / 16 |
| GPU compute units | 32 |
| RAM options | 32 / 64 GB unified |
| Storage slots | 1× Mini SSD + 1× M.2 (each to 4 TB) |
| TDP | Up to 85 W |
| External battery | 350 g, modular |
| Device weight | 565 g |

## Variant-specific notes

- Expect noticeably lower multi-core scores (8 vs 16 cores) and lower GPU ceiling (32 vs 40 CUs) than the [[gpd-win-5-max-395]].
- Single-core and 1080p light-gaming performance remain close to the 395 at lower TDPs.
- Preferred SKU for buyers who prioritise price or don't need 96 GB VRAM / heaviest GPU workloads.
