# Retroid Pocket 4 PRO

The [[retroid|Retroid]] Pocket 4 PRO is a portable Android retro gaming handheld available in two models: the standard Pocket 4 (MediaTek Dimensity 900, 4 GB RAM, Android 11) and the PRO (Dimensity 1100, 8 GB RAM, Android 13). Both share a 4.7-inch touchscreen and 128 GB UFS 3.1 storage. The PRO model offers 1080p output via USB-C in addition to the shared 720p HDMI output. Currently out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
