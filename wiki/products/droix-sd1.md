# DROIX SD1 Docking Station

6-in-1 USB-C docking station from [[droix|DROIX]], designed for handheld gaming PCs. Aluminium construction, 130 g. Compatible with Steam Deck, ASUS ROG Ally, MSI Claw, GPD Win 4, ONEXPLAYER 2, AYANEO 2S, and AYANEO GEEK 1S. Features USB-C PD 100W charging, 4K@60Hz HDMI output, Gigabit Ethernet, and 3x USB-A 3.0 ports. Plug-and-play, no drivers required. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
