# AYN Odin 2 Mini

[[ayn|AYN]]'s compact 5-inch Android handheld powered by the [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-gen-2|Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2]]. The smallest member of the Odin 2 family, featuring a Mini LED display with 1100 nits peak brightness and 1,000,000:1 contrast ratio. Available in Black and White.

## Design & Build

- 5" Mini LED, 1920x1080, 16:9, 440 PPI, touchscreen, 60 Hz, 1100 nits, 1,000,000:1 contrast
- [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-gen-2|Snapdragon 8 Gen 2]], Adreno 740 GPU
- Android 13
- 8 / 12 GB LPDDR5X RAM
- 128 / 256 GB UFS 4.0 storage + MicroSD expansion
- Active cooling system
- 5000 mAh battery, 65W QC 5.0 fast charging
- 320 g, 19.8 x 8.5 x 1.95 cm
- Colours: Black, White

## Ports & I/O

- USB 3.1 Type-C
- Micro HDMI video output
- 3.5 mm headphone jack
- Dual speakers
- Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.3

## SKU Configurations

- 8 GB LPDDR5X / 128 GB UFS 4.0
- 12 GB LPDDR5X / 256 GB UFS 4.0
