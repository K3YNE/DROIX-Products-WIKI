## Summary

The AYANEO GEEK is a Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo]], positioned as a lower-cost entry point into the AYANEO lineup while sharing the AMD Ryzen 7 6800U APU with the [[ayaneo-2]]. A subsequent GEEK 1S model refreshed the platform, likely with an updated processor. DROIX reviewed and stocked the GEEK as a budget-friendly option in the 6800U handheld segment. Emulation and gaming performance on the GEEK mirrors that of the AYANEO 2 — everything up to the Dolphin emulator era runs without issues, with strong PS3 (RPCS3) results and decent Nintendo Switch (Ryujinx/Yuzu) performance on compatible titles.

## Design & Build

The AYANEO GEEK shares AYANEO's established handheld design language with the AYANEO 2, featuring Hall Effect analogue sticks and the AYA SPACE 2 companion software for TDP control and game frontend. The GEEK differentiated itself primarily on price rather than form factor or hardware configuration.
