# RockTek GB1

The [[rocktek|RockTek]] GB1 is an ultra-compact Android TV mini box powered by the Realtek RTD1315C (quad-core Cortex-A55 @1.7 GHz) with Mali-G57 GPU. Runs Android TV 12 with the Google TV interface. Supports 4K streaming with Dolby Vision HDR, HDR10, and Dolby Atmos audio. Features Widevine L1 for premium streaming services. Measures just 70 x 70 x 15 mm. In stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
