# ONEXPLAYER Super X

14" AMOLED gaming tablet from [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]] powered by [[amd-ryzen-ai-max|AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 / Max 385]] (Strix Halo). Features a 2880x1800 120Hz VRR display with 100% DCI-P3, stylus support, detachable magnetic RGB keyboard, and a 170-degree kickstand. Dual-fan cooling with cTDP adjustable from 45W to 120W. Weighs approximately 1.3 kg. Out of stock at DROIX.

## Design & Build

- 14" AMOLED display with 170-degree full-width kickstand
- Detachable magnetic RGB backlit keyboard
- 12.5mm thickness at thinnest point, ~1.3 kg
- Dual-fan cooling system with double pure copper vapour chamber
- Fingerprint sensor (Windows Hello)
- AI front camera for Windows Hello facial recognition
- Removable Li-Po battery (85.58 Wh)
- Windows 11 Home

## Display

- 14" AMOLED, 2880x1800 (16:10), 243 PPI
- 120Hz refresh rate with Variable Refresh Rate (VRR)
- 100% DCI-P3 colour gamut
- 500 nits peak brightness
- Infinite contrast ratio (OLED)
- AMD FreeSync support
- 4096-level pressure-sensitive stylus support
- Touchscreen
