# DROIX Proteus 11

DROIX's own-brand mini PC under the Proteus sub-brand. Available in two 11th-gen Intel Tiger Lake configurations: Core i5-1135G7 and Core i7-1165G7. Features Thunderbolt 4 for eGPU connectivity, Intel Iris Xe graphics, up to 64 GB DDR4 SO-DIMM RAM @3200 MHz, and up to 4 TB NVMe storage plus a 2.5" SATA expansion bay. Ships with Windows 11 Pro. VESA-mountable compact chassis. All Proteus devices include a 2-year warranty from [[droix|DROIX]]. Out of stock.
