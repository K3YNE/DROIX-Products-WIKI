# GPD WIN 5 Smart Dock

First-party docking accessory for the [[gpd-win-5]]. DROIX's review verdict was negative — the dock does not pass charge through to the handheld itself (only to the battery slot), making it hard to recommend over a generic USB-C hub.

## Design & Build

- 317 g, all-plastic construction.
- Battery slot for the WIN 5's modular 350 g external battery.

## Ports & I/O

- HDMI 2.1 (4K 144 Hz output).
- 3× USB-A.
- Gigabit Ethernet.
- USB-C: **charges the battery slot only — does not charge the handheld directly**.

No firmware, BIOS, or driver resources applicable — this is an accessory.
