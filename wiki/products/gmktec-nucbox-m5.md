# GMKTec NucBox M5

[[gmktec|GMKTec]] NucBox M5 is a mid-range mini PC powered by the AMD Ryzen 7 5700U (8c/16t, base 1.8 GHz, boost 4.3 GHz, 10-25W TDP) with Radeon Graphics. Ships with 16 GB DDR4 and 512 GB NVMe PCIe 3.0 SSD with a second M.2 slot for expansion. Features triple display via HDMI, DP, and USB-C, dual 1 Gbps Ethernet, and Wi-Fi 6E. Runs Windows 11 Pro. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
