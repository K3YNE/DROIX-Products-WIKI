# Miyoo A30

Budget retro gaming handheld from [[miyoo|Miyoo]]. Powered by an Allwinner A33 quad-core ARM Cortex-A7 @1.2 GHz with Mali-400 MP2 GPU. 2.8-inch IPS display at 640x480 (4:3). 512 MB RAM, 64 GB MicroSD included. Wi-Fi 5 for multiplayer via RetroArch. 2600 mAh battery for up to 5 hours of gaming. Compact at 5 x 2.2 x 0.59 inches, 186 g. Includes free carry case. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
