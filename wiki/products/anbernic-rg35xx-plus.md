# Anbernic RG35XX Plus

Budget retro gaming handheld from [[anbernic|Anbernic]] running Linux. Allwinner H700 ARM Cortex-A53 quad-core @ 1.5 GHz, Mali-G31 MP2 GPU. 3.5" IPS 640x480 4:3 display at 228 PPI. 1 GB LPDDR4 RAM. Dual MicroSD slots (64 GB or 64+128 GB configurations). Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 4.2. Supports wireless and wired controllers. Built-in vibration motor. Available in Grey, White, and Transparent Black.
