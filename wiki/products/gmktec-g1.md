# GMKTec G1

[[gmktec|GMKTec]] G1 is a budget office mini PC powered by the Intel Alder Lake N95 (4c/4t, up to 3.4 GHz, 15W). Ships with 8 GB DDR4 and 256/512 GB NVMe PCIe 4.0. Features dual HDMI 4K@60Hz, 2.5 Gbps Ethernet, and Wi-Fi 6. Runs Windows 11 Pro with Linux support. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
