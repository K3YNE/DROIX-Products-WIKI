# GPD DUO

The GPD DUO is [[gpd|GPD]]'s dual-screen AMOLED mini laptop featuring two 13.3" displays with active capacitive stylus support. Available with AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 or Ryzen 7 8840U processors and up to 8 TB total storage (dual 4 TB SSDs). Sold by [[droix|DROIX]] with a 2-year warranty.

## Specs

| Spec | Detail |
|------|--------|
| CPU | AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 / Ryzen 7 8840U |
| GPU | Radeon 890M (12 CUs @ 2900 MHz) / Radeon 780M (12 CUs @ 2700 MHz) |
| RAM | Up to 64 GB LPDDR5X @ 7500 MT/s |
| Storage | Up to 8 TB (4 TB + 4 TB) PCIe 4.0 NVMe 2280 |
| Display | Dual 13.3" AMOLED, active capacitive stylus support |
| In-box | Power plug (EU/US), USB Type-C cable, user manual |

## Design & Build

- Dual 13.3" AMOLED screens in a laptop-style clamshell form factor.
- Active capacitive stylus support on both displays.
- One of the few dual-screen productivity UMPCs on the market.
