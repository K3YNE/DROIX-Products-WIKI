# AYANEO Pocket VERT

Vertical-orientation Android handheld from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]]. Positioned as the most premium vertical Android handheld in AYANEO's lineup. The [[ayaneo-pocket-vert-product-page]] identifies the SoC as [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-plus-gen-1|Snapdragon 8+ Gen 1]] and confirms Android 14.

## Design & Build

- Vertical form factor (portrait display, controls on the sides).
- CNC aluminum construction, premium AYANEO build tier.
- MagicTouch hidden dual-mode touchpad below the face buttons and MagicSwitch scroll wheel.
- Diamond-cut shoulder buttons.
- iSpace software ecosystem.

## Display

- 3.5-inch 1600 x 1440 touchscreen, 10:9 aspect ratio, 615 PPI, 450 nits, and 700:1 contrast ratio per [[ayaneo-pocket-vert-product-page]].
- Contradiction: [[ayaneo-pocket-vert-product-page]] body calls the panel LTPS, while the attribute table lists LCD.

## Ports & I/O

- USB 3.1 Type-C with video output; body copy claims external display output up to 4K.
- Dual speakers.
- Wi-Fi 6E and Bluetooth 5.2.

## Storage and Battery

- 8GB / 128GB UFS 3.1 and 12GB / 256GB UFS 3.1 configurations per [[ayaneo-pocket-vert-product-page]].
- MicroSD expansion.
- 6000 mAh battery.
