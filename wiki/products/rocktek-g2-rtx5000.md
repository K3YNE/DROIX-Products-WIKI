# RockTek G2 RTX5000

The [[rocktek|RockTek]] G2 RTX-5000 is a bundle variant of the [[rocktek-g2|RockTek G2]] Android TV box that includes the GP1500 Bluetooth game controller. Same Amlogic S905X4-K hardware with 4 GB LPDDR4 and 64 GB eMMC. Runs Google Certified Android TV 11. The GP1500 controller features D-Pad, dual analogue sticks, and gaming buttons for Android gaming and emulation. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].

| Controller | 1x GP1500 Bluetooth controller |
