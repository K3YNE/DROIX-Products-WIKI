# Anbernic RG35XXSP

Budget clamshell retro gaming handheld from [[anbernic|Anbernic]] with a GBA SP-style flip design, magnetic closure, and Hall switch auto-wake. Allwinner H700 ARM Cortex-A53 quad-core @ 1.5 GHz, Mali-G31 MP2 GPU. 3.5" IPS 640x480 4:3 display at 228 PPI. 1 GB LPDDR4 RAM. Dual MicroSD slots. Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 4.2. Available in Silver, Gray, Transparent Black, Transparent Blue, Transparent Green, Transparent Red, and Transparent White.
