# ONEXPLAYER ONEXGPU 2

Second-generation portable eGPU docking station from [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]] featuring the AMD Radeon RX 7800M (RDNA 3) with 12GB GDDR6. A significant upgrade over the [[onexplayer-onexgpu|ONEXGPU]] with a more powerful GPU, HDMI 2.1, DisplayPort 2.0, Ethernet, and aerospace-grade aluminium construction. Benchmark scores: Time Spy 15,916, Fire Strike 42,949. In stock at DROIX.

## Design & Build

- Aerospace-grade aluminium alloy chassis (CNC-cut, high-pressure die-cast)
- Polished, sandblasted, and anodised finish
- Advanced thermal management: 3600 RPM fan, 20.75 CFM airflow, 46.6-53.2 dB noise
- 330W GaN fast-charging power supply
- 100W reverse charging to connected devices
- Magnetic cover for easy M.2 SSD access

## GPU

- AMD Radeon RX 7800M (RDNA 3)
- Up to 2145 MHz
- 12GB GDDR6 dedicated memory

## Benchmarks

- 3DMark Time Spy (DX12 1440p): 15,916
- 3DMark Fire Strike (DX11 1080p): 42,949
- Outperforms RTX 4070 Mobile and RX 7600M XT per ONEXPLAYER claims
- Up to 245% higher FPS vs standard setups when paired with AMD 8840U

## Storage

- 1x M.2 2280 PCIe 3.0 NVMe slot (optional: 1TB/2TB/4TB)
- 1x MicroSD card slot

## Ports & I/O

- 1x OCuLink
- 1x USB 4.0 Type-C @ 40 GB/s
- 1x USB 4.0 Type-C DP (8K@60Hz)
- 2x USB-A 3.2 Gen 2
- 1x HDMI 2.1
- 2x DisplayPort (4K@60Hz)
- 1x RJ45 Ethernet
- Supports up to 3 screens simultaneously

## Power

- 330W GaN charger included
- 100W reverse charging to connected devices

## Availability

In stock at DROIX. Available new and refurbished (Class A).
