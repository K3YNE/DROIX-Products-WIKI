# Minisforum EliteMini UM690

The [[minisforum|Minisforum]] EliteMini UM690 is a high-performance mini PC powered by the AMD Ryzen 9 6900HX (8c/16t, base 3.3 GHz, boost 4.9 GHz, 45W TDP) with Radeon 680M (12 cores, 2400 MHz) integrated graphics. Supports up to 64 GB DDR5 RAM and up to 4 TB NVMe PCIe 4.0 storage with a 2.5" SATA bay. Features USB 4 (8K@60Hz via DP alt-mode, eGPU compatible), dual HDMI 4K@60Hz, and 2.5 Gbps Ethernet. Runs Windows 11 Pro. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
