# GMKTec NucBox G3

[[gmktec|GMKTec]] NucBox G3 is a compact budget mini PC available with Intel N100 (up to 3.4 GHz) or N150 (up to 3.6 GHz) processors, both 4c/4t at 6W TDP. Ships with 8 or 16 GB DDR4 RAM and 256/512 GB storage (NGFF SATA or NVMe PCIe 3.0). Features dual HDMI 4K@60Hz output, 2.5 Gbps Ethernet, and Wi-Fi 6. Also available as barebones. In stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
