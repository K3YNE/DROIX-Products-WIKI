# AYANEO Flip KB / DS

The original AYANEO Flip clamshell Windows handheld from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]], available in two models: the **DS** (dual-screen) with a 7-inch main display and a 3.5-inch IPS 960x640 3:2 secondary screen, and the **KB** (keyboard) model with a physical keyboard in place of the second screen. Powered by an AMD Ryzen 7 8840U (or 7840U) with Radeon 780M. Features a 7-inch IPS 1080p 120 Hz 400-nit touchscreen, up to 64GB LPDDR5X, and up to 2TB PCIe 4.0 storage. Connectivity includes USB4, OCuLink, and Wi-Fi 6E. Currently out of stock at DROIX. Succeeded by the [[ayaneo-flip-1s-ds|AYANEO Flip 1S DS]].

## Model Variants

- **DS (Dual-Screen)**: Second 3.5-inch IPS display inside the clamshell lid, similar in concept to the [[ayaneo-flip-1s-ds|Flip 1S DS]].
- **KB (Keyboard)**: Physical keyboard replaces the second screen.

These are form-factor variants sharing the same APU and main display; they do not warrant separate variant pages.
