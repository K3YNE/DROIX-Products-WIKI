# Anbernic RG353P

Dual-boot retro gaming handheld from [[anbernic|Anbernic]]. RK3566 quad-core Cortex-A55 @ 1.8 GHz, Mali-G52 2EE GPU @ 850 MHz. 3.5" IPS touchscreen, 640x480, 4:3. Runs Android 11 + Linux dual boot. 2 GB LPDDR4X RAM, 32 GB eMMC. Wi-Fi 5, BT 4.2, Mini HDMI. Retro controller-inspired design.
