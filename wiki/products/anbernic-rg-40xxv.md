# Anbernic RG 40XXV

Budget retro gaming handheld from [[anbernic|Anbernic]]. H700 ARM Cortex-A53 quad-core @ 1.5 GHz, Mali-G31 MP2 GPU. 4" IPS display, 640x480, 4:3 aspect ratio. Runs Linux with custom firmware support. Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 4.2, micro HDMI output.
