# AYN Odin 3

[[ayn|AYN]]'s flagship Android handheld, powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite. Became the Android performance benchmark champion on launch, overtaking the AYN Odin 2 Portal that was the prior reference device in DROIX reviews.

## Design & Build

- Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite SoC.
- Swappable buttons — a distinguishing AYN feature.
- Glass front, RGB analog sticks.
- Optional TPU grip accessories.
