## Summary

The AYANEO AM01S is a Windows mini PC from [[ayaneo]], a current-generation member of the AM-series mini PC line. A key feature is a built-in small screen, confirmed by the driver package which explicitly includes a "built-in small screen driver". This continues AYANEO's approach (seen also on the [[ayaneo-am02]]) of integrating a compact secondary display into their mini PC chassis. DROIX stocks the AYANEO AM01S.
