# GPD MicroPC 2

Successor to GPD's 2019 MicroPC. A 7" 2-in-1 UMPC aimed at IT professionals and field technicians rather than gamers. Built around low-power Intel N-series silicon; notable omission vs the original: no RS232 serial port.

## Design & Build

- 7" rotating touchscreen, 2-in-1 laptop/tablet form factor.
- 500 g.
- Small but usable thumb-typable keyboard.

## Display

- 7" touchscreen, 1080p.

## Ports & I/O

- 2.5 GbE Ethernet.
- HDMI 2.1.
- Multiple USB ports.
- Supports up to 4 external monitors.
- **No RS232 serial port** — removed vs the original MicroPC. Controversial omission for field-work users.

## Variants

- [[gpd-micropc-2-n250]] — Intel N250, 4 cores. Lower-cost SKU.
- [[gpd-micropc-2-n300]] — Intel N300, 8 cores. Recommended for sustained multi-threaded workloads.
