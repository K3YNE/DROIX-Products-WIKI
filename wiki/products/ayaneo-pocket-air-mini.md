## Summary

The AYANEO Pocket AIR Mini is an Android gaming handheld from [[ayaneo]], a compact sibling to the [[ayaneo-pocket-air]]. As the name implies it is a smaller form factor device in the Pocket Air family, targeting retro gaming and emulation in a more pocketable size. Firmware for the device is distributed as a full flash package (including drivers, flashing tools, documentation, and system package). DROIX stocks the AYANEO Pocket AIR Mini.
