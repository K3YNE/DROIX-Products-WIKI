# AYANEO Pocket Micro

Ultra-compact Android handheld from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]] powered by a MediaTek Helio G99 (8 cores, 2.2 GHz) with Mali-G57 GPU at 950 MHz. Features a 3.5-inch IPS 960x640 3:2 non-touch display at 400 PPI with 400-nit brightness and 100% sRGB coverage, housed in a CNC aluminium alloy frame. Available in 6GB or 8GB LPDDR4X with 128GB or 256GB UFS 2.2 storage. Hall-effect sticks and triggers, gyroscope, X-axis linear motor for haptics, and fingerprint recognition via the power key. Active air-cooled heat dissipation keeps temperatures in check. The 2600 mAh battery provides approximately 6 hours of use. No video output is available. Currently out of stock at DROIX.
