# Beelink EQi12

[[beelink|Beelink]] EQi12 is a compact mini PC powered by the Intel Core i7-12650H (10c/16t, up to 4.7 GHz, 45-115W TDP). It features 24 GB LPDDR5 RAM, a 500 GB PCIe 4.0 NVMe SSD with a second empty M.2 slot for expansion, dual 1 Gbps Ethernet, and dual HDMI 4K@60Hz output. Runs Windows 11 Pro. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
