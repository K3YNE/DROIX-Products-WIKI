# R36S

Budget retro gaming handheld released late 2023. Powered by Rockchip RK3326 quad-core ARM Cortex-A35 @1.5 GHz with Mali-G31 MP2 GPU. 3.5-inch IPS display at 640x480 (4:3). 1 GB LPDDR3 RAM. Vertical form factor. Compatible with ArkOS and ROCKNIX custom firmware. Targets PS1 and earlier era emulation. 3500 mAh battery for up to 6 hours. Available in 8 transparent and solid colour options. Branded as "GameForce" on DROIX. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
