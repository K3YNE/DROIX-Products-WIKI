# GPD WIN 5 (Ryzen AI Max+ 395)

Flagship variant of the [[gpd-win-5]]. Uses the top-tier [[amd-ryzen-ai-max|AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395]] — 16 CPU cores with the full 40-CU Radeon 8060S GPU. This is the SKU most reviewers received and the one DROIX lists from $2,079.95.

## Real-world performance

- Runs Cyberpunk 2077 with ray tracing, DOOM: The Dark Ages at Nightmare, Warhammer at High.
- Emulation: [[emulation-performance|Vita3K at 2× render, Eden "smooth as silk", Xbox 360 "very well"]].
- Performance at this tier matches or exceeds prior-gen HX 370 paired with an [[ayaneo-ag01|AG01 eGPU]] setup — rendering eGPU redundant for many WIN 5 Max+ 395 buyers.

## Variant-specific notes

- 128 GB RAM configuration allows up to 96 GB VRAM allocation — unique to this variant among current handhelds.
- Mini SSD format tops out at ~1,700 MB/s read due to 4×1 PCIe on WIN 5 (vs 4×2 on [[onexplayer-onexfly-apex]] → ~3,551 MB/s).
