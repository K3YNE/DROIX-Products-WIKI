# GPD WIN 4

The GPD WIN 4 is [[gpd|GPD]]'s sliding-keyboard Windows handheld, refreshed annually from 2022 to 2025. Each yearly model upgrades the APU while retaining the 6" H-IPS touchscreen and signature slide-up keyboard design. Sold by [[droix|DROIX]] with a 2-year warranty.

## Yearly Models

| Year | CPU | GPU | RAM | Storage | Wi-Fi | Bluetooth |
|------|-----|-----|-----|---------|-------|-----------|
| 2022 (original) | AMD Ryzen 7 6800U | Radeon 680M (12 CUs @ 2200 MHz) | Up to 32 GB LPDDR5 @ 6400 MT/s | Up to 2 TB PCIe M.2 2280 | Wi-Fi 6 | 5.2 |
| 2023 | AMD Ryzen 5 7640U / Ryzen 7 7840U | Radeon 760M / 780M | Up to 64 GB LPDDR5X @ 6400 MT/s | Up to 4 TB PCIe Gen 4 NVMe | Wi-Fi 6 | 5.2 |
| 2024 | AMD Ryzen 7 8840U (28W TDP) | Radeon 780M (12 CUs @ 2700 MHz) | 32 GB LPDDR5X @ 6400 MT/s | Up to 4 TB PCIe 4.0 NVMe | Wi-Fi 6E | 5.3 |
| 2025 | AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 / Ryzen 7 8840U | Radeon 890M / 780M | Up to 32 GB LPDDR5X @ 7500 MT/s | Up to 4 TB PCIe 4.0 NVMe | Wi-Fi 6E | 5.3 |

## Design & Build

- 6" H-IPS touchscreen display.
- Signature sliding keyboard — display slides up to reveal a physical QWERTY keyboard.
- Compact handheld form factor with integrated gamepad controls.
- OCuLink support added from the 2023 model onward (used with [[gpd-g1]] eGPU).
