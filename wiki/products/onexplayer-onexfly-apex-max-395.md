# ONEXPLAYER ONEXFLY Apex (Ryzen AI Max+ 395)

Top-tier variant of the [[onexplayer-onexfly-apex]]. Shares the AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 APU with the [[gpd-win-5-max-395]] — the performance comparison between these two is the headline story of the 2026 Strix Halo handheld market.

## Specs

- APU: AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 (16 cores, 40 CUs).
- Mini SSD: up to ~3,551 MB/s (2× PCIe lanes — double the GPD WIN 5's throughput).
- Display, chassis, and connectivity: see [[onexplayer-onexfly-apex]].
