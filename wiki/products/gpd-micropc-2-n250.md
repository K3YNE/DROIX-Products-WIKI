# GPD MicroPC 2 (Intel N250)

Lower-cost variant of the [[gpd-micropc-2]]. 4-core Intel N250 — adequate for single-threaded productivity tasks and field-work tooling, but noticeably slower than the [[gpd-micropc-2-n300|N300 variant]] in multi-threaded workloads.

## Specs

- Intel N250, 4 cores / 4 threads, 6W TDP.
- 16 GB LPDDR5.
- Shares display, chassis, and connectivity with the N300 variant — see [[gpd-micropc-2]] for details.
