# AYANEO Pocket S2

[[ayaneo|AYANEO]]'s [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3-gen-3|Qualcomm Snapdragon G3 Gen 3]] Android handheld. Step up from the [[ayaneo-pocket-ace]] with a gaming-class Snapdragon SoC. The [[ayaneo-pocket-s2-product-page]] adds Android 14, a 2560 x 1440 IPS display, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.3, and S2 / S2 Pro battery distinctions.

## Design & Build

- [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3-gen-3|Qualcomm Snapdragon G3 Gen 3]] (same SoC family tier as [[konkr-pocket-fit]], at a higher AYANEO price point).
- TMR electromagnetic joysticks with RGB lighting and linear Hall-effect triggers.
- CNC arc aluminium alloy frame and quad-curved UV-coated back cover.
- AYANEO iSpace ecosystem.

## Display

- 6.3-inch IPS touchscreen, 2560 x 1440, 466 PPI, 600 nits, 16:9 per [[ayaneo-pocket-s2-product-page]].
- Contradiction: [[ayaneo-pocket-s2-product-page]] has one heading that says 6.7-inch, but its body and attribute table support 6.3-inch.

## Ports & I/O

- USB 3.2 Type-C with video output.
- Built-in microphone and dual speakers.
- Wi-Fi 7 and Bluetooth 5.3.

## Storage and Battery

- 8GB / 128GB UFS 3.1, 12GB / 256GB UFS 4.0, 16GB / 512GB UFS 4.0, and 16GB / 1TB UFS 4.0 configurations per [[ayaneo-pocket-s2-product-page]].
- MicroSD expansion.
- Battery row distinguishes S2 at 8000 mAh and S2 Pro at 10000 mAh, but the source does not map those names to each RAM/storage tier.

## Accessories

- [[ayaneo-pocket-s2-bag-product-page]] — protective bag / case with soft lining, scratch-resistance, and portable travel design.
