## Summary

The AYANEO SLIDE is a Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo]] featuring a sliding form factor — the device can reveal a physical keyboard when the display panel is slid up, combining the touchscreen handheld gaming experience with a physical QWERTY keyboard. It ships with Windows 11 pre-installed and supports 16 GB, 32 GB, and 64 GB RAM configurations, each requiring a different BIOS version. AYA SPACE 2 is the included companion software for TDP control and game launching. DROIX stocks the AYANEO SLIDE and provides a full getting-started guide and firmware resources.

## Design & Build

The SLIDE uses a slider mechanism: the display panel slides upward to reveal a keyboard, making it suitable for both gaming and productivity use cases. The package includes the device, 65 W power adapter, USB-C cable, two USB-C to USB-A adapters, and user manual. RAM configurations of 16 GB, 32 GB, and 64 GB are available; the correct BIOS version must be matched to the installed RAM.

## Ports & I/O

Standard AYANEO handheld I/O; specific port listing not confirmed in available sources. The USB-C port supports charging (65 W included adapter).
