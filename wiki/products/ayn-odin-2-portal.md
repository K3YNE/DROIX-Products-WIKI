# AYN Odin 2 Portal

[[ayn|AYN]]'s premium 7-inch OLED Android handheld powered by the [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-gen-2|Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2]]. The largest member of the Odin 2 family, featuring a 120 Hz OLED display with 3D curved touch glass and zero-bezel design. Available in Black, Indigo, and White.

Previously the Android performance reference device in DROIX reviews before being overtaken by the [[ayn-odin-3]].

## Design & Build

- 7" OLED, 1920x1080, 16:9, 120 Hz, 3D curved touch glass, zero bezels
- [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-gen-2|Snapdragon 8 Gen 2]], Adreno 740 GPU
- Android 13
- 8 / 12 / 16 GB LPDDR5X RAM
- 128 / 512 GB / 1 TB UFS 4.0 storage + MicroSD expansion
- Active cooling system
- 8000 mAh battery, 27W fast charging
- Colours: Black, Indigo, White

## Ports & I/O

- USB 3.1 Type-C
- 3.5 mm headphone jack
- Dual speakers
- Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.3

## SKU Configurations

- 8 GB LPDDR5X / 128 GB UFS 4.0
- 12 GB LPDDR5X / 512 GB UFS 4.0
- 16 GB LPDDR5X / 1 TB UFS 4.0
