# GPD MicroPC 2 (Intel N300)

Higher-tier variant of the [[gpd-micropc-2]]. 8-core Intel N300 — recommended for users with sustained multi-threaded workloads (compile jobs, parallel tooling, concurrent VMs).

## Specs

- Intel N300, 8 cores / 8 threads, 6W TDP.
- 16 GB LPDDR5.
- Shares display, chassis, and connectivity with the N250 variant — see [[gpd-micropc-2]] for details.
