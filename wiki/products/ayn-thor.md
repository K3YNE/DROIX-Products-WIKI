# AYN Thor

[[ayn|AYN]]'s dual-screen Android handheld (7 OLED variant reviewed). Direct competitor to the [[ayaneo-pocket-ds]] — DROIX's review concluded the Pocket DS was the better device overall.

## Design & Build

- Primary: 6" AMOLED, 1080x1920, 120 Hz, 367 PPI
- Secondary: 3.92" AMOLED, 1240x1080, 60 Hz, touchscreen
- Dual-screen clamshell form factor
- Precision Hall effect joysticks and triggers (zero drift)
- Active cooling system for sustained performance
- USB-C DisplayPort output up to 4K
- Custom AOSP launcher optimised for dual screens
- 6000 mAh Li-Po battery
- Wi-Fi 6/7, Bluetooth 5.1/5.3

## Models

- **Thor Lite:** Snapdragon 865, 8 GB LPDDR4x, 128 GB UFS 3.1, Wi-Fi 6, BT 5.1
- **Thor Base/Pro/Max:** [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-gen-2|Snapdragon 8 Gen 2]], 8/12/16 GB LPDDR5x, 128 GB/256 GB/1 TB UFS 3.1, Wi-Fi 7, BT 5.3
