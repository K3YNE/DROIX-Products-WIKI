## Summary

The AYANEO Founder is a Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo]] and one of the earliest devices in the AYANEO product lineup, predating the [[ayaneo-2021]] series. It is an early-era legacy device, built around an AMD Ryzen 5 4500U platform. The Founder edition was a limited production device aimed at early adopters and backers of AYANEO's initial crowdfunding campaign, establishing the brand's design philosophy before the mainstream [[ayaneo-2021]] release. DROIX supplies firmware resources for the AYANEO Founder.

## Design & Build

The AYANEO Founder established AYANEO's signature horizontal handheld form factor with analogue sticks and a large touchscreen display. As an early-era device it predates AYA SPACE software and some of the ergonomic refinements seen in later models.
