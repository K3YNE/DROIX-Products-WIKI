# AYANEO Pocket DMG

Vertical Game Boy-style Android handheld from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]] powered by the [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3x-gen-2|Qualcomm Snapdragon G3x Gen 2]] (8 cores, 3.36 GHz, 15W TDP) with Adreno A32 GPU at 1000 MHz. Features a compact 3.92-inch OLED 1240x1080 8:7 display at 419 PPI with 500-nit brightness and 100,000:1 contrast ratio. Available in configurations from 8GB/128GB UFS 3.1 up to 16GB/512GB UFS 4.0 LPDDR5X. Equipped with Hall-effect sticks and triggers, gyroscope, active cooling, Wi-Fi 7, and a 6000 mAh battery with 25W PD charging. Currently in stock at DROIX.
