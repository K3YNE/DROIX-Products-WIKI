# ONEXPLAYER Mini Pro

7" Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]] powered by AMD Ryzen 7 6800U with Radeon 680M integrated graphics. Features USB4 connectivity, 16/32GB LPDDR5, and a 48 Wh battery. Weighs 599g. Currently out of stock at DROIX.

## Design & Build

- 7" IPS 10-point touchscreen
- 26 x 10.6 x 2.3 cm (10.2 x 4.17 x 0.9 inches)
- 599g weight
- Clickable dual analogue sticks, D-pad, gaming buttons
- Shortcut buttons for quick task access and Gamer Centre
- Fingerprint sensor (Windows Hello)
- Windows 11 Home
- Available in Black and White

## Display

- 7" IPS, 1920x1200 (16:10), 323 PPI
- 10-point touchscreen

## Processor & Graphics

- AMD Ryzen 7 6800U: 8 cores / 16 threads, 2.7 GHz base, up to 4.7 GHz boost, 15-28W TDP
- AMD Radeon 680M: 12 cores, up to 2200 MHz, shared memory

## Memory & Storage

- 16GB or 32GB LPDDR5 6400 MT/s
- 1TB or 2TB M.2 2280 PCIe 3.0 x4 NVMe SSD

## Ports & I/O

- 2x USB 4.0 Type-C @ 40 GB/s (supports eGPU, video out at 8K@60Hz)
- 1x USB-C 3.2 Gen 1
- 1x 3.5mm headphone/microphone combo jack
- Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.0

## Battery

- 48 Wh (12,450 mAh) Li-Po
- Gaming: up to 2.5 hours
- Video playback: up to 10 hours

## Configurations

- 16GB LPDDR5 / 1TB PCIe 3.0 2280
- 16GB LPDDR5 / 2TB PCIe 3.0 2280
- 32GB LPDDR5 / 2TB PCIe 3.0 2280

## Availability

Out of stock at DROIX. Available new and refurbished (Class A/B).
