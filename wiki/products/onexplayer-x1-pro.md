# ONEXPLAYER X1 Pro

10.95" 3-in-1 Windows tablet/handheld from [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]] powered by [[amd-ryzen-ai-9-hx-370|AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370]] with Radeon 890M (RDNA 3.5). An upgraded version of the [[onexplayer-x1-amd]] with a Strix Point processor, delivering superior gaming and AI performance. Features the same 2.5K 120Hz LTPS display, detachable controllers, Hall effect joysticks, native OCuLink, and Harman speakers. Out of stock at DROIX.

## Design & Build

- 10.95" LTPS 2.5K display
- 3-in-1 device: tablet, console, laptop (with detachable controllers and keyboard)
- Ergonomic detachable gaming controller
- Hall effect joysticks with tactile triggers
- Fingerprint sensor (power button) + AI front camera (Windows Hello)
- Active cooling: liquid bearing fan, pure aluminium heat sink fins, dual copper heat pipes
- Harman AudioEFX dual speakers
- Windows 11 Home

## Display

- 10.95" IPS/LTPS, 2560x1600 (16:10), 275 PPI
- 120Hz refresh rate
- 540 nits brightness
- Touchscreen

## Processor & Graphics

- AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 (Zen 5 + Zen 5c, Strix Point): 12 cores / 24 threads, up to 5.10 GHz, 15-54W TDP
- AMD Radeon 890M (RDNA 3.5): 16 cores, up to 2900 MHz, shared memory

## Memory & Storage

- 32GB or 64GB LPDDR5X 7500 MT/s
- 1TB / 2TB / 4TB M.2 2280 PCIe 4.0 NVMe SSD
- 1x MicroSD card slot (up to 2TB)

## Ports & I/O

- 2x USB 4.0 Type-C
- 1x USB-A 3.2
- 1x OCuLink (native)
- 1x MicroSD 4.0 card slot
- 1x 3.5mm headphone jack
- Dual speakers by Harman
- Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2

## Battery

- 65.02 Wh Li-Po (~8 hours battery life)
- 100W GaN charger

## Configurations

- 32GB LPDDR5X / 1TB NVMe PCIe 4.0 2280
- 32GB LPDDR5X / 2TB NVMe PCIe 4.0 2280
- 64GB LPDDR5X / 2TB NVMe PCIe 4.0 2280
- 64GB LPDDR5X / 4TB NVMe PCIe 4.0 2280

## Availability

Out of stock at DROIX. Available new and refurbished (Class A).
