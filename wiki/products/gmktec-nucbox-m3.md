# GMKTec NucBox M3

[[gmktec|GMKTec]] NucBox M3 is a mid-range mini PC powered by the Intel Core i5-12450H (8c/12t, up to 4.4 GHz, 35-95W TDP) with Intel UHD Graphics. Ships with 16 or 32 GB DDR4 and 512 GB or 1 TB NVMe PCIe 3.0 SSD. Features triple display via 2x HDMI and Thunderbolt 4, 1 Gbps Ethernet, and Wi-Fi 6. Runs Windows 11 Pro. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
