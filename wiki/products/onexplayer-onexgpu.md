# ONEXPLAYER ONEXGPU

Portable eGPU docking station from [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]] featuring the [[amd-rx-7600m-xt|AMD Radeon RX 7600M XT]] GPU with 8GB GDDR6. Billed as the "world's first portable eGPU with storage" thanks to its built-in M.2 2280 NVMe slot. Connects via OCuLink or USB 4.0 and supports up to 4 displays simultaneously. Weighs 789g. Currently out of stock at DROIX.

## Design & Build

- 19.6 x 12.0 x 3.2 cm, 789g
- Compact slim-profile design
- 300W GaN charger with up to 100W USB-C PD charging

## GPU

- AMD Radeon RX 7600M XT (RDNA 3)
- 32 compute units, up to 2300 MHz
- 8GB GDDR6 dedicated memory

## Storage

- 1x M.2 2280 PCIe 3.0 NVMe slot (optional: 1TB/2TB/4TB)

## Ports & I/O

- 1x OCuLink
- 1x USB 4.0 Type-C @ 40 GB/s
- 1x USB 4.0 Type-C DP (8K@60Hz)
- 2x USB-A 3.2 Gen 2
- 2x HDMI (4K@60Hz)
- Supports up to 4 displays simultaneously (2x HDMI + 2x DP via USB-C)

## Power

- 300W GaN charger included
- Up to 100W USB-C PD device charging

## Availability

Out of stock at DROIX. Available new and refurbished (Class A).
