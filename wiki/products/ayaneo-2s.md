# AYANEO 2S

Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]] powered by the AMD Ryzen 7 7840U with Radeon 780M integrated graphics. Features a 7-inch IPS 1920x1200 16:10 touchscreen at 323 PPI with 500-nit brightness and 130% sRGB coverage. Available in 32GB and 64GB LPDDR5X configurations with up to 4TB PCIe 4.0 storage. Dual USB4 Type-C ports support eGPU connectivity. Hall-effect sticks and triggers, dual gyroscope, fingerprint sensor, and HD linear motor round out the input system. Currently in stock at DROIX.
