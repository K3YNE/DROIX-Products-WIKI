## Summary

The AYANEO NEXT Lite is a Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo]], positioned as a budget-friendly entry in the AYANEO NEXT family. It is based on an AMD Ryzen 5 4500U platform (inferred from the driver package and BIOS era). The NEXT Lite also officially supports HoloISO (a SteamOS-like Linux distribution), with AYANEO providing a dedicated HoloISO image and tutorial — making it one of the few AYANEO devices with explicit Linux support resources. DROIX supplies firmware and driver resources for the NEXT Lite.
