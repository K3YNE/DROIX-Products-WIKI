# Anbernic RG405V

Android-based retro gaming handheld from [[anbernic|Anbernic]] in a vertical Game Boy-style form factor. Unisoc Tiger T618 octa-core (2x Cortex-A75 + 6x Cortex-A55) @ 2.0 GHz, Mali-G52 MP2 @ 850 MHz. 4" IPS 640x480 4:3 touchscreen at 200 PPI. 4 GB LPDDR4X @ 1866 MT/s. 128 GB eMMC + MicroSD expansion. Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 5.0. 5500 mAh battery (~9 hours, ~3 hours charge). Runs Android 12. Available in Grey, Wood, and Transparent Purple.
