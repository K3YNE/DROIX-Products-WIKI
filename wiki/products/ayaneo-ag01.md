# AYANEO AG01

External GPU (eGPU) dock from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]]. Pairs with handhelds over Oculink / USB4 to deliver desktop-class GPU performance. See [[egpu-docking]] for category context.

## Summary

External GPU dock from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]] featuring [[amd-rx-7600m-xt|AMD Radeon RX 7600M XT]] (32 CU, 8 GB GDDR6, 2300 MHz). Connects via OCuLink SFF-8612 or USB4. Compact spacecraft-inspired design: 18 cm x 6.3 cm x 3 cm, 869 g, aerospace-grade aluminium alloy. Turbine-style cooling with dual air inlets.

## Design & Build

- Aerospace-grade aluminium alloy chassis
- Dimensions: 18 cm x 6.3 cm x 3 cm, 869 g
- Turbine-style cooling system with dual air inlets and high-efficiency blower fan
- Futuristic spacecraft-inspired industrial design

## Ports & I/O

- 1x OCuLink SFF-8612
- 1x USB 4.0 Type-C @ 40 GB/s (DP 8K@60Hz)
- 1x USB 3.2 Type-C
- 1x USB Type-A 3.2 Gen 2
- 2x DisplayPort (4K@60Hz)
- 2x HDMI (4K@60Hz)
- 1x RJ45 Ethernet @ 1000 Mbps
- 1x M.2 SSD 22x80 Slot
- 1x SD Card Slot
