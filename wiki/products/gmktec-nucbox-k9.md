# GMKTec NucBox K9

[[gmktec|GMKTec]] NucBox K9 is a performance mini PC powered by the Intel Core Ultra 5 125H (14c/18t, up to 4.5 GHz, 28W TDP) with Intel Arc Graphics at 2.2 GHz. Ships with 32 GB DDR5 @5600 MT/s and 1 TB NVMe PCIe 4.0 SSD, expandable up to 96 GB RAM and 4 TB storage. Features triple 4K display output, dual 1 Gbps Ethernet, Thunderbolt 4, and Wi-Fi 6. Runs Windows 11 Pro. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
