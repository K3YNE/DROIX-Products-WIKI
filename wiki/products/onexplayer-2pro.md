## Summary

The ONEXPLAYER 2 Pro is a premium Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer]], available in Ryzen 7 7840U and Ryzen 7 8840U configurations. It builds on the ONEXPLAYER 2 with AMD's Phoenix-generation APUs featuring RDNA 3 integrated graphics, offering improved GPU performance and efficiency over the 6800U generation. The two processor options give buyers a choice between the 7840U (2023 release) and 8840U (refreshed) variants.
