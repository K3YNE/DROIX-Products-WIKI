## Summary

The ONEXPLAYER 1 (Intel) is the original full-size Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer]], released in mid-2021. Powered by the Intel Core i7-1195G7, it features a large 8.4-inch display and a full gamepad controller layout — thumbsticks, triggers, bumpers, and face buttons — making it one of the earliest large-screen Windows handheld gaming PCs. It was followed by an AMD variant ([[onexplayer-1-amd]]) and the more compact Mini lineup.
