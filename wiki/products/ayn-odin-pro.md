# AYN Odin Pro

[[ayn|AYN]]'s original Android handheld, a legacy device powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon SD845. Out of stock. The Odin Pro was succeeded by the [[ayn-odin-2]] family and the [[ayn-odin-3]].

## Design & Build

- 5.98" IPS LCD, 1920x1080, 16:9, 369 PPI, touchscreen, Dragontrail glass
- Qualcomm Snapdragon SD845 (8 cores / 8 threads, Kryo Gold 2.8 GHz + Silver 1.8 GHz), Adreno 630 @ 787 MHz
- Android 10
- 8 GB LPDDR4X RAM
- 128 / 256 GB storage
- 6600 mAh battery, QC 3.0 / 4.0+ fast charging
- 365 g, 8.8 x 3.7 x 0.5 in
- Colours: Black, Cold Grey, Clear White

## Ports & I/O

- USB-C 3.1
- Micro HDMI video output
- 3.5 mm headphone jack
- 2.4 / 5 GHz Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.0
