## Summary

The ONEXPLAYER 1 AMD is a large-screen Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer]], released in late 2021 as an AMD variant of the original ONEXPLAYER 1 lineup. It features an 8.4-inch display and an AMD Ryzen processor, positioning it as the AMD alternative to the Intel i7-1195G7 model. The AMD edition was reviewed by DROIX alongside a direct performance comparison against the Intel variant, demonstrating competitive frame rates across AAA Windows titles.

## Design & Build

The ONEXPLAYER 1 AMD shares the same large, landscape-orientation form factor as the Intel model — a chunky, gamepad-style device built around an 8.4-inch display. It is designed for those who want a full Windows PC in their hands with a dedicated controller layout including thumbsticks, bumpers, triggers, and face buttons.
