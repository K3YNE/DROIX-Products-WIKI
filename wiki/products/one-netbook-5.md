## Summary

The One-Netbook 5 is a Windows UMPC (ultra-mobile PC) from the One-Netbook sub-brand under [[onexplayer]], reviewed by DROIX in May 2024. It features an Intel Core i7 processor and a distinctive interchangeable design functioning as both a mini laptop and a tablet. DROIX described it as an "Interchangeable Mini Laptop & Tablet," positioning it as a versatile portable computing device for productivity and gaming on the go.

## Design & Build

The One-Netbook 5 features a unique convertible/interchangeable form factor that can operate as both a mini laptop and a tablet. This dual-mode capability distinguishes it from traditional Windows handhelds in the ONEXPLAYER lineup.
