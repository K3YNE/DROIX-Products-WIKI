# ONEXPLAYER Mini

Legacy 7" Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]] (One-Netbook). Available with a choice of AMD Ryzen 7 5800U or Intel Core i7-1195G7. A compact and lightweight device at 589g, designed for portable AAA and retro gaming. Still in stock at DROIX.

## Design & Build

- 7" IPS touchscreen, 1920x1200, 16:10 aspect ratio, 323 PPI
- 589g weight
- Body angled at 145 degrees at corners for improved grip
- Redesigned back panel for improved thumb comfort on shoulder buttons
- Original Japanese ALPS joysticks with 18-degree tilt angle and ABXY buttons
- Analogue triggers, dual vibration motors
- Windows 11 Home

## Display

- 7" IPS, 1920x1200 (16:10), 323 PPI
- 10-point touchscreen

## Processors

Two processor options:

| | Intel | AMD |
|---|---|---|
| CPU | Core i7-1195G7 | Ryzen 7 5800U |
| Cores/Threads | 4C/8T | 8C/16T |
| Base Clock | 1.30 GHz | 1.90 GHz |
| Boost Clock | 5.00 GHz | 4.40 GHz |
| TDP | 12-28W | 10-25W |
| GPU | Iris Xe Graphics (96 EU) | Radeon Vega 8 (8 cores) |
| GPU Max Freq | 1400 MHz | 2000 MHz |

## Memory & Storage

- 16GB LPDDR4X 4266 MT/s (dual-channel, soldered)
- 1TB or 2TB PCIe Gen 3.0 NVMe (M.2 2280 slot, used)
- Read speeds up to 2242 MB/s

## Ports & I/O

- **Intel model**: 1x Thunderbolt 4 USB-C + 1x USB-C 3.1 Gen 1
- **AMD model**: 2x USB-C 3.1 Gen 1
- Video out via Thunderbolt 4 (Intel only)
- 1x 3.5mm headphone/microphone combo jack
- Built-in microphone, dual speakers
- Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.0

## Battery

- 40.25 Wh Li-Po
- Gaming: up to 2.5 hours
- Video playback: up to 9 hours

## Availability

In stock at DROIX. Available new and refurbished (Class A/B/C).
