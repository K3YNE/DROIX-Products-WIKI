# AYANEO Pocket ACE

Android pocket-form-factor handheld from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]]. Positioned for Android gaming and emulation at a mid-range price. Both [[2025-05-23-ayaneo-pocket-ace-review-youtube]] and [[ayaneo-pocket-ace-product-page]] identify its SoC as [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3x-gen-2|Snapdragon G3x Gen 2]].

## Design & Build

- Hall-effect sticks and triggers (AYANEO house standard).
- iSpace software ecosystem — manual library organisation (no auto-scraping).
- Compact horizontal form factor with Shadow Dance Black, Light Blade White, and Retro Power colour options per [[ayaneo-pocket-ace-product-page]].

## Display

- 4.5-inch IPS touchscreen.
- 1620 x 1080 resolution, 400 nits, and 433 PPI per [[ayaneo-pocket-ace-product-page]].
- Contradiction: [[ayaneo-pocket-ace-product-page]] body says the display is 3:2, while its attribute table says 16:9. The 1620 x 1080 resolution is 3:2, so the 16:9 row is likely incorrect.

## Ports & I/O

- USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-C with 10Gbps data transfer and DisplayPort 1.4 video output.
- Wi-Fi 7 and Bluetooth 5.3.
- Built-in microphone and dual speakers.

## Storage and Battery

- RAM / storage configurations from [[ayaneo-pocket-ace-product-page]]: 8GB / 128GB UFS 3.1, 12GB / 256GB UFS 4.0, 16GB / 512GB UFS 4.0, and 16GB / 1TB UFS 4.0.
- MicroSD expansion.
- 6000 mAh battery with 40W PD fast charging.

## Accessories

- [[ayaneo-pocket-ace-case-product-page]] — protective case with soft lining, lightweight construction, and scratch-resistance claims.
- [[ayaneo-pocket-ace-grips-product-page]] — ergonomic attachable grips for improved comfort and control.

## Contradictions and Corrections

- Older wiki navigation/entity entries previously attached the [[ayaneo-pocket-ace|Pocket ACE]] to [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-gen-2|Snapdragon 8 Gen 2]]. [[2025-05-23-ayaneo-pocket-ace-review-youtube]] and [[ayaneo-pocket-ace-product-page]] both state [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3x-gen-2|Snapdragon G3x Gen 2]], so this hub now treats G3x Gen 2 as the supported chipset.
