# Retroid Pocket 3+

The [[retroid|Retroid]] Pocket 3+ is a budget Android retro gaming handheld powered by the Unisoc Tiger T618 octa-core processor with a 4.7-inch touchscreen display. It runs Android 11 with Google Play Store access and is designed for retro console emulation up to fifth-generation systems (PS1 era). Available in multiple colour options. Currently out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
