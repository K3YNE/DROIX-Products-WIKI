# AOKZOE A1 Pro

The [[aokzoe|AOKZOE]] A1 Pro is a Windows handheld gaming PC powered by the AMD Ryzen 7 7840U (8c/16t, up to 5.1 GHz, Zen 4) with AMD Radeon 780M (RDNA 3, 12 CU, 2700 MHz) integrated graphics. Features an 8-inch IPS display (1920x1200, 100% sRGB, 350 nits), up to 64 GB LPDDR5X RAM, and up to 2 TB PCIe 4.0 NVMe storage. Includes USB 4.0, Wi-Fi 6E, and a 65 Wh battery. Ships with OneXConsole software for TDP management and game library organisation. In stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
