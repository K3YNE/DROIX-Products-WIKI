# X9 Pro Game Stick

Upgraded retro gaming console stick for TV. Same Amlogic S905X3 platform as the [[x9-game-stick|X9]] with 2 GB DDR3 RAM and Mali-G31 MP2 GPU @750 MHz. Available in 64/128/256 GB storage options with 30,000-50,000 pre-loaded games. 4K@60fps output via HDMI 2.1. Runs EmuELEC 4.3. Handles PS1 and PSP well; N64 and Dreamcast may see occasional performance drops. Includes two 2.4 GHz wireless controllers. Branded as "GameForce" on DROIX. Refurbished only. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
