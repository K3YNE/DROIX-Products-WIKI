## Summary

The AYANEO KUN is a Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo]], notable as one of AYANEO's larger-format handhelds with an optional 4G LTE module for mobile connectivity. It ships with AMD Ryzen 7 8840U (confirmed by an 8840U-specific driver package) alongside an earlier AMD 7840U configuration. The KUN is AYANEO's premium large-format handheld, with a higher-capacity battery and a touchpad integrated into the design. The device runs Windows 11 with AYA SPACE 3.0 companion software. DROIX stocks the AYANEO KUN.

## Design & Build

The KUN is one of AYANEO's larger handhelds, featuring a built-in touchpad — a distinguishing feature from the rest of the AYANEO handheld lineup. An optional 4G LTE module is supported, enabling cellular connectivity. The AYA SPACE 3.0 software is updated via EC firmware.
