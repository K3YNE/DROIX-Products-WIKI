# RockTek GX1

The [[rocktek|RockTek]] GX1 is a UHD Google TV set-top box powered by the Realtek RTD1325TPV (quad-core 64-bit Cortex-A55, 20K DMIPS) with Mali-G57 GPU. Runs Google TV 12 (with Google TV 14 update planned). Supports 4K@60fps with Dolby Vision, HDR10, HDR10+, Dolby Atmos, and Dolby Audio. Features 4 GB DDR4 RAM, 32 GB eMMC storage, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, and HDMI 2.1 with eARC. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
