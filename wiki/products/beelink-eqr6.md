# Beelink EQR6

[[beelink|Beelink]] EQR6 is a performance mini PC powered by the AMD Ryzen 7 6800H (8c/16t, up to 4.7 GHz, 45W TDP) with Radeon 680M integrated graphics. Ships with 32 GB LPDDR5 RAM and 512 GB PCIe 4.0 NVMe SSD, expandable with a second M.2 slot. Features dual 2.5 Gbps Ethernet, triple display support, and Wi-Fi 6. Runs Windows 11 Pro. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
