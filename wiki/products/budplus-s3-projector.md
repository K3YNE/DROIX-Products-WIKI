# BudPlus S3 Projector

Portable home cinema projector sold by [[droix|DROIX]]. Features a 3.5" LCD optical engine with native 1080p resolution (1920x1080), 370 ANSI / 7500 lumens brightness, and 5000:1 contrast ratio. Powered by ARM Cortex-A53 quad-core CPU with Mali-G31 GPU, 2 GB RAM, 32 GB storage, running Android 9. Wi-Fi 6 (dual-band 2.4G+5G), Bluetooth 5.0. Projects 40-200 inch image. Built-in dual 5W JBL-level speakers with DSP. LED light source rated for 50,000 hours. 1.6 kg, compact with carrying handle. In stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
