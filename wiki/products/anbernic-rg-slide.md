# Anbernic RG Slide

[[anbernic|Anbernic]]'s budget Android handheld with a sliding-screen mechanism. Positioned as a budget alternative to devices like the AYANEO Pocket Micro. The [[anbernic-rg-slide-product-page]] confirms Android 13, [[unisoc-t820|Unisoc T820]], 8GB LPDDR4X, 128GB UFS 2.2 storage, and a 5000 mAh battery.

## Design & Build

- Sliding-screen mechanism that reveals the gaming controls.
- 15.4 x 9 x 2.6 cm chassis; 0.379 kg weight per [[anbernic-rg-slide-product-page]].
- [[unisoc-t820|Unisoc T820]] SoC (same family noted for the Anbernic RG Cube).
- Mid-range performance — suited to retro gaming and lighter emulation.

## Display

- 4.7-inch LTPS In-Cell touchscreen with OCA full lamination.
- 120Hz refresh rate, 4:3 aspect ratio, and 340 PPI per [[anbernic-rg-slide-product-page]].
- Contradiction: [[anbernic-rg-slide-product-page]] body lists 1280 x 960, while its attribute table lists 1290 x 960. Treat 1280 x 960 as the likely intended 4:3 resolution until superseded.

## Ports & I/O

- USB Type-C with video output up to 1080p via DisplayPort according to [[anbernic-rg-slide-product-page]].
- The same source warns the USB-C port supports either video output or charging, but not both at the same time.
- 3.5mm headphone/microphone combo jack and dual speakers.
- Wi-Fi 5 and Bluetooth 5.0.

## Storage and Battery

- 8GB LPDDR4X RAM.
- 128GB UFS 2.2 storage, expandable by MicroSD / TF card up to 2TB.
- 5000 mAh battery with up to 6 hours claimed gameplay and approximately 3-hour full charge via 5V/2A charging.

## Software

- RG Launcher for retro library organisation.
- Built-in AI Translation utility.
- [[anbernic-rg-slide-product-page]] also lists FOTA wireless upgrades and AI functions including guide retrieval, real-time translation, dialogue, text-to-image generation, and image processing.
