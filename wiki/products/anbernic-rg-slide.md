# Anbernic RG Slide

[[anbernic|Anbernic]]'s budget Android handheld with a sliding-screen mechanism. Positioned as a budget alternative to devices like the AYANEO Pocket Micro.

## Design & Build

- Sliding-screen mechanism (display slides up to reveal controls or settings).
- Unisoc T820 SoC (same as the Anbernic RG Cube).
- Mid-range performance — suited to retro gaming and lighter emulation.

## Software

- RG Launcher for retro library organisation.
- Built-in AI Translation utility.
