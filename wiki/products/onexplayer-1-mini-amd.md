## Summary

The ONEXPLAYER Mini AMD is a compact Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer]], released in mid-2022, powered by the AMD Ryzen 7 5800U. It is the AMD counterpart to the Intel i7-1195G7 Mini, bringing AMD's Zen 3 CPU architecture and Radeon integrated graphics to the smaller ONEXPLAYER form factor. DROIX reviewed and compared it against the Intel Mini in May 2022.

## Design & Build

The ONEXPLAYER Mini AMD shares the compact form factor of the original Mini, featuring a smaller display and body than the full-size ONEXPLAYER 1, while retaining the full gamepad control layout with thumbsticks, triggers, and face buttons.
