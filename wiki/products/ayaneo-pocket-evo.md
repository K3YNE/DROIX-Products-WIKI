# AYANEO Pocket EVO

Android handheld from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]] powered by the [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3x-gen-2|Qualcomm Snapdragon G3x Gen 2]] with Adreno A32 GPU at 1000 MHz. Features a 7-inch OLED 1920x1080 16:9 display at 315 PPI with 800-nit brightness and 120/165/60 Hz refresh rate options. Available in configurations from 6GB/128GB UFS 3.1 up to 12GB/1TB UFS 4.0 LPDDR5X. Equipped with RGB Hall joysticks (250 Hz polling), linear Hall triggers (250 Hz), HD linear motor, gyroscope, fingerprint sensor, and an 8600 mAh battery with PD charging. Currently in stock at DROIX.
