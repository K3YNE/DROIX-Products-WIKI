# Anbernic RG353PS

Linux-only variant of the [[anbernic-rg353p|RG353P]] from [[anbernic|Anbernic]]. Same RK3566 quad-core Cortex-A55 @ 1.8 GHz, Mali-G52 2EE GPU. 3.5" IPS touchscreen, 640x480. 1 GB LPDDR4 RAM (vs 2 GB on RG353P). Linux only (no Android dual boot). Wi-Fi 5, BT 4.2, Mini HDMI, vibration feedback.
