# Anbernic RG28XX

Ultra-compact budget retro gaming handheld from [[anbernic|Anbernic]]. H700 ARM Cortex-A53 quad-core @ 1.5 GHz, Mali-G31 MP2 GPU. 2.83" IPS display, 640x480, 4:3. Runs Linux. Micro HDMI output. One of Anbernic's smallest handhelds at 125 g.
