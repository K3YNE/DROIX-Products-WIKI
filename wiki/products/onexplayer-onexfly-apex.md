# ONEXPLAYER ONEXFLY Apex

Strix Halo flagship Windows handheld from [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]]. Direct competitor to the [[gpd-win-5]] — same APU, larger 8" 1200p display, heavier body, faster Mini SSD (dual PCIe lanes). Optional liquid-cooled "Frostbay" variant exists.

## Design & Build

- 8" H-IPS display with Corning Gorilla Glass 5, 1920x1200, 120 Hz VRR, 500 nits, 100% sRGB
- Native landscape orientation
- Device weight: 706 g. External battery: 387 g. Combined ~1,093 g.
- Liquid-cooling option available via Frostbay accessory.
- 9mm capacitive joysticks (zero-drift), adjustable triggers (2mm short / 8mm long throw)
- Dual linear rumble motors
- Fingerprint sensor (Windows Hello)
- Modular 85 Wh swappable battery via blade connector, with separate battery dock (LCD status display)

## Ports & I/O

- 1x USB 4.0 Type-C @ 40 GB/s (eGPU support)
- 1x USB-A 3.2 Gen 2
- 1x USB-C 3.2 Gen 2
- 3.5mm headphone/microphone combo jack
- Dual speakers
- Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2
- Mini SSD slot with 2× PCIe lanes (~3,551 MB/s — faster than the GPD WIN 5's 4×1 ~1,785 MB/s)
- 1x Micro SD Card Slot
