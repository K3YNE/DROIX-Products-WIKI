# Beelink EQR5

[[beelink|Beelink]] EQR5 is a compact mini PC powered by the AMD Ryzen 5 Pro 5650U (6c/12t, up to 4.2 GHz, 15W TDP). Ships with 16 GB DDR4 and 512 GB PCIe 3.0 NVMe SSD, expandable up to 64 GB RAM. Features dual HDMI 4K@60Hz output, dual 2.5 Gbps Ethernet, and triple display support. Runs Windows 11 Pro. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
