# GPD WIN 5

The GPD WIN 5 is [[gpd|GPD]]'s 2025 flagship Windows handheld and the first device on the market to ship with AMD's [[amd-ryzen-ai-max|Ryzen AI Max (Strix Halo)]] APU. Pre-mass-production units arrived with reviewers in October 2025; retail ships with an updated 85W TDP ceiling. The WIN 5 is sold by [[droix|DROIX]] with a 2-year warranty and is one of two Strix Halo handhelds available, alongside the [[onexplayer-onexfly-apex|ONEXPLAYER ONEXFLY Apex]].

## Design & Build

- 7" form factor handheld, LG-DOW aviation-grade ABS plastic construction.
- **No physical keyboard** — a break from GPD WIN tradition. First WIN-series handheld without one.
- Device weight: 565g. External battery: 350g (modular, separately carried).
- Dual-fan active cooling.
- Windows Hello fingerprint sensor supported (variant-dependent).

## Display

- 7" 1920×1080 IPS touchscreen.
- 120 Hz refresh with AMD FreeSync.
- External 4K 144 Hz output available via the [[gpd-win-5-smart-dock|Smart Dock]].

## Ports & I/O

- USB4 / USB-C (charging + display).
- HDMI output.
- Mini SSD slot (new BIWIN format, user-upgradeable to 4 TB).
- Full M.2 SSD slot (user-upgradeable to 4 TB).
- RAM is soldered (not upgradeable after purchase — choose capacity at order).

## Variants

- [[gpd-win-5-max-395]] — AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 (16 cores / 40 CUs). Flagship SKU. RAM options: 32 / 64 / 128 GB.
- [[gpd-win-5-max-385]] — AMD Ryzen AI Max 385 (8 cores / 32 CUs). Lower-cost SKU.

Colour/storage SKUs do not get their own pages; choose variant by APU.
