# RockTek G2

The [[rocktek|RockTek]] G2 is an Android TV box powered by the Amlogic S905X4-K (quad-core Cortex-A55 @2.0 GHz) with Mali-G31 MP2 GPU. Runs Google Certified Android TV 14 OS with full Google Play Store access. Features 4K@60fps output via HDMI 2.1 with dynamic HDR, Wi-Fi 6, and Bluetooth 5.0. Available with 32 or 64 GB eMMC storage and 4 GB LPDDR4 RAM. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].

## Specs

| Spec | Value |
