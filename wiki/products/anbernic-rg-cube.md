# Anbernic RG Cube

Android-based retro gaming handheld from [[anbernic|Anbernic]] with a unique 1:1 square display. [[unisoc-t820|Unisoc T820]] octa-core processor, Mali-G57 GPU. 3.95" IPS, 720x720, touchscreen. 8 GB LPDDR4X RAM, 128 GB UFS 2.2 storage. Runs Android 13. Active cooling with high-speed fan. Hall effect joysticks with 6-axis gyroscope and RGB lighting. Moonlight streaming support.
