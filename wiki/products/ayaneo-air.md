## Summary

The AYANEO AIR is a Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo]], released in 2022 as a thinner and lighter sibling to the AYANEO 2. The AIR prioritised portability and a slimmer profile compared to other AYANEO handhelds of the era, making it the brand's most travel-friendly Windows device at the time. It runs Windows 11 with the AYA SPACE companion software for TDP and frontend management. DROIX reviewed and stocked the AYANEO AIR. The AIR line was subsequently followed by the [[ayaneo-air-plus]] and [[ayaneo-air-1s]].

## Design & Build

The AYANEO AIR was designed around a slim form factor, distinguishing it from the larger AYANEO 2. It retains AYANEO's characteristic ergonomic handheld layout with Hall Effect analogue sticks and RGB. The design emphasised reduced weight and thickness for portability.
