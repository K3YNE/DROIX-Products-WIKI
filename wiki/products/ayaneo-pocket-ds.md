# AYANEO Pocket DS

Dual-screen Android handheld from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]]. Directly competes with the [[ayn-thor|AYN Thor]]; the DROIX review preferred the Pocket DS over the Thor 7 OLED.

## Design & Build

- Dual-screen "DS" layout (primary + secondary). See [[dual-screen-handhelds]].
- Hall-effect controls.
- iSpace software ecosystem.
