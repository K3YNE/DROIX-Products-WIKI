# AYANEO Pocket DS

Dual-screen Android handheld from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]]. Directly competes with the [[ayn-thor|AYN Thor]]; the DROIX review preferred the Pocket DS over the Thor 7 OLED. The [[ayaneo-pocket-ds-product-page]] identifies the SoC as [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3x-gen-2|Snapdragon G3x Gen 2]] and confirms Android 13.

## Design & Build

- Dual-screen "DS" layout (primary + secondary). See [[dual-screen-handhelds]].
- TMR joysticks and linear Hall triggers.
- iSpace software ecosystem.

## Display

- Main display: 7-inch 1920 x 1080 OLED, 16:9, 314 PPI, 800 nits per [[ayaneo-pocket-ds-product-page]].
- Secondary display: 5-inch 1024 x 768 LCD, 4:3, 256 PPI, 550 nits per [[ayaneo-pocket-ds-product-page]].
- Refresh rates listed by source: 60Hz, 90Hz, 120Hz, 144Hz, and 165Hz, without mapping each rate to a mode.
- Contradiction: one paragraph in [[ayaneo-pocket-ds-product-page]] describes dual IPS displays, but its display table specifies OLED + LCD.

## Ports & I/O

- USB 3.2 Type-C with video output.
- Built-in microphone and dual speakers.
- Wi-Fi 7 and Bluetooth 5.3.

## Storage and Battery

- 8GB / 128GB UFS 3.1, 12GB / 256GB UFS 4.0, 16GB / 512GB UFS 4.0, and 16GB / 1TB UFS 4.0 configurations per [[ayaneo-pocket-ds-product-page]].
- MicroSD expansion.
- 8000 mAh battery.

## Accessories

- [[ayaneo-pocket-ds-bag-product-page]] — protective bag / case with soft lining, scratch-resistance, and portable travel design.
