# Retroid Pocket Flip

The [[retroid|Retroid]] Pocket Flip is a clamshell-design Android retro gaming handheld powered by the Unisoc Tiger T618. The foldable form factor provides screen protection during travel. It features dual hall-effect analogue sliders, double-injected ABXY buttons, and a 5000 mAh battery. Same T618 SoC as the [[retroid-pocket-3-plus]] but in a folding shell. Currently out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
