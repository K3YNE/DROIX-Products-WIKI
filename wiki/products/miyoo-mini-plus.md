# Miyoo Mini Plus

Compact retro gaming handheld from [[miyoo|Miyoo]]. Powered by SigmaStar SSD202D dual-core ARM Cortex-A7 @1.2 GHz. 3.5-inch IPS display at 640x480 (4:3, 228 PPI). 128 MB LPDDR3 RAM. Ships with 64 GB MicroSD and MiniUI OS. Wi-Fi 5 for RetroArch online play and file transfer. 3000 mAh battery for up to 7-8 hours. 165 g. Supports custom firmware including Onion OS. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
