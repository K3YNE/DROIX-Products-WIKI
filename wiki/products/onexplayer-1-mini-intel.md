## Summary

The ONEXPLAYER Mini Intel (i7-1260P) is a special-edition compact Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer]], featuring the Intel Core i7-1260P processor and released as a Gundam Limited Edition. It represents a generational step up from the i7-1195G7 Mini variant, featuring Intel's 12th-generation Alder Lake-U architecture with a hybrid P-core/E-core design. The Gundam branding makes it a collectible edition within the ONEXPLAYER Mini lineup.
