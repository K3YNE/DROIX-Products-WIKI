# R46S

Budget retro gaming handheld with a unique 1:1 aspect ratio display. Powered by Rockchip RK3566 quad-core ARM Cortex-A53 @1.8 GHz with Mali-G52 MP2 GPU. 4-inch IPS OCA laminated screen at 720x720 (1:1). 1 GB LPDDR4 RAM. Dual TF card slots (one for OS, one for games). Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity. Handles PS1, PSP, Dreamcast, and N64 emulation. 4000 mAh battery for up to 7-8 hours. Branded as "GameForce" on DROIX. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
