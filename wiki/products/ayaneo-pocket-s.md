# AYANEO Pocket S

Android handheld from [[ayaneo|AYANEO]] powered by the [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3x-gen-2|Qualcomm Snapdragon G3x Gen 2]] with Adreno A32 GPU at 1000 MHz. Features a 6-inch IPS touchscreen available in 1080p (368 PPI) or 1440p (490 PPI) resolution, 16:9 aspect ratio, at 400-nit brightness. Available in configurations from 12GB/128GB UFS 3.1 up to 16GB/1TB UFS 4.0 LPDDR5X. Equipped with Hall-effect sticks and triggers, gyroscope, Wi-Fi 7, and a 6000 mAh battery. Currently in stock at DROIX.
