## Summary

The AYANEO AIR Plus is a Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo]], positioned as a larger and more powerful member of the AIR family compared to the slim [[ayaneo-air]]. It shipped in three distinct hardware variants: AMD Ryzen 7 6800U, AMD Ryzen 7 7520U / 7320U ("7X20U"), and Intel Core i5-1215U. Each variant has its own BIOS, EC firmware, and driver packages. The 6800U variant is the highest-performing configuration. The Intel variant targets a different price-to-performance segment. All variants share the same handle firmware (v1.2) and run Windows 11 with AYA SPACE software. DROIX stocks the AYANEO AIR Plus.

## Variants

- **AMD 6800U** — Ryzen 7 6800U, 16/32 GB RAM options; BIOS supports video memory modification
- **AMD 7X20U** — Ryzen 7 7520U or 7320U; updated platform
- **Intel 1215U** — Intel Core i5-1215U; 8/16 GB RAM options
