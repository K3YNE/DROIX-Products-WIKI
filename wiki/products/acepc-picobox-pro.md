# ACEPC PicoBox Pro

[[acepc|ACEPC]] PicoBox Pro is a budget mini PC powered by the Intel N100 (4c/4t, up to 3.4 GHz, 6W TDP). Ships with 32 GB DDR4 SO-DIMM and 512 GB or 1 TB NVMe PCIe 3.0 SSD with a 2.5" SATA expansion bay. Features dual HDMI 4K@60Hz output and Wi-Fi 6. Runs Windows 11 Pro. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
