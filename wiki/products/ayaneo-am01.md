## Summary

The AYANEO AM01 is a Windows mini PC from [[ayaneo]], the predecessor to the [[ayaneo-am02]] in AYANEO's AM-series desktop mini PC line. It is a compact Windows 11 system built around an AMD APU platform. The AM01 targets home and desktop use cases where AYANEO's software ecosystem and compact design are desired without a handheld form factor. DROIX supplies driver resources for the AYANEO AM01.
