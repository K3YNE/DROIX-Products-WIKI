# AYN Odin 2

[[ayn|AYN]]'s 6-inch Android handheld powered by the [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-gen-2|Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2]]. The Odin 2 is the standard-size flagship in the Odin 2 family, sitting between the smaller [[ayn-odin-2-mini]] and the larger [[ayn-odin-2-portal]]. Available in three RAM/storage configurations: 8 GB/128 GB, 12 GB/256 GB, and 16 GB/512 GB.

## Design & Build

- 6" IPS LCD, 1920x1080, 16:9, 367 PPI, touchscreen
- [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-gen-2|Snapdragon 8 Gen 2]] (8 cores / 8 threads), Adreno 740 GPU
- Android 13
- 8 / 12 / 16 GB LPDDR5X RAM
- 128 / 256 / 512 GB UFS 4.0 storage + MicroSD expansion
- 8000 mAh battery, 65W QC 5.0 fast charging

## Ports & I/O

- USB 3.1 Type-C
- Micro HDMI video output
- 3.5 mm headphone jack
- Dual speakers
- Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.3
