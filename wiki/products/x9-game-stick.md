# X9 Game Stick

Compact retro gaming console that plugs directly into a TV via HDMI. Powered by Amlogic S905X3 quad-core Cortex-A55 with Mali-G31 MP2 GPU @750 MHz. 2 GB DDR3 RAM, 64 GB storage with 30,000+ pre-installed games. Runs EmuELEC 4.3. Supports up to 4K output. Includes two 2.4 GHz wireless controllers and HDMI cable. Branded as "GameForce" on DROIX. Refurbished only. Out of stock at [[droix|DROIX]].
