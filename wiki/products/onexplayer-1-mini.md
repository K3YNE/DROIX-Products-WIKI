## Summary

The ONEXPLAYER Mini (i7-1195G7) is a compact Windows handheld gaming PC from [[onexplayer]], released in early 2022. It is the smaller sibling of the full-size ONEXPLAYER 1, built around the Intel Core i7-1195G7 processor. DROIX reviewed the Mini as "a smaller handheld packing a punch," noting its more pocketable dimensions while retaining the Windows PC gaming capability of the larger model.

## Design & Build

The ONEXPLAYER Mini trades the large 8.4-inch display of the full-size model for a more compact form factor designed to be easier to hold and carry. It retains the Windows handheld layout with thumbsticks, triggers, bumpers, and face buttons typical of the ONEXPLAYER lineup.
