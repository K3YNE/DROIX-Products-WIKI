## Summary

The AYANEO 2021 is a Windows handheld gaming PC from [[ayaneo]], and one of the earliest handheld PCs to offer genuine competition to GPD in the handheld gaming space. The base model is powered by the AMD Ryzen 5 4500U. A more capable AYANEO 2021 Pro variant followed, offering a more powerful CPU configuration. The device runs full Windows and brought AYANEO's distinctive ergonomic handheld form factor to consumers for the first time at scale. DROIX stocked the 2021 series including Dark Star and Light Moon colourways, and later 1 TB storage configurations. The AYANEO 2021 series driver package covers the standard 2021, 2021 Pro, and Retro Power models.

## Design & Build

The AYANEO 2021 established AYANEO's design language: a premium handheld chassis with analogue sticks, Xbox-style face buttons, and a large touchscreen display. The device was noted by DROIX for competing directly with GPD WIN 3, GPD WIN MAX 2021, and the ONEXPLAYER 1S. A packaging update mid-run introduced a premium unboxing experience described as "Apple tier". Colour options included Dark Star, Light Moon, and Retro Power editions.
