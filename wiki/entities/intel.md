# Intel

Intel is a US semiconductor company whose N-series processors power the [[gpd-micropc-2|GPD MicroPC 2]] — the only Intel-powered product currently in this wiki. Intel N-series (formerly Alder Lake-N) are low-power, fanless-capable processors aimed at field work and productivity rather than gaming.
