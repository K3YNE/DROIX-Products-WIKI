# Unisoc

Unisoc is a Chinese semiconductor company whose T-series SoCs power budget Android handhelds. In the DROIX product lineup, Unisoc appears in the [[anbernic-rg-slide|Anbernic RG Slide]], where the [[unisoc-t820|T820]] delivers mid-tier performance sufficient for retro gaming and lighter emulation.
