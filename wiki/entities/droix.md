# DroiX

DroiX is both a YouTube channel producing handheld gaming device reviews and a retailer selling these devices. The channel covers a wide range of products from budget to premium, including handheld gaming PCs, Android handhelds, eGPUs, UMPCs, and accessories.

## Retail Operations

DroiX operates three regional storefronts:

- **droix.co.uk** (UK)
- **droix.eu** (EU)
- **droix.net** (worldwide)

All orders come with an exclusive 2-year warranty and dedicated customer service. DroiX ships globally.

DroiX does not stock every device reviewed on the channel. For example, the [[ONEXPLAYER G1]] and [[AYANEO Flip 1S DS]] are not sold by DroiX, though alternatives are available in the store.

## YouTube Channel

The DroiX channel reviews devices from manufacturers including [[AYANEO]], [[AYN]], [[GPD]], [[ONEXPLAYER]], [[Anbernic]], and [[KONKR]], covering everything from budget retro handhelds to flagship Windows gaming PCs.
