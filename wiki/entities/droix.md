# DROIX

DROIX is a UK-based retailer of handheld gaming PCs, Android handhelds, UMPCs, mini PCs, eGPUs, and related accessories, and the publisher of the DROIX YouTube channel which produces reviews of the products it sells. DROIX is both the origin of this knowledge base and a major voice in its coverage.

## Retail

Three regional storefronts:

- **droix.co.uk** — UK.
- **droix.eu** — EU.
- **droix.net** — worldwide.

All orders ship with an exclusive 2-year warranty and dedicated customer service. DROIX ships globally.

DROIX does not stock every device reviewed on the channel — for example the [[onexplayer-g1]] and the [[ayaneo-flip-1s-ds]] have been reviewed but are not listed in the store. Alternatives are typically recommended in the review.

## YouTube channel

The DROIX channel reviews devices from [[gpd]], [[ayaneo]], [[ayn]], [[onexplayer]], [[anbernic]], [[konkr]], [[retroid]], and other manufacturers — from budget retro handhelds to flagship Windows gaming PCs. Every source page tagged `review-youtube` in this wiki traces back to the DROIX channel.

## Brands DROIX represents

Primary: [[gpd]], [[ayaneo]], [[onexplayer]], [[ayn]], [[retroid]]. Also covered: [[anbernic]], [[konkr]], [[miyoo]], and others as they appear in the lineup.
