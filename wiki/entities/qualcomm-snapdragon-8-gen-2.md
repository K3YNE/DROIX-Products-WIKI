# Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2

The Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 is Qualcomm's 2022 flagship mobile SoC, built on a 4nm TSMC process. In the DROIX lineup it powers the [[ayn-thor|AYN Thor]].

## Key Specs

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| CPU | 1× Cortex-X3 (3.2 GHz) + 4× A715 + 3× A510 |
| GPU | Adreno 740 |
| Process | 4nm TSMC |
| NPU | Hexagon |

## Products Using This Chip

- [[ayn-odin-2]] — 6" IPS LCD Android handheld
- [[ayn-odin-2-mini]] — compact 5" Mini LED Android handheld
- [[ayn-odin-2-portal]] — 7" OLED Android handheld
- [[ayn-thor]] — compact dual-screen Android handheld

## Performance Context

The Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 was superseded in DROIX reviews by the [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3-gen-3|Snapdragon G3 Gen 3]] as the Android gaming performance benchmark. See [[android-handheld-gaming]] for comparative context.

## Contradictions and Corrections

Earlier wiki navigation attached [[ayaneo-pocket-ace]] to Snapdragon 8 Gen 2, but [[2025-05-23-ayaneo-pocket-ace-review-youtube]] and [[ayaneo-pocket-ace-product-page]] both identify that product as [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3x-gen-2|Snapdragon G3x Gen 2]]. The [[ayaneo-pocket-ace|Pocket ACE]] has therefore been removed from this chip page.
