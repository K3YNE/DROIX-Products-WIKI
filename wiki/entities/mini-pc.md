## Key Buying Considerations

- **Processor class**: Budget N-series (web/office) vs mid-range Core i5/Ryzen 5 (productivity) vs performance i7/i9/Ryzen 9 (creative work, light gaming) vs flagship Core Ultra (AI workloads, best iGPU).
- **RAM type**: DDR4 (budget/mid) vs DDR5/LPDDR5 (performance/flagship). DDR5 offers higher bandwidth.
- **Storage**: NGFF SATA (budget) vs NVMe PCIe 3.0 (mid) vs PCIe 4.0 (performance). Check expansion slots.
- **Connectivity**: Budget models often have 1 Gbps Ethernet; performance models offer 2.5 Gbps. Wi-Fi 6/6E/7 varies.
- **Display output**: Most support dual 4K@60Hz; flagship models add Thunderbolt 4 for triple display and eGPU.
