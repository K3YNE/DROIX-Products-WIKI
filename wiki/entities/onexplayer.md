# ONEXPLAYER

ONEXPLAYER (also stylised 1X Player; operating company One-Netbook) manufactures Windows handheld gaming PCs, eGPU docking stations, and gaming tablets. Known for large displays, OCuLink connectivity, modular battery systems, and Harman audio.

## Hardware Characteristics

- Large displays vs competitors (7"-14" range).
- OCuLink for external GPU connectivity (X1 series, eGPUs).
- External/modular battery systems (Apex).
- Optional liquid-cooling accessories (e.g. Frostbay for the Apex).
- Harman AudioEFX speakers across product lines.
- OneXConsole game management software.
- Detachable controllers on X1 series (3-in-1 tablet/console/laptop).
