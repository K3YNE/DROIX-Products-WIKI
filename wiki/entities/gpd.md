# GPD

GPD (GamePad Digital) is a Chinese manufacturer of Windows handheld PCs and ultra-mobile PCs (UMPCs), founded in 2016. They pioneered the handheld Windows PC category and have consistently been first to market with new AMD APU families. [[droix|DROIX]] is a primary UK/EU/worldwide retailer.

## Hardware Characteristics

- The WIN series historically featured integrated keyboards; the [[gpd-win-5]] dropped this.
- External modular battery systems keep base unit lighter.
- Mini SSD format adopted first on the WIN 5.
- Windows Hello fingerprint sensors on select models.
