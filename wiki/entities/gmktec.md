# GMKTec

GMKTec is a Chinese manufacturer of compact mini PCs sold under the NucBox brand. Their lineup ranges from budget Intel N95/N100 systems to mid-range Intel Core i5 and AMD Ryzen 7 models, and up to Intel Core Ultra 5 125H for performance users. DROIX stocks GMKTec NucBox mini PCs for home office and business use.
