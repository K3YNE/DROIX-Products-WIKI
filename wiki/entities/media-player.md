## Key Buying Considerations

- **HDR support**: GB1 and GX1 support Dolby Vision; G2 series has dynamic HDR but no Dolby Vision.
- **Audio**: GB1 and GX1 support Dolby Atmos; G2 has SPDIF optical.
- **Storage**: G2 series has more storage (32-64 GB + MicroSD); GB1 has only 16 GB with no expansion.
- **Gaming**: G2 RTX5000 bundle includes GP1500 Bluetooth controller.
- **Premium streaming**: All support Widevine L1 for Netflix/Disney+ in HD.
