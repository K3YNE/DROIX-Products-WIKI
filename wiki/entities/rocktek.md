# RockTek

RockTek manufactures Android TV boxes and streaming media players. Their product line includes the G2 series (Amlogic S905X4-based Android TV boxes with optional game controller bundles) and the GB1/GX1 (Realtek RTD-based Google TV streaming sticks/boxes with Dolby Vision and Dolby Atmos support). DROIX stocks RockTek devices as home entertainment solutions.
