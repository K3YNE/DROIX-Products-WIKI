# AYN

AYN manufactures Android gaming handhelds known for AMOLED displays, glass fronts, competitive pricing, and swappable buttons (Odin series).

## Products covered in this wiki

- [[ayn-thor]] — Dual-screen Android handheld (7 OLED variant reviewed).
- [[ayn-odin-3]] — Flagship Android handheld with Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite.

## Other AYN products (not yet covered)

- AYN Odin 2 Portal — predecessor flagship; previously the Android performance reference in DROIX reviews (now overtaken by the [[ayn-odin-3]]).

## Hardware Characteristics

- AMOLED displays across the lineup.
- Glass front construction.
- RGB-lit analog sticks.
- Swappable face buttons on the Odin 3.
- Optional TPU grip accessories.
