# AYN

AYN is a manufacturer of Android gaming handhelds known for AMOLED displays, competitive pricing, and solid build quality.

## Key Products Reviewed

- [[AYN Thor]] - dual-screen Android handheld
- [[AYN Odin 3]] - flagship Android handheld with Snapdragon 8 Elite
- **AYN Odin 2 Portal** - predecessor device, frequently used as a benchmark reference in earlier [[DroiX]] reviews

## Hardware Characteristics

- AMOLED displays across the lineup
- Glass front construction for a premium feel
- RGB-lit analog sticks
- Swappable buttons on the [[AYN Odin 3]]
- Optional TPU grips available as accessories
- Competitive pricing relative to premium competitors like [[AYANEO]]

## Notable Achievements

The [[AYN Odin 3]] with Snapdragon 8 Elite became the Android performance benchmark champion, overtaking the Odin 2 Portal that had served as the reference device in earlier reviews. The [[AYN Thor]] is AYN's dual-screen entry, competing directly with the [[AYANEO Pocket DS]].
