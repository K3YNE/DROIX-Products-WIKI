# KONKR

KONKR is [[AYANEO]]'s budget brand, offering flagship-level processors at significantly reduced prices compared to the premium AYANEO lineup.

## Key Products Reviewed

- [[KONKR Pocket FIT]] - budget Android handheld

## Value Proposition

KONKR devices use the same flagship processors found in premium [[AYANEO]] handhelds (Snapdragon G3 Gen 3, Snapdragon 8 Elite) at approximately 58% lower price. They also inherit AYANEO's iSpace software ecosystem.

## Tradeoffs vs Premium AYANEO

- LCD display instead of IPS or OLED panels
- Slightly lower build quality feel compared to AYANEO's CNC aluminum models
- Same processor performance and software experience

KONKR represents excellent value for money for buyers who prioritize performance and software over display quality and premium materials.
