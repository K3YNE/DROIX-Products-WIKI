# KONKR

KONKR is [[ayaneo|AYANEO]]'s budget sub-brand, delivering flagship-class SoCs at approximately 58 % lower prices than the equivalent premium AYANEO handhelds.

## Products covered in this wiki

- [[konkr-pocket-fit]] — Budget Android handheld with Qualcomm Snapdragon G3 Gen 3.

## Value Proposition

- Same flagship SoCs found in premium AYANEO handhelds (Snapdragon G3 Gen 3, Snapdragon 8 Elite).
- Shares AYANEO's iSpace software ecosystem.
- Tradeoffs vs premium AYANEO: LCD display (not IPS/OLED); slightly lower build tier (no CNC aluminium).
- Same processor performance and the same software experience.
