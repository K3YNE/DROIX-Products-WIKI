# Beelink

Beelink is a Chinese manufacturer specialising in compact Windows mini PCs. Their product line spans budget Intel Celeron systems (Mini S, S12, T5) through mid-range Intel Core and AMD Ryzen office PCs (SEi, EQR series) to high-performance workstations (GTi series with i9-13900HK, Core Ultra 9 185H). DROIX stocks a wide range of Beelink mini PCs for home and office use.
