# UMPC (Ultra-Mobile PC)

Ultra-Mobile PCs are compact Windows devices designed for productivity, field work, or portable computing rather than gaming. They run full Windows and typically include features like touchscreens, rotating displays, or industry-specific ports that gaming handhelds omit.

## Key Buying Considerations

- **GPD MicroPC 2** targets IT professionals, field technicians, and developers needing a ruggedised pocket PC with rotating touchscreen. Not suited to gaming. N250 for basic tasks; N300 for multi-threaded workloads.
- **GPD Pocket 4** is more versatile — AMD APU options make it capable of light gaming alongside productivity.
- UMPCs are typically smaller and lighter than [[handheld-pc|Handheld PCs]], with smaller displays.
