# Miyoo

Miyoo is a Chinese manufacturer of budget retro gaming handhelds. Their devices run Linux-based operating systems and target the affordable end of the retro emulation market. Miyoo handhelds are popular for their compact form factors, custom firmware community support (notably Onion OS for the Mini Plus), and low price points.
