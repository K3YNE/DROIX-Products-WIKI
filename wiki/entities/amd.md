# AMD

Advanced Micro Devices (AMD) is a US semiconductor company whose APUs and discrete GPUs power several DROIX products. AMD's Ryzen AI (Strix Point) and Ryzen AI Max (Strix Halo) families brought a step-change in handheld gaming performance, while AMD's discrete GPU (RX 7600M XT) appears in the [[ayaneo-ag01|AYANEO AG01]] eGPU dock.
