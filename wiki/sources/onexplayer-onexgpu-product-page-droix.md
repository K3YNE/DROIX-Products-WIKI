# ONEXPLAYER ONEXGPU -- DROIX Product Page

Source: [DROIX product page](https://droix.net/product/onexplayer-onexgpu)

## Product Overview

The [[onexplayer-onexgpu|ONEXPLAYER ONEXGPU]] eGPU docking station features the [[amd-rx-7600m-xt|AMD Radeon RX 7600M XT]] GPU. Billed as the "world's first portable eGPU with storage" due to its built-in M.2 2280 NVMe slot. Connects via OCuLink and USB 4.0. Out of stock at DROIX.

## Key Marketing Claims

- "World's 1st Portable EGPU With Storage"
- AMD Radeon RX 7600M XT: 32 CU, up to 2300 MHz, 8GB GDDR6
- AAA gaming up to 4K resolution
- Drives up to 4 displays (2x HDMI + 2x DP)
- 300W GaN charger with 100W USB-C PD charging
- 19.6 x 12.0 x 3.2 cm, 789g
- M.2 2280 PCIe 3.0 NVMe expansion slot
