- Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity.
- Handles PS1, PSP, Dreamcast, N64 emulation.
- Open-source Linux OS.
- Ergonomic design with vibration support.

- Body claims 4000 mAh and up to 8 hours; spec table says up to 7 hours. Minor discrepancy.
- Body says 256 GB expandable storage; spec table lists 64/128 GB options.
