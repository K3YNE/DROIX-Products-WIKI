## Summary

GPDStore blog review of the [[gpd-win-5]] (pre-production) with AMD Ryzen AI MAX+ 395 and Radeon 8060S. Revolutionary performance leap — more than double previous gen at 28 W TDP.

## Key Specs

### Ryzen AI MAX+ 395 Model
- **CPU:** 16-core Zen 5, XDNA 2 (126 TOPS)
- **GPU:** AMD Radeon 8060S (integrated), RDNA 3.5, 40 CU (2560 SP), 2.9 GHz, FreeSync Premium, FSR 3.1

### Ryzen AI MAX 385 Model
- **CPU:** 8 cores/16 threads, Zen 5, up to 5 GHz, 106 TOPS
- **GPU:** Radeon 8050S, 32 CU, 2.8 GHz

### Shared Specs
- **RAM:** 32/64/128 GB LPDDR5x unified memory @ 8000 MT/s
- **Storage:** 1/2/4 TB M.2 2280 PCIe Gen4 + Mini SSD slot (PCIe Gen4 x1) + MicroSD
- **Display:** 7", 120 Hz, 1920x1080, AMD FreeSync Premium, 100% sRGB, 315 PPI, 500 nits
- **Controls:** Capacitive joysticks (zero dead zone), hall effect triggers
- **Power:** 80 Wh external removable battery + 180 W DC adapter (supports up to 85 W TDP at launch)
- **Dimensions:** 10.5 x 4.3 x 0.95 inches (handheld); battery adds 350 g
- **Weight:** 565 g (handheld) + 350 g (battery)

## Battery & Thermals

- 80 W TDP max brightness Cinebench: ~42 min
- 55 W idle desktop 50% brightness: ~10 hours
- Average gaming: ~2-3 hours
- Fan noise: 67 dB peak
- Temperature: 48°C peak (without battery)

## Mini SSD

First device with BIWIN Mini SSD. PCIe 4x1 interface in WIN 5 — CrystalDiskMark: ~1700 MB/s read/write (half of full 4x2 potential of ~3700 MB/s).
