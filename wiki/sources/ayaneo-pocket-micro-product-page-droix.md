## Notable Features

- **Ultra-compact form factor**: The smallest handheld in the AYANEO Pocket series with a 3.5-inch non-touch display.
- **CNC aluminium alloy construction**: Premium build quality despite the budget-tier SoC.
- **No video output**: USB-C is USB 2.0 OTG only — no DisplayPort or video-out capability.
- **Non-touch display**: One of the few modern handhelds without a touchscreen; relies on physical controls only.
- **4 colour options**: Magic Black, Retro Gold, Soul Red, and Gray.
- **Helio G99 SoC**: A mid-range MediaTek chip — significantly less powerful than the Snapdragon G3x Gen 2 used in other Pocket models. Suited to retro emulation and lighter Android games.
