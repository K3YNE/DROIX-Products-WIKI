- Emulates 2nd to 7th generation handheld systems.
- Compact: 4.25 x 3.07 x 0.8 in (10.8 x 7.8 x 2.2 cm), 165 g.

## Pros/Cons (from listing)

**Pros:** Powerful dual-core for retro gaming, 64 GB storage + 128 GB expansion, 3.5" 640x480 IPS, Wi-Fi for online play, multiple colour options.

**Cons:** Basic default OS menus (Onion firmware recommended), 3.5" screen may feel small compared to larger handhelds.
