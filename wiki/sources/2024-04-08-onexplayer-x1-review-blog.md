## FPS / Gaming Performance

We can see the pattern continues with the X1 trailing behind across the benchmarks. While we do get over 60 FPS at 720P 15W and above, we see the [AMD](https://droix.net/product-attribute/brands/amd/) models have vastly higher scores.
