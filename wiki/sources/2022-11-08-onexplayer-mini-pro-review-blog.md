## Summary

DROIX blog review of the [[onexplayer-mini-pro]] with AMD Ryzen 7 6800U. 16 GB and 32 GB models tested. Benchmarks at 11/20/28 W TDP with comparison to [[gpd-win-max-2]] and AOKZOE A1.

## Key Specs

| Spec | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **CPU** | AMD Ryzen 7 6800U, Zen 3, 8 cores / 16 threads, 4.7 GHz, up to 28 W TDP |
| **GPU** | Radeon 680M |
| **Display** | 7" IPS, 1920x1200, 323 PPI, 10-point touch |
| **RAM** | 16 GB or 32 GB LPDDR5-6400 |
| **Storage** | 1 TB or 2 TB M.2 2280 NVMe PCIe 3.0 x4 |
| **Connectivity** | WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.0 |
| **Dimensions** | 126 x 106 x 23 mm, 599 g |
| **Battery** | 48 Wh; idle @ 28 W: 4 hr 48 min; full load @ 28 W: 1 hr 12 min |
| **Fan noise** | 68 dB |
| **Temperature** | 52 C |

## Reviewer Verdict

Huge step up from previous generation. Right size for a handheld, comfortable for extended play. ONEXPLAYER Overlay is excellent. Battery life at 28 W TDP (just over 1 hour full load) is the main concern. 25 W TDP recommended as sweet spot.
