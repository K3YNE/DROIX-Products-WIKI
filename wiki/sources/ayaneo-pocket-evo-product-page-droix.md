## Notable Features

- **800-nit 7" OLED**: The brightest display in the G3x Gen 2 Pocket lineup, with variable refresh rate (120/165/60 Hz).
- **250 Hz polling rate controls**: Both the RGB Hall joysticks and linear Hall triggers run at 250 Hz for low-latency input.
- **8600 mAh battery**: The largest battery in the AYANEO Pocket series, supporting extended play sessions.
- **Fingerprint sensor**: Biometric security not available on all Pocket models.
- **HD linear motor**: Advanced haptic feedback for immersive gaming.
