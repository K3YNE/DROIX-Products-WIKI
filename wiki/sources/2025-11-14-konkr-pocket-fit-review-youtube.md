# KONKR Pocket FIT Review

The [[konkr-pocket-fit|KONKR Pocket FIT]] is a budget Android gaming handheld from [[konkr|KONKR]], [[ayaneo|AYANEO]]'s budget brand. Reviewed on 2025-11-14, the device was praised as a "great alternative to the premium [[ayaneo-pocket-s2|Pocket S2]]" — delivering flagship-level benchmark scores at roughly 58% less cost.


## Key Specs

- **Processor options:** [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3-gen-3|Snapdragon G3 Gen 3]] (same as Pocket S2) or **Snapdragon 8 Elite**
- **Display:** 6" LCD 1080p 144Hz
- **Weight:** 386g
- **Battery:** 8,000mAh
- **Ergonomics:** Built-in grips

## Key Findings

- Benchmark scores match the premium [[ayaneo-pocket-s2|AYANEO Pocket S2]]
- Approximately 58% cheaper than the Pocket S2
- Has built-in grips, which the Pocket S2 lacks
- Uses an LCD display (vs IPS on the S2) — a tradeoff for the lower price
- The [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3-gen-3|Snapdragon G3 Gen 3]] option uses the same chip as the Pocket S2
- The **Snapdragon 8 Elite** option provides an even more powerful alternative
- Budget champion with flagship-level performance
