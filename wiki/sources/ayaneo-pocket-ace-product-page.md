## Contradictions and Notes

- The page identifies the SoC as [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3x-gen-2|Snapdragon G3x Gen 2]], matching [[2025-05-23-ayaneo-pocket-ace-review-youtube]]. Some older wiki index/entity entries previously attached the [[ayaneo-pocket-ace|Pocket ACE]] to [[qualcomm-snapdragon-8-gen-2|Snapdragon 8 Gen 2]]; this product page supports correcting that attachment to G3x Gen 2.
- The page body states a 3:2 display aspect ratio, while the attribute table lists 16:9. The 1620 x 1080 resolution is mathematically 3:2, so treat the 16:9 attribute as suspect unless a later source confirms it.
- The body lists a 12GB / 256GB configuration, while the captured attribute table's storage-capacity row omits 256GB. The configuration row includes the 12GB / 256GB UFS 4.0 SKU.
