## Summary

DROIX blog review of the [[beelink-sei-11-pro]] mini PC with Intel Core i5-11320H processor. Includes system benchmarks, gaming benchmarks, gameplay tests, and emulator performance.

## Key Specs

| Spec | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **CPU** | Intel Core i5-11320H, 4 cores / 8 threads, up to 4.5 GHz, 35 W TDP |
| **GPU** | Intel Iris Xe, up to 1.35 GHz |
| **RAM** | 16 GB DDR4 dual channel (max 64 GB) |
| **Storage** | 500 GB M.2 NVMe 2280 SSD (max 2 TB) + 2.5" HDD/SSD bay |
| **Connectivity** | Gigabit Ethernet, WiFi 6 (802.11ax), Bluetooth 5.2 |
| **OS** | Windows 11 |
| **Dimensions** | 4.9 x 4.4 x 1.5 in (12.6 x 11.3 x 4.0 cm) |
| **Weight** | 439 g |
| **Ports** | 2x USB 3.0, 1x USB-C (front); 2x USB 2.0, 2x HDMI, Gigabit Ethernet (rear); 3.5 mm headphone |
| **Fan noise** | Max 55 dB (Cinebench 10 min) |
| **Temperature** | Max 60 C (Cinebench 10 min, tested at 23 C ambient) |

## System Benchmarks

| Benchmark | SEi 11 PRO | SEi 10 i5 | SER4 |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|------|
| **PassMark** | 4118 | 2289 | 4484 |
| **PCMark** | 4925 | 3635 | 5224 |
| **3DMark** | 1402 | 700 | 1209 |
| **Cinebench** | 4906 | N/A | 6653 |
| **CrystalDiskMark** | 2490 / 1933 MB/s | 2490 / 1711 MB/s | 2559 / 1835 MB/s |
| **Price** | GBP 499.95 | GBP 499.95 | GBP 509.00 |

57%, 30%, and 66% improvement over SEi 10 i5 on first three tests. SER4 (AMD) 5-30% ahead of SEi 11.

## Emulation Results

| System | Emulator | Performance |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| PS2 | PCSX2 | Full FPS, no issues, can increase rendering |
| 3DS | Citra | Mixed; shader cache lag on first play |
| GameCube/Wii | Dolphin | Spot on, no major issues |
| PSP | PPSSPP | God of War mostly solid 60 FPS |
| PS3 | RPCS3 | Wipeout HD at 30 FPS cap has occasional drops; depends on game |

## Reviewer Conclusion

Capable of light gaming at decent frame rates despite not being a gaming mini PC. Good value compared to AMD SER4 equivalent. SEi 11 PRO recommended for budget-conscious buyers.
