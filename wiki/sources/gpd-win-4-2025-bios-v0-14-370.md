# GPD WIN 4 (2025, AI 9 HX 370) — BIOS v0.14

Current BIOS for the [[gpd-win-4]] 2025 model with AI 9 HX 370 APU only. Does not apply to the 8840U variant.

**Installation:** Format a USB drive to FAT32, place the BIOS file on the drive, power on and press F7 to select USB boot and flash. Keep device on charge. WinRAR decompression password: `123`.
