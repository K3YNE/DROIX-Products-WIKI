## FPS / Gaming Performance

- While this is far from being a gaming laptop, more of a laptop for home office, as part of this [GMKTec Gbook](https://droix.net/product/gmktec-gbook/) review we can’t resist trying them as it can run some games just fine. As a test, we ran the Forza Horizon benchmark at 720P on the Very Low graphics settings and we got a fairly impressive 37 FPS which is enough to keep the game playable locked to 30 FPS with some minor dips in performance now and again.
- Something a little simpler but equally as fun. Lower demanding games work great! You can run them at higher resolutions and/or graphics quality and get some decent results. The recently released Minecraft Legends is running well above 60 FPS, and the great Turtles Shredder’s Revenge runs perfectly.
