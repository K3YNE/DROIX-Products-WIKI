## Summary

DROIX blog review of the DROIX X4 Android TV box with Amlogic S905X4 processor. Covers benchmarks, video playback, gaming, and emulation. Bundled with DROIX G10S air mouse remote.

## Key Specs

| Spec | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **CPU** | Amlogic S905X4 Quad-Core ARM |
| **GPU** | ARM Mali-G31 MP2 |
| **RAM** | 4 GB DDR3 |
| **Storage** | 64 GB |
| **Connectivity** | WiFi 5, Bluetooth |
| **Dimensions** | 4.1 x 4.1 x 0.98 in (10.5 x 10.5 x 2.5 cm), 139 g |
| **Temperature** | Max 50 C (Antutu 10 min) |

## Benchmarks

| Benchmark | Score |
|-----------|-------|
| **Geekbench** | 139 SC / 470 MC |
| **AnTuTu** | 101,393 |
| **3DMark Sling Shot** | 596 |
| **3DMark Wild Life** | 187 |

Performance roughly in the ballpark of the Anbernic RG353V.

## Features

- LCD panel on front shows current time
- Google Play Store pre-installed
- eShare app for phone/tablet mirroring and remote control
- DROIX G10S air mouse remote with backlight, voice search, gyro cursor control
- Supports 4K and 8K video decoding (output at 4K)
- Root/unroot toggle without reboot

## Emulation Performance

- All 8-bit and 16-bit consoles: no issues via RetroArch
- Up to PlayStation 1: runs fine
- PSP (PPSSPP): mixed; 30 FPS games OK, demanding games need frame skipping
- Xbox Cloud Gaming: AAA games stream perfectly at highest quality

## Reviewer Verdict

Best Android TV box available from DROIX. Good for streaming, media playback, retro gaming up to PS1 era, and cloud gaming.
