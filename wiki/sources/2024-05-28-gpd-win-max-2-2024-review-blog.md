## Summary

GPDStore blog review of the [[gpd-win-max-2]] 2024 model with AMD Ryzen 7 8840U. Compared against the 2023 model and other 8840U devices including [[gpd-win-4]] 2024 and GPD WIN Mini 2024.

## Key Specs

- **CPU:** AMD Ryzen 7 8840U, 8 cores/16 threads, up to 5.1 GHz
- **GPU:** AMD Radeon 780M, up to 2700 MHz
- **RAM:** 32 GB or 64 GB LPDDR5x @ 6400 MHz
- **Storage:** 1 TB / 2 TB / 4 TB M.2 NVMe SSD
- **Display:** 10.1" IPS touchscreen, 1920x1200 (supports up to 2560x1600), 16:10, 299 PPI
- **I/O:** USB 4, USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-C, USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-A, Oculink (SFF-8612), HDMI, Micro SD, SD, 3.5 mm audio
- **Battery:** 67 Wh (Cinebench loop: 1 hr 52 min; average use: 6-8 hours)
- **Connectivity:** WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.2
- **Weight:** 1005 g

## Thermals

- Fan noise: peaks at 60 dB
- Temperature: 43°C keyboard, 63°C exhaust grill

## Benchmark Results

| Benchmark | Score |

## Gaming Benchmarks

- **Forza Horizon 5**: slight improvement over 2023 model at 1080p; near-identical at 720p
- **Shadow of the Tomb Raider**: consistent at 1080p
- **Call of Duty: MW III**: strong at 1080p
- **Street Fighter VI**: minor FPS differences

Also tested with [[gpd-g1|GPD G1]] 2024 eGPU — comparable performance to WIN 4 2024 in game benchmarks.

## Verdict

Significant improvements over 2023 model with 8840U. Excellent balance of work and gaming. 10.1" screen ideal for both productivity and gaming. Price: $1,076.95-$1,838.95.
