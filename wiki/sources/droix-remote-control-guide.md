Here are several ways to control your TV wirelessly that installed Android mini PC:

**1. USB Mouse and Keyboard**  
Connect a mouse and/or keyboard to your DROIX device, via USB. If you run out of sockets, you can connect a USB hub to your DROIX model.  
*Pros: It is convenient to read news online or type a lot of text in this way, and it is a familiar control mechanism.*  
*Cons: There are too many wires.  The distance from your TV is governed by the length of your mouse/keyboard’s USB cables.*

**2. 2.4GHz Wireless Mouse and Keyboard**Plug in the USB dongle (receiver) to your DROIX device and it is ready to use (check if your mouse/keyboard requires pairing first).  
**Pros: No worry about the wires. Control without any wires, depending upon the range of the dongle. See also 1.’s Pros***Cons: Not as convenient to control, whilst sitting on your sofa.*

**3. 3-in-1 Air Mouse and Keyboard**A 3-in-1 air mouse like the [D](http://miniandroidpc.com/shop/2-4ghz-air-mouse-wireless-full-keyboard)[roidBOX® VIP Remote](http://www.amazon.co.uk/DROIX-VIPRemote-Wireless-AndroidMini-Raspberry/dp/B00FFJYB0Q/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1393884682&sr=8-1&keywords=vipremote) or multi-controller [DROIX i8](http://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/product/B00H27V3AA/ref=cm_cr_ryp_prd_ttl_sol_0) combine the function of mouse and keyboard. It’s convenient to type and scroll up or down.  
**Pros: Media shortcut buttons are often available. No wires.***Cons: It can be a little uncomfortable to type large amounts of text.*
