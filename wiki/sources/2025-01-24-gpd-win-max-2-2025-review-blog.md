## Summary

GPDStore blog review of the [[gpd-win-max-2]] 2025 with AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 and Radeon 890M. Compared against all WIN MAX 2 generations (2022-2024) and other HX 370 devices.

## Key Specs

- **CPU:** AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370, 12 cores/14 threads, up to 5.1 GHz (also available in 8840U)
- **GPU:** AMD Radeon 890M, up to 2900 MHz
- **Display:** 10.1", IPS, up to 2560x1600, 60 Hz, 16:10, 299 PPI, stylus support (4096 levels)
- **RAM:** 32 GB or 64 GB LPDDR5X
- **Storage:** 1/2/4 TB NVMe PCIe 4.0 2280
- **Battery:** 67 Wh (Cinebench loop: 1 hr 20 min; average: 3-6 hours)
- **I/O:** 2x USB 4.0, Oculink, 2x USB-A 3.2 Gen2, HDMI 2.1, SD + MicroSD, 3.5 mm
- **Camera:** 2 MP super wide-angle (1612x1212)
- **Connectivity:** WiFi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3
- **Weight:** 1098 g
- **Price:** $1,272.95-$2,880.95

## Thermals

- Fan noise: 68.8 dB peak
- Temperature: 55°C max
