#### Updating Intel Drivers Manually

The official driver package may not be updated in real time. Intel processor-based GPD devices involve Intel drivers for Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, chipset, and integrated graphics.

1. Open [Intel Driver & Support Assistant](https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/support/detect.html)
2. Download and install Intel® DSA — it is not a driver itself but identifies the driver versions of Intel hardware in your device. If the version is outdated, it will automatically connect to the internet for an upgrade.
