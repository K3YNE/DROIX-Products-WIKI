# ONEXPLAYER X1/X1 Mini Controller Connector -- DROIX Product Page

Source: [DROIX product page](https://droix.net/product/onexplayer-x1-connector)

Accessory for [[onexplayer-x1-amd|ONEXPLAYER X1 AMD]] and [[onexplayer-x1-mini|ONEXPLAYER X1 Mini]].

## Product Overview

Specialised connector that links the detachable controllers to the X1/X1 Mini main unit. Enables detached controller mode for tabletop/TV gaming while maintaining lag-free input. Also allows controllers to charge while connected.

## Key Features

- Compatible with ONEXPLAYER X1 and X1 Mini
- High-quality conductive materials for efficient signal transmission
- Zero-lag button/analog input transmission
- Robust connectors for frequent attach/detach
- Enables wireless-style play with wired reliability
- Controllers can charge while connected
- Ergonomic form factor -- does not affect grip comfort
- Quick install, no additional software needed
- Condition: New
