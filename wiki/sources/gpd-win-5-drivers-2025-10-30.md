# GPD WIN 5 — Driver Package (2025-10-30)

Current driver bundle for the [[gpd-win-5]]. Extract and run `AutoInstallDrivers.bat` for automatic installation.

GPD notes that the bundled AMD graphics driver may lag behind AMD's latest; users can manually update from AMD's downloads portal at https://www.amd.com/en/support/download/drivers.html.
