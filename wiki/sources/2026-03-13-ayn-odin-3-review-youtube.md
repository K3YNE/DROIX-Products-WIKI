# AYN Odin 3 Review

Review published 2026-03-13 covering the [[ayn|AYN]] Odin 3, the first handheld to feature the **Snapdragon 8 Elite** processor and Android 15. Described as an "excellent choice" for Android gaming.


## Key Specifications

- **SoC**: **Snapdragon 8 Elite** (first handheld with this chip)
- **Display**: 6" AMOLED, 120Hz
- **Weight**: 390g
- **OS**: Android 15 (first handheld)
- **Colors**: Five available including clear blue and clear purple
- **Controls**: Hall effect analog sticks with LEDs, swappable buttons
- **Connectivity**: Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6.0

## Performance

Benchmark scores demolish the competition: Geekbench 3123 single-core / 9819 multi-core, Antutu 2.8 million, 3DMark 6684. Smashes both the [[ayaneo-pocket-s2|AYANEO Pocket S2]] and [[konkr-pocket-fit|KONKR Pocket FIT]] in benchmarks. The new Xbox emulator (X1Box) runs on the device. However, some emulator and Vulkan driver issues were noted at the time of review.

## Design and Build

At 390g, the device is lightweight and portable. Five color options including transparent variants add personality. Hall effect analog sticks with LED lighting and swappable button caps allow customization.

## Pricing

Priced similarly to the budget [[konkr-pocket-fit|KONKR Pocket FIT]] and much cheaper than the premium [[ayaneo-pocket-s2|AYANEO Pocket S2]], making it strong value for the performance level.

## Availability

Available at [[droix|DROIX]] at the time of review.
