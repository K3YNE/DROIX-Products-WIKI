# ONEXPLAYER ONEXFLY F1 Pro -- DROIX Product Page

Source: [DROIX product page](https://droix.net/product/onexplayer-onexfly-f1-pro)

## Product Overview

The [[onexplayer-onexfly-f1-pro|ONEXPLAYER ONEXFLY F1 Pro]] is a 7" OLED 144Hz handheld gaming PC. Named PC Gamer's "Best Gaming Handheld PC in 2024". Available with three processor options: AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370, HX 365, and Ryzen 7 8840U. In stock at DROIX.

## Key Marketing Claims

- PC Gamer "Best Gaming Handheld PC in 2024" award
- Zen 5 + Zen 5c homogeneous large-small core design (HX 370/365)
- RDNA 3.5 architecture on Radeon 890M, TSMC 4nm process
- 800 nits OLED brightness with 10,000:1 contrast ratio
- NVMe SSD speeds up to 7,109 MB/s
- Harman AudioEFX dual speakers -- "best sounding handheld" per ETA PRIME
- Active cooling: liquid bearing fan up to 4700 RPM, 3 pure copper heat pipes, 14,952 mm2 aluminium radiator
- OneXConsole Game Center for performance tuning

## Notable Details

- Three processor tiers in one product listing: 8840U (budget), HX 365 (mid), HX 370 (flagship).
- Display is 16:9 (not 16:10 like many competitors), resolution is 1920x1080 (not 1200p).
- OLED panel is a major differentiator vs IPS competitors.
- Up to 4TB storage with PCIe 4.0.
- MicroSD 4.0 slot for expansion.
- 48.5 Wh battery -- similar to Mini Pro but with more power-hungry processors.
