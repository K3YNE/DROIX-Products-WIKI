# ONEXPLAYER X1 AMD -- DROIX Product Page

Source: [DROIX product page](https://droix.net/product/onexplayer-x1-amd)

## Product Overview

The [[onexplayer-x1-amd|ONEXPLAYER X1 AMD]] is a 10.95" 3-in-1 handheld gaming PC with AMD Ryzen 7 8840U, featuring detachable controllers, native OCuLink, Hall effect joysticks, and Harman AudioEFX. Out of stock at DROIX.

## Key Marketing Claims

- "Revolutionary leap in handheld gaming PCs"
- 3-in-1: gaming, work, leisure
- Ryzen 8840U: Zen 4, 8C/16T, up to 5.1 GHz, 16MB L3 cache
- Radeon 780M at 2700 MHz
- 10.95" 2.5K LTPS 120Hz display (2560x1600)
- M.2 2280 PCIe 4.0: 7114 MB/s read, 6501 MB/s write
- MicroSD up to 2TB
- 65.02 Wh battery (~8 hours), 100W GaN fast charger
- Hall effect joysticks
- Liquid bearing fan, aluminium fins, dual copper heat pipes
- Harman AudioEFX speakers
- Fingerprint + AI camera facial recognition
- 2x USB 4.0, native OCuLink, USB-A 3.2, MicroSD 4.0, 3.5mm
- Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2
