## Notable Features

- **Vertical Game Boy-style form factor**: The 3.92-inch 8:7 OLED display and vertical layout pay homage to the original Game Boy DMG design while delivering modern performance.
- **8:7 OLED display**: 1240x1080 at 419 PPI with 100,000:1 contrast — the highest pixel density and contrast ratio among AYANEO Pocket devices.
- **Wi-Fi 7**: Latest wireless standard, shared with the [[ayaneo-pocket-s|Pocket S]] but not the Pocket EVO (Wi-Fi 6E).
- **5 colour options**: The widest colour range in the Pocket series, including the limited Retro edition.
- **Active cooling**: Fan-based cooling for sustained [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3x-gen-2|G3x Gen 2]] performance despite the compact form factor.
