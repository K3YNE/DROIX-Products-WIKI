## Notable Features

- **Dual resolution options**: Available with a 1080p IPS (368 PPI) or 1440p IPS (490 PPI) display — the 1440p option is one of the highest-resolution panels in the AYANEO Pocket series.
- **Wi-Fi 7**: Latest wireless standard, shared with the [[ayaneo-pocket-dmg|Pocket DMG]].
- **Built-in microphone**: Included alongside dual speakers — not available on all Pocket models.
- **4 colour options**: Includes the Sakura Pink option not available on other Pocket devices.
- **6-inch form factor**: A mid-size option between the compact Pocket DMG (3.92") and the larger Pocket EVO (7").
