Supplier resource page for the GPD XD Plus, extracted from the GPD official firmware page. The XD Plus is an Android gaming handheld. The XD Plus has two mainboard hardware revisions; the newer "VR" revision supports PC-based recovery and direct internal storage (eMMC) upgrades. Retrieved 2026-04-21.

**Board identification:** Go to Settings → Advanced Setting → About Tablet → Build Number. If firmware version contains "VR", it is the new board. Otherwise it is the old board.
