## Notable Features

- **Legacy Zen 3 device**: The Ryzen 7 5825U is two APU generations behind current AYANEO Windows handhelds. Lacks USB4, OCuLink, and Wi-Fi 6E.
- **Refurbished option**: Available in Grade B refurbished condition alongside new units, both currently out of stock.
- **PCIe 3.0 storage**: Older storage bus compared to PCIe 4.0 on current models.
- **1280x800 display**: Lower resolution than the 1080p+ panels on newer AYANEO handhelds.
