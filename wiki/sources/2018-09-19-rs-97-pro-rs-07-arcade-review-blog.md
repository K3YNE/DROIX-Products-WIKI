## Summary

DROIX blog unboxing and demo of the RS-97 Pro handheld and RS-07 Arcade retro gaming devices.

## Key Features

- Both include 3000+ built-in games
- External SD card slot for additional games
- A/V cables for TV connection
- RS-07 includes two game pads for multiplayer
- RS-97 Pro supports custom firmware (Amstrad, Spectrum, Amiga emulators)
