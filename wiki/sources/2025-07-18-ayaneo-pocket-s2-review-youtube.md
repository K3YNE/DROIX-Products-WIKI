# AYANEO Pocket S2 Review

Review published 2025-07-18 covering the [[ayaneo|AYANEO]] Pocket S2, the first device to ship with the [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3-gen-3|Snapdragon G3 Gen 3]] chipset.


## Key Specifications

- **SoC**: [[qualcomm-snapdragon-g3-gen-3|Snapdragon G3 Gen 3]] (first device with this chipset)
- **Display**: 6.3" IPS, 1440p
- **Build**: CNC aluminum alloy frame
- **Weight**: 428g (standard) / 440g (Pro)
- **Battery**: 8000mAh (standard) / 10000mAh (Pro)

## Performance

Massive generational gains over the predecessor:

- **Antutu**: 34% improvement
- **Multi-core**: 29% improvement
- **3DMark**: 25% improvement

Crowned the new performance champion among Android gaming handhelds at time of review. The reviewer suggested it "could be Android gaming handheld of the year."

## Emulation Capabilities

- **Citra (3DS)**: Up to 4K resolution
- **PS2**: Playable with upscaling
- **Nintendo Switch**: Good emulation results

A significant step up in emulation capability compared to previous-generation **Snapdragon G3X Gen 2** devices like the [[ayaneo-pocket-ace|Pocket ACE]].

## Design and Build

The CNC aluminum alloy frame delivers a premium feel. The display is IPS rather than OLED, but the 1440p panel is noted as high quality. The Pro variant's 10000mAh battery is notably large for the category.

## Availability

Available at [[droix|DROIX]] at the time of review.
