## Product Overview

The [[onexplayer-mini-pro|ONEXPLAYER Mini Pro]] handheld gaming PC features the AMD Ryzen 7 6800U with Radeon 680M integrated graphics. Claims "full 60 FPS rates" on latest AAA Windows titles. Supports retro gaming up to most recent handheld consoles. Out of stock at DROIX.
