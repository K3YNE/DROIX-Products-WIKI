## Summary

GPDStore blog review of the [[gpd-win-5-smart-dock|GPD WIN 5 Smart Docking Station]], an official accessory for the [[gpd-win-5]].

## Key Specs

- **Dimensions:** 7.4 x 6.6 x 1.49 inches
- **Weight:** 317 g
- **Material:** Plastic construction
- **Ports:** 1x HDMI 2.1 (4K 144 Hz), 3x USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-A, 1x Gigabit Ethernet, 1x USB Type-C (battery charging only), 1x USB data (front)
- **Battery slot:** Charges GPD WIN 5 external battery with LED status indicators

## Limitations

- Rear USB-C port only charges battery in slot — does NOT power the console
- Bottom USB port on WIN 5 does not support charging
- Must power handheld separately via DC barrel jack or connected battery
- Two chargers needed if gaming while charging spare battery

## Suggested Improvements

- DC barrel jack passthrough for single-cable power
- Higher wattage USB-C to handle both console and battery
- Second port or cable for WIN 5 top USB (which does support charging)

## Verdict

Very specific accessory. Essential for users with multiple batteries. However, the inability to power the console through the dock is a significant limitation. Third-party hubs provide similar connectivity if battery charging is not needed.
