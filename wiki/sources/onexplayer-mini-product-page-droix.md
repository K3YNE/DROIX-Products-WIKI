# ONEXPLAYER Mini -- DROIX Product Page

Source: [DROIX product page](https://droix.net/product/onexplayer-mini)

## Product Overview

The [[onexplayer-mini|ONEXPLAYER Mini]] is a 7" PC gaming handheld described as the "smaller brother" of the original ONEXPLAYER. Available with AMD Ryzen 7 5800U or Intel Core i7-1195G7. In stock at DROIX.

## Key Marketing Claims

- "Like carrying around two Switch Lite consoles" (based on CPU power comparison)
- 589g weight, fits in cargo pants pocket
- Body corners angled at 145 degrees for improved grip
- Back redesigned for improved thumb comfort on shoulder buttons
- Original Japanese ALPS joysticks with 18-degree tilt angle
- ALPS triggers for FPS and driving games
- Dual vibration motors

## Notable Details

- Two processor variants with significantly different connectivity: Intel model has Thunderbolt 4 with video output; AMD model has no video output capability via USB-C.
- Storage uses PCIe Gen 3.0 (not Gen 4.0).
- 16GB RAM only -- no 32GB option.
- LPDDR4X (not LPDDR5).
