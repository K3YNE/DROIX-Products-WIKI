Welcome to our getting started with the GPD WIN Mini guide. The GPD WIN Mini is a compact and powerful handheld gaming PC that combines the portability of a pocket-sized device with the performance of a high-end laptop. Featuring a clamshell design with a 7-inch 120Hz high-refresh gaming screen, the WIN Mini is powered by AMD’s latest Ryzen 7 8840U, offering exceptional gaming and multitasking capabilities.

This device is aimed at on-the-go gamers and professionals who demand desktop-level performance in a ultra-portable form factor. With its built-in gamepad controls, keyboard, and touchpad, the WIN Mini is versatile enough for both gaming and productivity tasks.

Our getting started guide aims to help you quickly set up and optimize your GPD WIN Mini, ensuring you can make the most of its impressive features and performance right out of the box.

## Inspect the GPD WIN Mini

When you first receive your GPD WIN Mini, we recommend performing an inspection to ensure everything is in working order:

* Check the device case/shell: Carefully examine the outer case for any cracks, dents, or other damage that may have occurred during shipping.
* Test all keyboard keys: Test the keyboard at <https://keyboard-test.space/> to ensure all keys are registering correctly.
* Test all buttons and joysticks: Ensure all buttons and joysticks are responsive and registering properly at <https://hardwaretester.com/gamepad>. You can use the built-in gamepad tester or a game to verify functionality.
* Touchscreen functionality: Test every part of the touchscreen to ensure it responds correctly to touch. Make sure you can interact with all corners and areas of the screen without any unresponsive zones.
* Verify clamshell hinge: Test the clamshell hinge that allows the screen to open and close. Ensure it operates smoothly and holds the screen securely at various angles.
* Test USB ports: Plug in common USB devices such as a mouse, keyboard, or flash drive to ensure all USB ports are functioning properly.
* Check Oculink port: If you have compatible accessories, test the Oculink port to confirm it’s working correctly.
* Verify audio output: Test the speakers and headphone jack to ensure clear audio output.

By thoroughly inspecting your GPD WIN Mini, you can ensure that all components are working as intended and address any issues promptly.  
If you have any issues, here at GPD Store we offer our full support. Just head over to our [Contact Us](https://gpdstore.net/contact-us/) page and reach out.

## Update GPD WIN Mini Windows & Drivers

### Updating Windows 11

Keeping your GPD WIN Mini up-to-date is crucial for optimal performance and security. Here’s how to check for and install Windows 11 updates

1. Open the Start menu by clicking the Windows icon or pressing the Windows Key
2. Select the Settings icon (gear symbol) or type “Settings” in the search bar and press RETURN(Enter).

1. In the Settings window, click on “Windows Update” in the left sidebar.
2. To check for available updates, click the “Check for updates” button.

1. If updates are available, Windows will begin downloading them automatically.
2. Once downloaded, you may need to restart your GPD WIN Mini to complete the installation process.

**Note:** Your GPD WIN Mini is set to automatically check for updates, but it’s a good practice to manually check periodically. You can customize update settings by clicking “Advanced options” in the Windows Update menu

### How to Update Drivers on the GPD WIN Mini

#### **Windows Update**

Windows Update is the simplest and most reliable method for updating most drivers:

1. Open the Start menu and click on Settings (gear icon).
2. Select “Windows Update” from the left sidebar.
3. Click “Check for updates.”

Windows will automatically download and install available driver updates along with other system updates.

#### **Device Manager**

For more specific driver updates:

1. Right-click the Start button and select “Device Manager.”
2. Expand the category for the device you want to update.
3. Right-click on the device and select “Update driver.”
4. Choose “Search automatically for drivers.”

**AMD Drivers Updates**

Since the GPD WIN Mini uses AMD processors, the AMD Auto-Detect driver updates will update the GPU and chipset related drivers.

1. Download and install the AMD Auto-Detect and Install Driver Updates from [here](https://www.amd.com/en/support/download/drivers.html).
2. Run the software and follow the prompts to choose what to update or install.

**Note:** Always download drivers from official sources to ensure system stability and security. While Windows Update covers most essential drivers, using the AMD tool can help keep AMD-specific components optimized for your GPD Win Mini. By regularly updating your drivers, you’ll ensure your mini laptop maintains peak performance and compatibility with the latest software and hardware
