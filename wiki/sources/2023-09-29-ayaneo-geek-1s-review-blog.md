## Summary

DROIX blog review of the [[ayaneo-geek-1s]]. Published 2023-09-29 on droix.net.

## FPS / Gaming Performance

- As always we get some great results on Forza Horizon 5. We are using the Very Low graphics settings and at 800P 28W TDP we get 115 frames per second, dropping to 107 FPS at 20W and 62 FPS at 11W.
- For Shadow of the Tomb Raider we are running at 800P on the Lowest graphics settings. At 28W TDP we get 77 FPS, and at 20W TDP we strangely get 1 frame higher at 78. We retested this a few times and got the same results. And at 11W TDP we got 55 FPS.
- For Cyberpunk we are using an older version of the game and not the latest as the benchmarks were made just before the game updated. We are on the default Low graphics settings with no FSR enabled. At 28W TDP we get 64 frames per second, at 20W we get 59, and at 11W TDP we get 33 frames per second.
- On our Street Fighter VI benchmarks we are running at 28W TDP 720P on the highest graphics settings to push the handheld. Across the three benchmarks we got 47, 49 and 42 frames per second.
