# DROIX Products Benchmark Results

A comprehensive benchmark dataset covering all products reviewed by [[droix|DROIX]], maintained as a running spreadsheet. Contains synthetic benchmarks, real-world gaming benchmarks at multiple TDP levels, and hardware specs for Android and Windows handhelds, UMPCs, mini PCs, and eGPU configurations.

## Dataset Scope

Sheets covered: 2026 Windows Gaming Handhelds, 2025 Windows Gaming Handhelds, Android Handhelds, eGPU Benchmarks, Ultrabook (MicroPC series), GPD Series Benchmarks, Mini PC benchmarks (budget, mid-range, high-end), Android TV Box, and historical sheets.

Test suites used: Cinebench 2024 / R23, Geekbench 6, Geekbench 5, 3DMark (Time Spy, Night Raid, Fire Strike), PCMark 10, AnTuTu, 3DMark Slingshot, 3DMark Wildlife Extreme.

Gaming benchmarks: Forza Horizon 5 (1080p), Shadow of the Tomb Raider (1080p), Cyberpunk 2077 (1080p), Street Fighter 6, Call of Duty series.
