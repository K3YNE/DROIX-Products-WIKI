# GPD WIN 5 — Official Product Page

GPD Store's first-party [[gpd-win-5|GPD WIN 5]] product listing. The canonical GPD marketing page — headline specs, infographics, warranty terms, shipping policy, and the pricing matrix across the two APU SKUs.

Published **2025-08-14** and maintained by the GPD Store team (multiple contributors listed in frontmatter). Current listing price starts at **$2,079.95** (USD, pre-tax).

## Key facts sourced from this page

- **SKUs listed:** [[gpd-win-5-max-395|AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395]] (16C/32T, Radeon 8060S, 40 CU @ 2900 MHz) and [[gpd-win-5-max-385|AMD Ryzen AI Max 385]] (8C/16T, Radeon 8050S, 32 CU @ 2800 MHz).
- **RAM options:** 32 GB / 64 GB / 128 GB LPDDR5X at 8000 MT/s — soldered.
- **Storage options:** up to 4 TB PCIe 4.0 NVMe M.2 2280 SSD, plus a separate Mini SSD slot (BIWIN format) up to 2 TB.
- **Display:** 7" Full HD (1920×1080), 120 Hz, 500 nits, AMD FreeSync Premium, Gorilla Glass Victus (6th gen), 10-point multi-touch with 180° rotation.
- **Connectivity:** Dual USB 3.2, USB4 (40 Gbps), 3.5 mm audio jack, MicroSD expansion, Mini SSD slot, dedicated BIOS-reset switch.
- **Controls:** Hall-effect triggers with short/long-travel toggle, capacitive analog sticks, 6-axis motion, programmable L4/R4 rear paddles, fingerprint scanner on the power button for Windows Hello.
- **Cooling:** Dual-fan + quad heat pipe.
- **Weight / dimensions:** 565 g (1.25 lbs); 26.7 × 11.1 × 2.4 cm.
- **Battery:** 80 Wh detachable external pack; swappable across multiple batteries.
- **Build:** LG-DOW 121H aviation-grade ABS (UL94 V-0 fire-retardant).
- **Warranty:** 2-year warranty via [[droix|DROIX]] Global (GPD Store) — competitors typically only offer 1 year.
