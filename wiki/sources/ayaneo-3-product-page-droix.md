## Notable Features

- **Modular controller system**: 56 possible layout combinations. Swappable modules include a touchpad and a dedicated 6-button fighting game layout. This is a unique differentiator among Windows handhelds.
- **Dual-stage Hall triggers**: 7.5mm long-travel and 3mm short-travel modes. Magnetic suspension motor developed in collaboration with GuliKit.
- **Two processor options**: The [[amd-ryzen-ai-9-hx-370|HX 370]] tier offers 12 cores/24 threads with Radeon 890M (16 CUs), while the 8840U tier provides 8 cores/16 threads with Radeon 780M (12 CUs).
- **Two display options**: OLED at 144 Hz with 800 nits and 110% DCI-P3, or LCD at 120 Hz with VRR FreeSync and 500 nits.
- **OCuLink**: Direct PCIe connection for eGPU docking alongside dual USB4.
