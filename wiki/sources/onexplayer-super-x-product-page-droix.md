# ONEXPLAYER Super X -- DROIX Product Page

Source: [DROIX product page](https://droix.net/product/onexplayer-super-x)

## Product Overview

The [[onexplayer-super-x|ONEXPLAYER Super X]] is a 14" AMOLED gaming tablet powered by [[amd-ryzen-ai-max|AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 / Max 385]] (Strix Halo). Described as merging desktop-class power with a premium tablet form factor. Out of stock at DROIX.

## Key Marketing Claims

- "Monumental shift in portable computing"
- AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395: 16C/32T, up to 5.1 GHz, 64 MB L3 cache
- cTDP adjustable 45W-120W
- Radeon 8060S: 40 CU, rivals dedicated mobile GPUs
- AMD FreeSync support
- NPU: 50 TOPS AI, total system AI: 126 TOPS
- 14" AMOLED 2880x1800, 120Hz VRR, 100% DCI-P3, 500 nits, infinite contrast
- 4096-level pressure-sensitive stylus support
- LPDDR5X 8000 MT/s quad-channel memory
- M.2 2280 PCIe 4.0 x4 + Mini SSD slot + MicroSD 4.0
- Dual USB-C 4.0, HDMI 2.1, Wi-Fi 7, BT 5.2
- 85.58 Wh removable battery
- Dual-fan + double copper vapour chamber cooling
- 170-degree kickstand
- Detachable magnetic RGB backlit keyboard
- ~1.3 kg, 12.5mm at thinnest point
- Fingerprint + AI camera (Windows Hello)
- Harman-certified audio

## Notable Details

- Up to 128GB RAM -- highest in any ONEXPLAYER device.
- Removable battery -- unusual for a tablet form factor.
- Mini SSD slot provides additional storage expansion beyond standard M.2.
- Wi-Fi 7 (upgrade from Wi-Fi 6E on X1 series).
- TDP listed as 45-75W in spec table but product copy says cTDP adjustable to 120W.
- This is a tablet form factor, not a traditional handheld -- larger and heavier than gaming handhelds.
