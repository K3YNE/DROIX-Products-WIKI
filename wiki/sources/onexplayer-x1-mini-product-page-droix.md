# ONEXPLAYER X1 Mini -- DROIX Product Page

Source: [DROIX product page](https://droix.net/product/onexplayer-x1-mini)

## Product Overview

The [[onexplayer-x1-mini|ONEXPLAYER X1 Mini]] is an 8.8" 3-in-1 handheld gaming PC with AMD Ryzen 7 8840U and 2.5K 144Hz LTPS display. Features swappable M.2 2230/2242 SSD, detachable controllers, and multiple usage modes. Out of stock at DROIX.

## Key Marketing Claims

- "Redefining Portable Gaming Power"
- 8.8" 2.5K 144Hz LTPS display (2560x1600)
- Swappable M.2 2230/2242 PCIe 4.0 SSD (easy installation)
- 5 usage modes: tablet, console, laptop, tabletop, TV
- ~710g (main body only), 2/3 size of A4 sheet
- 65.02 Wh battery, 100W GaN charger (50% in 30 min)
- Hades at 5W: 340 minutes
- 4700 RPM fan with hydraulic bearing
- Harman AudioEFX speakers
- Hall effect joysticks
- OneXConsole software: Super Game Library, Performance Control Center, Game Cloud Storage
- Game Cloud Storage compatible with AOKZOE A1, A1 Pro, OXP2, OXP2 Pro, OXP Mini

## Notable Details

- Uses M.2 2230/2242 (not 2280) -- smaller form factor but swappable without tools.
- Max 2TB SSD (limited by 2230/2242 form factor availability).
- 16GB option available (not on the larger X1 AMD).
- 144Hz refresh rate (vs 120Hz on X1 AMD).
- 500 nits (vs 540 nits on X1 AMD).
- OneXConsole game cloud compatible with AOKZOE devices (sister brand).
