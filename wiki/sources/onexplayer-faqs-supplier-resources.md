## ONEXPLAYER FAQs

Official FAQs from the [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]] drivers and FAQs page. 4 sections, 50 Q&A items total.

### General FAQs

<details>
<summary>How do I choose a charger suitable for my country when placing an order?</summary>
ONEXPLAYER will match the plug adapter based on your country. Contact support@onexplayerstore.com for custom adapter requests.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Will the OneXConsole feature be updated?</summary>
Yes, OneXConsole will continue to be updated.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What type of warranty or customer support is provided?</summary>
7-day return policy for quality issues with free refunds or exchanges. One-year warranty covering repairs and shipping costs for non-human damage.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I contact technical support?</summary>
Email support@onexplayerstore.com.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Why do the joystick/mouse move by themselves?</summary>
The device has automatic joystick calibration. Movement is usually caused by shortcut key conflicts from installed software. Contact support for the corresponding driver fix.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I restore the system?</summary>
System restoration wipes all data. Steps:
1. Press Power for 1.5s, then hold Home + click Volume+.
2. Select UEFI OS.
3. Select System Recovery.
*Requires 60%+ battery or plugged in.*
</details>

<details>
<summary>Why does the mouse cursor jump?</summary>
Ensure screen is not covered by tempered glass protector, which may impact the joystick.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I turn off joystick vibration?</summary>
Press back key + left joystick, then press right trigger to toggle vibration.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Why am I not able to charge?</summary>
Use original charger and cable. Each ONEXPLAYER model has specially debugged charging.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Why does the game quit unexpectedly?</summary>
Ensure game patch is installed, check installed files haven't changed, and update graphics drivers from Intel/AMD official website.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I reduce fan noise?</summary>
Press the mute button on the left-hand side on the back.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Why is FPS low?</summary>
Adjust screen resolution to 1280x800 for demanding games to improve FPS.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I create a partition?</summary>
Right-click **This Computer** > **Management** > **Disk Management**. Right-click disk > **Compressed Volume**. Right-click compressed space > **Allocate Volume**.
</details>

### ONEXSUGAR FAQs

<details>
<summary>Does it have a microSD card slot?</summary>
Yes, MicroSD 2.0 slot, up to 100MB/s, supports up to 1TB.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Are the screens touch-enabled?</summary>
Yes, both screens support touch input.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the battery life?</summary>
3.85V 5600mAh battery. Around 5 hours for games like Honor of Kings.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does it support OTG?</summary>
One fully-featured USB-C port supporting OTG, USB 3.1 Gen 2 (10Gbps), HDMI/DP video output. Stable DP output at 1920x1080@60Hz. Only digital audio via USB-C. Included cable does not support video output.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is it an OLED screen?</summary>
6.01" OLED main screen + 3.92" OLED secondary screen, both 60Hz, max 450 nits brightness.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does it have macro/extra buttons?</summary>
R1, R2, R3 buttons only. No M1/M2/L4/R4 macro buttons.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does it have dedicated VRAM?</summary>
No. Powered by Snapdragon G3 Gen 3 SoC with shared memory. 16GB RAM can allocate up to 5-6GB for GPU. More available VRAM leads to better emulator performance.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can it run emulators and modern Android games?</summary>
Runs on Android OS with capability for mainstream Android games. Check developer requirements for specific game compatibility.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How does the secondary screen handle DS/3DS/Wii U games?</summary>
Configure dual-screen functionality in Sugar Console app for customized mapping.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I use only the small screen for 4:3 games?</summary>
Yes, you can split display content and show different screen ratios on each screen.
</details>

### ONEXGPU 2 FAQs

<details>
<summary>Advantages of external power supply?</summary>
Prevents extra heat buildup, offers 330W power, easier maintenance compared to eGPUs with built-in PSUs.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How is 100W reverse power supply better than 65W?</summary>
More stable power allowing high-power and turbo functions.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can it charge devices?</summary>
Yes, up to 100W via PD fast-charging protocol.
</details>

<details>
<summary>SSD support and speed?</summary>
M.2 2280 NVMe, PCIe 3.0 speeds. Smaller 2242/2230 SSDs compatible but lack mounting screw holes.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Max SSD transfer speed?</summary>
8Gbps, shared with GPU through OCuLink or USB4.0.
</details>

<details>
<summary>OCuLink vs USB4.0 bandwidth?</summary>
OCuLink: 64Gbps (nearly no loss). USB4.0: 40Gbps (around 15% loss).
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does SSD usage reduce GPU bandwidth?</summary>
SSDs use max 8Gbps; both interfaces have sufficient bandwidth for no significant GPU impact.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Do I need drivers?</summary>
Yes. Download from the DRIVER DOWNLOAD section on the ONEXPLAYER website.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Compatible ONEXPLAYER models?</summary>
Models with Thunderbolt 3, Thunderbolt 4, or OCuLink ports.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Material and size?</summary>
6063-grade aluminum alloy, 229.5mm x 175mm x 43mm, 1590g.
</details>
