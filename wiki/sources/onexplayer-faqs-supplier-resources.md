## ONEXPLAYER FAQs

Official FAQs from the [[onexplayer|ONEXPLAYER]] drivers and FAQs page. 4 sections, 50 Q&A items total.

### General FAQs (13 items)

**Q: How do I choose a charger suitable for my country when placing an order?**
A: ONEXPLAYER will match the plug adapter based on your country. Contact support@onexplayerstore.com for custom adapter requests.

**Q: Will the OneXConsole feature be updated?**
A: Yes, OneXConsole will continue to be updated.

**Q: What type of warranty or customer support is provided?**
A: 7-day return policy for quality issues with free refunds or exchanges. One-year warranty covering repairs and shipping costs for non-human damage.

**Q: How do I contact technical support?**
A: Email support@onexplayerstore.com.

**Q: Why do the joystick/mouse move by themselves?**
A: The device has automatic joystick calibration. Movement is usually caused by shortcut key conflicts from installed software. Contact support for the corresponding driver fix.

**Q: How do I restore the system?**
A: System restoration wipes all data. Steps: 1) Press Power for 1.5s, then hold Home + click Volume+. 2) Select UEFI OS. 3) Select System Recovery. Requires 60%+ battery or plugged in.

**Q: Why does the mouse cursor jump?**
A: Ensure screen is not covered by tempered glass protector, which may impact the joystick.

**Q: How do I turn off joystick vibration?**
A: Press back key + left joystick, then press right trigger to toggle vibration.

**Q: Why am I not able to charge?**
A: Use original charger and cable. Each ONEXPLAYER model has specially debugged charging.

**Q: Why does the game quit unexpectedly?**
A: Ensure game patch is installed, check installed files haven't changed, and update graphics drivers from Intel/AMD official website.

**Q: How do I reduce fan noise?**
A: Press the mute button on the left-hand side on the back.

**Q: Why is FPS low?**
A: Adjust screen resolution to 1280x800 for demanding games to improve FPS.

**Q: How do I create a partition?**
A: Right-click This Computer > Management > Disk Management. Right-click disk > Compressed Volume. Right-click compressed space > Allocate Volume.

### ONEXSUGAR FAQs (10 items)

**Q: Does it have a microSD card slot?**
A: Yes, MicroSD 2.0 slot, up to 100MB/s, supports up to 1TB.

**Q: Are the screens touch-enabled?**
A: Yes, both screens support touch input.

**Q: What is the battery life?**
A: 3.85V 5600mAh battery. Around 5 hours for games like Honor of Kings.

**Q: Does it support OTG?**
A: One fully-featured USB-C port supporting OTG, USB 3.1 Gen 2 (10Gbps), HDMI/DP video output. Stable DP output at 1920x1080@60Hz. Only digital audio via USB-C. Included cable does not support video output.

**Q: Is it an OLED screen?**
A: 6.01" OLED main screen + 3.92" OLED secondary screen, both 60Hz, max 450 nits brightness.

**Q: Does it have macro/extra buttons?**
A: R1, R2, R3 buttons only. No M1/M2/L4/R4 macro buttons.

**Q: Does it have dedicated VRAM?**
A: No. Powered by Snapdragon G3 Gen 3 SoC with shared memory. 16GB RAM can allocate up to 5-6GB for GPU. More available VRAM leads to better emulator performance.

**Q: Can it run emulators and modern Android games?**
A: Runs on Android OS with capability for mainstream Android games. Check developer requirements for specific game compatibility.

**Q: How does the secondary screen handle DS/3DS/Wii U games?**
A: Configure dual-screen functionality in Sugar Console app for customized mapping.

**Q: Can I use only the small screen for 4:3 games?**
A: Yes, you can split display content and show different screen ratios on each screen.

### ONEXGPU 2 FAQs (10 items)

**Q: Advantages of external power supply?**
A: Prevents extra heat buildup, offers 330W power, easier maintenance compared to eGPUs with built-in PSUs.

**Q: How is 100W reverse power supply better than 65W?**
A: More stable power allowing high-power and turbo functions.

**Q: Can it charge devices?**
A: Yes, up to 100W via PD fast-charging protocol.

**Q: SSD support and speed?**
A: M.2 2280 NVMe, PCIe 3.0 speeds. Smaller 2242/2230 SSDs compatible but lack mounting screw holes.

**Q: Max SSD transfer speed?**
A: 8Gbps, shared with GPU through OCuLink or USB4.0.

**Q: OCuLink vs USB4.0 bandwidth?**
A: OCuLink: 64Gbps (nearly no loss). USB4.0: 40Gbps (around 15% loss).

**Q: Does SSD usage reduce GPU bandwidth?**
A: SSDs use max 8Gbps; both interfaces have sufficient bandwidth for no significant GPU impact.

**Q: Do I need drivers?**
A: Yes. Download from the DRIVER DOWNLOAD section on the ONEXPLAYER website.

**Q: Compatible ONEXPLAYER models?**
A: Models with Thunderbolt 3, Thunderbolt 4, or OCuLink ports.

**Q: Material and size?**
A: 6063-grade aluminum alloy, 229.5mm x 175mm x 43mm, 1590g.
