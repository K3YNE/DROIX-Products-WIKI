## Summary

DROIX blog review of the [[geekom-a6]]. Published 2025-01-29 on droix.net.

## FPS / Gaming Performance

- The GEEKOM A6 scores very well with an impressive 127 frames per second at 720P on the very low graphics settings. While the GEEKOM A8 does score around 30 frames per second higher due to the higher performing 780M GPU, we see the GT13 Pro with Intel graphics have around half the performance with only 67 frames per second.
- The A6 scores a very respectable 61 frames per second at 720P on what is a graphically demanding game. Interestingly the GT13 Pro has a similar score despite the lower graphics performance.
- On Dead Cells, we achieved a steady 60 frames per second at 4K. With Hades II, the game ran smoothly at 4K on Medium graphics settings, or 1080P High if you prefer a higher resolution. TMNT Shredder’s Revenge delivered an effortless 60+ FPS at 4K. For the recently released Heroes of Hammerwatch II, we reached 4K Max settings with a solid 120+ FPS, provided your monitor supports it.
- AMD FSR proves invaluable for handling more demanding games, as demonstrated with Cyberpunk. While not every graphically intensive game will hit 60+ FPS, many can manage around 30 FPS—though it’s worth noting that performance miracles should not be expected.
