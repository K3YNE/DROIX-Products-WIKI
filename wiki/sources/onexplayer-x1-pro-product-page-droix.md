# ONEXPLAYER X1 Pro -- DROIX Product Page

Source: [DROIX product page](https://droix.net/product/onexplayer-x1-pro)

## Product Overview

The [[onexplayer-x1-pro|ONEXPLAYER X1 Pro]] is a 10.95" 3-in-1 handheld gaming PC powered by [[amd-ryzen-ai-9-hx-370|AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370]] with Radeon 890M. An upgraded version of the [[onexplayer-x1-amd]] with the Strix Point processor. Out of stock at DROIX.

## Key Marketing Claims

- "Revolutionary leap in handheld gaming PCs"
- AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370: 12C/24T, up to 5.1 GHz boost, Radeon 890M at 2.9 GHz
- "Unmatched speed and efficiency" with "enhanced AI capabilities"
- Same body/display as X1 AMD: 10.95" 2.5K LTPS 120Hz
- Same I/O: 2x USB 4.0, OCuLink, USB-A 3.2, MicroSD, Harman speakers
- 65.02 Wh battery (~8 hours), 100W GaN charger
- Hall effect joysticks, fingerprint + face recognition

## Notable Details

- Essentially the same chassis as [[onexplayer-x1-amd]] but with HX 370 instead of 8840U.
- HX 370 has 12C/24T (vs 8C/16T) and 15-54W TDP (vs fixed 28W).
- Radeon 890M with 16 CU (vs 780M with 12 CU) -- significant GPU uplift.
- DROIX product page text references the "X1 AMD review" (same chassis review).
