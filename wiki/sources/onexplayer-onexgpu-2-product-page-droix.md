# ONEXPLAYER ONEXGPU 2 -- DROIX Product Page

Source: [DROIX product page](https://droix.net/product/onexplayer-onexgpu-2)

## Product Overview

The [[onexplayer-onexgpu-2|ONEXPLAYER ONEXGPU 2]] eGPU docking station features the AMD Radeon RX 7800M (RDNA 3). Significant upgrade over the [[onexplayer-onexgpu|ONEXGPU]] with better GPU, HDMI 2.1, DP 2.0, Ethernet, and aerospace-grade aluminium. In stock at DROIX.

## Key Marketing Claims

- AMD Radeon RX 7800M: Time Spy 15,916, Fire Strike 42,949
- "Outpaces RTX 4070 Mobile and RX 7600M XT"
- Up to 245% higher FPS vs standard setups when paired with AMD 8840U CPU
- 330W GaN fast-charging power supply with 100W reverse charging
- Aerospace-grade aluminium alloy (CNC cut, die-cast, polished, sandblasted, anodised)
- Advanced cooling: 3600 RPM fan, 20.75 CFM airflow, 46.6-53.2 dB noise
- Magnetic cover for easy M.2 SSD access
- Supports up to 3 screens simultaneously

- Storage expansion still PCIe 3.0.
- Compatible brands: Crucial, DROIX, Kingston, ONEXPLAYER.
