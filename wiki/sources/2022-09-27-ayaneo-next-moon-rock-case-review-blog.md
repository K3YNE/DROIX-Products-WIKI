## Summary

Brief DROIX blog look at the official Moon Rock carry case for the [[ayaneo-next]] and AYANEO NEXT PRO handheld gaming PCs. Accessory review with no benchmarks.

## Product Details

- **Product:** AYANEO NEXT & NEXT PRO Moon Rock Case
- **Compatibility:** [[ayaneo-next]], AYANEO NEXT PRO only
- **Design:** Moon-style landscape embossed on front with AYANEO NEXT text; raised areas for controls protection
- **Features:** Non-removable hand strap on top, two zippers with small removable straps, soft interior lining, small accessory pouch
- **Protection:** Hard shell protects against knocks and scratches for carrying or bag storage

## Key Takeaways

- Case fits snugly and does the job of protecting the handheld
