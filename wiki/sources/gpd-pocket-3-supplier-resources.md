## Driver Update Notes

Intel drivers (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, chipset, integrated graphics) may become outdated over time. Use the [Intel Driver & Support Assistant (DSA)](https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/support/detect.html) to identify and upgrade outdated Intel hardware drivers automatically.

For connecting the GPD G1 eGPU via Thunderbolt, download [AMD Radeon RX 7600M XT Drivers](https://www.amd.com/en/support/downloads/drivers.html/graphics/radeon-rx/radeon-rx-7000-series/amd-radeon-rx-7600m-xt.html). Use [Furmark](https://www.techpowerup.com/download/furmark/) to test the G1 dock.
