# GPD DUO (AI 9 HX 370) — Windows 11 24H2 Home Firmware

Full flash Windows 11 24H2 Home firmware for the [[gpd-duo]] with AI 9 HX 370 APU only.

**Important:** You must upgrade BIOS to V2.12 or later before upgrading to this firmware on the 370 version. See [[gpd-duo-bios-v2-17-370]].

**Warning:** Reinstallation removes all data on drive C. Back up before proceeding.
