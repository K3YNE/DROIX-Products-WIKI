## Summary

DROIX blog review of the [[gpd-win-max-2]] AMD Ryzen 7 6800U model. Three SKUs tested (16 GB/1 TB, 32 GB/1 TB, 32 GB/2 TB). Extensive benchmarks at multiple TDP levels, gaming tests, and emulator performance. Compared to Intel 1260P model (discontinued).

## Key Specs

| Spec | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **CPU** | AMD Ryzen 7 6800U, 8 cores / 16 threads, up to 4.7 GHz, default 28 W TDP |
| **GPU** | AMD Radeon 680M, 12 cores, up to 2200 MHz |
| **RAM** | 16 GB or 32 GB LPDDR5-6400 |
| **Storage** | 1 TB or 2 TB M.2 PCIe 4.0 SSD |
| **Display** | 10.1" touchscreen, 1920x1200 (supports up to 2560x1600), 2 MP camera |
| **Connectivity** | WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, optional 4G LTE |
| **Battery** | 67 Wh; idle: 10 hr 50 min; Cinebench full load @ 28 W: 2 hr 5 min |
| **Dimensions** | 8.9 x 6.2 x 0.9 in (22.7 x 16.0 x 2.3 cm), 1005 g |
| **Fan noise** | 63 dB (Cinebench 10 min @ 28 W) |
| **Temperature** | 63 C (Cinebench 10 min @ 28 W) |
| **Ports** | USB 4, 2x USB 3.2 Gen 2, HDMI, 3.5 mm, Micro SD, SD card |
| **Controls** | Detachable covers, clickable hall sensor sticks, D-Pad, QWERTY keyboard (backlit) |

## System Benchmarks (30 W TDP)

| Benchmark | 16 GB/1 TB | 32 GB/1 TB | 32 GB/2 TB |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **PassMark** | 6002 | 5964 | 6179 |
| **PCMark** | 6425 | 6427 | 6421 |
| **3DMark Time Spy** | 2840 | 2819 | 2771 |
| **3DMark Fire Strike** | 6823 | 6770 | 6681 |
| **3DMark Night Raid** | 24124 | 23936 | 23624 |
| **Cinebench** | 11501 | 11507 | 10991 |
| **CrystalDiskMark** | 2091 / 1955 MB/s | 2091 / 1961 MB/s | 5225 / 4891 MB/s |

2 TB model slightly slower on compute benchmarks (possibly due to SSD drawing PCIe lanes/power).

## Reviewer Verdict

"The AMD model is to put it quite simply a beast!" Far higher performance than any previous handheld. Highest FPS ever recorded on a handheld for Forza Horizon 5 at time of review.
