# AYANEO Pocket VERT Review

Review published 2026-03-31 covering the [[AYANEO]] Pocket VERT, a vertical format Android gaming handheld described as the "flashiest vertical handheld" available. Powered by the [[Snapdragon 8+ Gen 1]] with [[Adreno 730]] GPU.

## Key Specifications

- **SoC**: [[Snapdragon 8+ Gen 1]] + [[Adreno 730]]
- **Display**: 3.5" LTPS LCD, 1440x1600, 60Hz (10x Game Boy resolution for pixel-perfect upscaling)
- **Weight**: 318g
- **Body**: Full metal CNC construction
- **Input**: Touchpad (replaces physical analog stick), Magic switch scroll wheel (from [[AYANEO Pocket DMG]])
- **Gaming Buttons**: Smaller than usual at 6.6mm

## Design and Build

The Pocket VERT features a premium full metal CNC machined body with a standout look and feel. The 3.5" display at 1440x1600 resolution is specifically chosen as 10x the original Game Boy resolution, enabling pixel-perfect upscaling for retro titles. The Magic switch scroll wheel carries over from the [[AYANEO Pocket DMG]].

## Concerns

The touchpad replaces a physical analog stick and is not considered a great substitute. Gaming buttons are smaller than usual at 6.6mm which may affect comfort. Performance from the [[Snapdragon 8+ Gen 1]] is lower than current [[Snapdragon G3X Gen 2]] and Gen 3 devices. A "style over substance" concern was noted in the review.

## Comparisons

Compared with the [[AYANEO Pocket DMG]] which has the advantage of a physical analog stick. The Pocket VERT prioritizes premium aesthetics and the vertical form factor over raw performance and input flexibility.

## Availability

Available at [[DroiX]] at the time of review.
