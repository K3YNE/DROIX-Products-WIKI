# GPD WIN 5 Smart Docking Station Review

The [[gpd-win-5-smart-dock|GPD WIN 5 Smart Docking Station]] is an accessory dock for the [[gpd-win-5|GPD WIN 5]]. Reviewed on 2025-11-20, the verdict was firmly negative — "anything but smart." The reviewer found it hard to recommend over any generic USB hub.


## Key Specs

- **Weight:** 317g
- **Construction:** All plastic
- **Video output:** HDMI 2.1 4K 144Hz
- **Ports:** 3x USB-A, Gigabit Ethernet
- **Battery:** Charging slot for WIN 5 external battery
- **USB-C:** Present, but only charges the battery in the slot

## Key Findings

- The critical flaw: the USB-C port only charges the battery sitting in the dock's slot — it does **not** charge the WIN 5 console itself
- You cannot charge the handheld through the dock; a separate charger is required
- This defeats a primary purpose of a docking station
- All-plastic build feels cheap at 317g
- The feature set (HDMI + USB-A + Ethernet) is standard for any generic USB-C hub
- Any generic hub does the same job better for less money

## Verdict

A brief, negative review. The inability to charge the console through the dock is a dealbreaker. The "Smart" branding is misleading — the dock adds no meaningful value over commodity alternatives.
