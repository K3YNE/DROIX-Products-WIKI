## Summary

DROIX blog review of the Minisforum NUCXi5 (i5-11400H + RTX 3060) and NUCXi7 (i7-11800H + RTX 3070) gaming mini PCs. Extensive benchmarks at 800p and 4K, gameplay tests, and emulation.

## Key Specs

| Spec | NUCXi5 | NUCXi7 |
|------|--------|--------|
| **CPU** | Intel i5-11400H, 6C/12T, up to 4.5 GHz | Intel i7-11800H, 8C/16T, up to 4.6 GHz |
| **GPU** | Nvidia GeForce RTX 3060 6 GB | Nvidia GeForce RTX 3070 8 GB |
| **RAM** | Up to 64 GB DDR4 | Up to 64 GB DDR4 |
| **Storage** | Up to 2 TB M.2 2280 NVMe | Same |
| **Connectivity** | WiFi 6, Bluetooth, 2.5 Gb Ethernet | Same |
| **Dimensions** | 10.2 x 15.4 x 3.1 in (on stand), 2.4 kg | Same |
| **Peak power** | 163.5 W | 181.2 W |
| **Temperature** | 64 C | 64 C |
| **Fan noise** | 51 dB | 51 dB |

## System Benchmarks

| Benchmark | NUCXi5 | NUCXi7 |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **PassMark** | 6,889 | 7,551 |
| **PCMark** | 6,420 | 6,660 |
| **Cinebench** | 9,417 | 11,022 |
| **3DMark Time Spy** | 8,394 | 10,218 |
| **3DMark Fire Strike** | 18,339 | 21,298 |
| **3DMark Night Raid** | 46,453 | 55,105 |

## Gaming Benchmarks

| Game | NUCXi5 (800p / 4K) | NUCXi7 (800p / 4K) |
|------|---------------------|---------------------|
| **Forza Horizon 5** | 150 / 24 FPS | 165 / 46 FPS |
| **Tomb Raider** | 142 / 39 FPS | 156 / 87 FPS |
| **Cyberpunk 2077** | 71 / 16 FPS | 123 / 29 FPS |
| **CoD: MW2** | 167 / 33 FPS | 188 / 48 FPS |

At 4K max settings, NUCXi7 averages 50% higher performance than NUCXi5.

## Gameplay Tests (NUCXi7)

| Game | Resolution | Settings | Performance |
|------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Doom Eternal | 4K | Ultra Nightmare (no RT) | Solid 60+ |
| Cyberpunk 2077 | 4K | Medium | 60+ FPS |
| Overwatch 2 | 4K | Epic | Mostly 60+ |
| CoD: MW2 | 1440p | Ultra | 60-70 FPS |
| Forza Horizon 5 | 4K | High | Solid 60 |

## Emulation Results

| System | Performance |
|--------|-------------|
| Xbox 360 (Xenia) | Sonic Transformed, PGR2 @ 30 FPS; Halo 3 mostly 30 with dips |
| PS3 (RPCS3) | Wipeout HD solid 60, Skate 3 solid 60 |
| Switch (Yuzu) | Cruis'n Blast solid 60; first party games mixed but generally high FPS |

## Reviewer Verdict

NUCXi7 with RTX 3070 is the clear winner with average 50% performance increase over NUCXi5 at 4K. Great space-saving gaming solution with peak power of only 181 W. Far smaller than a desktop gaming PC while remaining competitive.
